# "Subject to Underwriting" Meaning

Based on the webpage content, here's the explanation:

## Core Definition

When an insurance provider includes "subject to underwriting" on a quotation, it indicates the quote is **provisional and dependent on successful completion of the underwriting process**.

## Two-Step Process

**Quoting (Initial Step):**
The provider offers a preliminary quote based on information supplied, typically using "worst case scenario" assumptions to ensure the estimate covers potential risks.

**Underwriting (Final Step):**
This involves "a careful review of any medical reports" and detailed examination of your medical history to reach a final determination.

## Key Outcome

After underwriting completion, you may receive a **revised quote** that's usually more favorable than the initial projection. As stated: *"the process delivers a fully underwritten policy that provides peace of mind that all assessments decisions have been completed."*
