# Progressive Renters Insurance Coverage Summary

## Coverage Types

Progressive offers three standard coverage types:

**Personal Property Coverage**: Protects belongings from "fire, smoke, falling objects, theft, vandalism, or other peril specified by your insurance carrier," including bicycles stored on or off-campus.

**Liability Insurance**: Covers accidental damage to others' property or injuries, plus legal fees if sued.

**Additional Living Expenses**: Covers hotel, meals, and laundry if you cannot occupy your residence due to damage.

## Optional Add-Ons

- **Home Computer Coverage**: Specialized protection for computers and related equipment, extending to business use
- **Personal Injury**: Expands liability to cover libel, slander, defamation, privacy invasion, and wrongful eviction/arrest
- **Water Backup Coverage**: Protects against sewer/drain backups and sump pump overflow
- **Homeshield R Package**: Increases limits on jewelry, artwork, and collectibles; includes personal injury and water backup coverage

## Coverage Limits

- Personal property: $20,000–$100,000 (in $500 increments)
- Computer coverage: $3,000–$10,000
- Liability: $100,000–$500,000
- Medical payments: $1,000–$3,000

## Deductibles

Standard options include $250, $500, or $1,000 per claim.

## Pricing Examples

Annual quotes vary by location:
- Virginia: $110
- Texas: $140–$150
- Chicago: $125
- NYC: $237

## Discounts

Savings available for yearly prepayment, early quotes, gated community living, paperless billing, and policy bundling.
