## State Farm vs. American Family
Both State Farm and American Family offer a wide variety of coverage options and discounts, including bundling discounts. However, State Farm is available in 47 U.S. states, while American Family is only available in 19 states.
American Family offers home insurance coverage in Arizona, Colorado, Georgia, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, Nevada, North Dakota, Ohio, Oregon, South Dakota, Utah, Washington, and Wisconsin.
American Family is the more expensive of the two, but it offers discounts that can bring the cost of your premium down, and Costco members may be eligible for discounted coverage or added benefits at no additional cost.
State Farm ranks as one of the most affordable home insurance companies on our list and offers a variety of discounts, making it a solid option for people who need affordable coverage.
In terms of coverage offerings, State Farm offers more comprehensive coverage for additional living expenses and loss of use, while American Family specializes in custom home insurance plans that allow flexibility based on location and needs.
Below are the average annual rates for State Farm and American Family for several different levels of coverage:  

### Do State Farm and American Family cover mold damage or removal?
Both State Farm and American Family provide limited coverage for [mold damage](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/homeowners-insurance/when-does-homeowners-insurance-cover-mold) that is caused by a covered peril, such as water damage from a burst pipe. Mold removal coverage is often restricted and may require additional endorsements; check with your agent if you have questions about how mold may or may not be covered under your policy.
### Do State Farm and American Family cover roof leaks?
Both State Farm and American Family will cover [roof leaks](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/homeowners-insurance/does-homeowners-insurance-cover-roof-damage) that are caused by a covered peril. However, if the leak is due to wear and tear or lack of maintenance, it is typically not covered. Review your policy details to find out exactly what is and is not covered by your insurance.
### Do State Farm and American Family cover termite damage?
Termite damage is not covered by a standard homeowners insurance policy with any insurer, including State Farm and American Family. Homeowners are responsible for termite prevention and repair unless otherwise specified. Since [termite damage](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/homeowners-insurance/does-homeowners-insurance-cover-termite-damage) is excluded, it is vital to monitor your property and hire an exterminator if necessary.
### Do State Farm and American Family cover water damage?
Both State Farm and American Family cover water damage caused by sudden and accidental events, such as burst pipes or an appliance malfunction; however, other types of water damage, like flood damage or slow water damage caused by wear and tear, are not covered. The option of writing a flood insurance policy through the National Flood Insurance Program ([NFIP)](https://www.floodsmart.gov/) is available with both State Farm and American Family.
### Do State Farm and American Family offer homeowners insurance bundling?
Yes, both State Farm and American Family offer [bundling discounts](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/homeowners-insurance/best-home-and-auto-insurance-bundles). Auto and home insurance bundles are the most popular options, but each company offers a variety of bundling options, including condo, renters, and other types of coverage.

## FAQ
How can I reach State Farm customer service?
Customer service is available for State Farm customers 24/7 by phone at 800-782-8332 or by using the company’s online contact page. People who purchased coverage through an agent can reach out to them for assistance as well.
How can I reach American Family customer service?
Customers can call American Family for assistance at 800-692-6326, as well as use the company website or the MyAmFam app to reach out to file claims or maintain their policy. You can use the website to start the quote process, but all quotes must be finalized through an American Family agent.
