How to Get a Home and Car Insurance Bundling Discount

In order to get a home and auto discount, you will need to choose a company that offers a bundling discount. To qualify, you will need to purchase both home and car insurance from that company. The discount will be automatically applied.

Some companies will also include renters insurance as a qualifying coverage for a bundling discount, meaning you can buy both renters and auto insurance for bundling savings (Read More: Best and Cheapest Renters Insurance).

What coverages will qualify for a bundling discount will depend on the provider, but the majority of companies will always offer at least home and auto insurance bundling savings.

Other Ways to Save on Car Insurance

Discounts are a great way to save on your car insurance policy, and a bundling discount is usually one of the largest discounts you can secure on car and home insurance.

However, discounts aren't the only way to reduce your rates and get affordable auto insurance. Some other great hacks to save money on your car insurance include:

  • Dropping Unnecessary Coverages: Review your add-on coverages carefully. You may no longer need some coverage on older vehicles.
  • Raising Deductibles: If you feel comfortable raising your insurance deductibles, your insurance company will lower rates as its financial responsibility is reduced.
  • Comparison Shopping: The company you are with may no longer offer the best deal. Every few years, make sure to get quotes and shop around.

It is also easier to score cheap auto insurance coverage if you are a low-risk driver. Generally, this means that you have a clean driving record, drive a safe car, and live in a low-risk area.

Some companies also offer telematics driving programs that safe drivers can participate in to earn a lower rate on car insurance. Taking advantage of programs like these can help you maximize savings on your car insurance, leaving more money to spend on your home insurance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get one of the best home and car insurance bundle discounts?

Whether you are shopping at Allstate or Erie Insurance, you can get one of the best home and auto insurance bundling discounts by purchasing both coverages from the provider.

Which insurance company has the best bundle deals?

Allstate, Geico, Liberty Mutual, and American Family have the best bundle discount of 25%.

Who has the most affordable car and home insurance?

USAA has some of the cheapest home and car insurance, with auto insurance rates starting at $84 per month for drivers with a clean record. Compare more quotes in our USAA review.

Is it really cheaper to bundle home and auto insurance?

Yes, in most cases you can get the cheapest home and auto insurance by bundling. However, make sure to check the cost of home and auto insurance separately at different companies to see if bundling at the same company is actually cheaper.

What is the most reputable auto and home insurance company?

One of the most reputable auto and home insurance companies is USAA, with an A++ rating from A.M. Best and an A+ rating from the BBB. However, USAA is only for military and veterans. To find other reputable companies, enter your ZIP in our free quote finder.

How often should I change home and auto insurance?

If you are happy with your current provider, there is no need to change. However, it is recommended to shop around for free quotes periodically, just to make sure you are still getting a good deal.

What is a good discount for bundles?

The best discounts are at least 10%, but make sure to also consider the overall rate before the discount is applied. More expensive companies may still be expensive even after a large discount is applied.

What are the disadvantages of bundled insurance?

Having bundled insurance may have a few disadvantages, one being that it may be harder to switch providers if you have multiple policies with one company. If you have unique car or home insurance needs, you may also be unable to find the coverage you need all at one company.

What is the 80% rule in homeowners insurance?

The 80% rule in home insurance is that you should purchase a home insurance policy that will cover 80% of the replacement cost of your home.

Why are my home and auto insurance so high?

There are several reasons your home and auto insurance may be expensive, from having multiple accidents on your record to living in a high-risk wildfire area. Shopping around for different quotes may help you find cheaper home and auto insurance rates.

How many quotes should you get for homeowners insurance?

We recommend getting at least three quotes when shopping for homeowners insurance.
