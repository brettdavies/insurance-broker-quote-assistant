#  Teen Driver Insurance Discounts
### Take Our Online Drivers Ed Course And Qualify For Teen Driver’s Insurance Discounts 
Teen drivers typically have higher insurance rates due to their limited driving experience, but many insurance companies offer discounts to help make coverage more affordable. Completing a driver’s education course and practicing safe driving can qualify you for significant savings.
![](https://www.yourdriversedonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Teen-Driver-Mistakes.png)
### Available Discounts for Teen Drivers 
**Driver’s Education Discount** – Completing a California DMV-approved driver’s ed course can qualify you for lower rates. 
**Good Student Discount** – Many insurers offer savings for students who maintain a 3.0 GPA (B average) or higher. 
**Safe Driver Discount** – If you have a clean driving record with no accidents or violations, you may receive additional discounts. 
**Low Mileage Discount** – If you drive fewer miles per year, some providers offer reduced rates. 
**Defensive Driving Course Discount** – Completing an approved defensive driving course can also help lower premiums. 
**Student Away Discount** – If you attend school more than 100 miles from home and don’t have regular access to a car, you may qualify for savings. 
###  How to Qualify for a Teen Driver Discount 
**Complete a DMV-Approved Driver’s Ed Course** – Many insurers require proof of completion to apply the discount. 
**Maintain Good Grades** –Submit a copy of your most recent report card showing a 3.0 GPA or higher. 
**Drive Safely** – Avoiding accidents and traffic violations helps maintain lower insurance rates over time. 
**Ask About Policy Options** –Some insurers offer family policy discounts or multi-car discounts when teen drivers are added to a parent’s plan.
