Most companies provide at least a few, and Geico is one of them. However, Geico offers fewer options than many of its similarly-sized competitors. One crucial difference between Geico and many of its competitors is that Geico doesn’t offer gap insurance (Learn More: Does Geico offer gap insurance?). Gap insurance pays the difference between what you owe on a loan and what your car is worth.

If you’re unsure of how much insurance you need, a Geico representative can help. Some of the available coverages are dependent on what state you live in. For example, whether you can buy PIP or MedPay coverage depends on your state (Read More: Personal Injury Protection (PIP) and Medical Coverage that Your Car Insurance Policy will Pay For).

And while it doesn’t offer the most add-on options, Geico offers a few valuable ways to add extra protection to your policy, such as Geico’s roadside assistance comes to the rescue for situations like a flat tire, dead battery, empty gas tank, or when you need a tow (Learn More: Best Auto Insurance for Towing and Roadside Assistance).

Geico also offers mechanical breakdown insurance, which pays for mechanical problems with your car that result in a trip to the repair shop. Mechanical breakdown insurance can save you thousands in repairs but does not cover maintenance issues like worn brakes.

Adding more coverage is never a bad idea, but you should only purchase what you need. Every add-on increases your monthly rates, quickly making your policy expensive.

If you’re a cautious driver, you might be excited about Geico’s usage-based car insurance option. Geico’s DriveEasy program monitors your driving habits and produces a score that determines your car insurance rate. Along with tracking your driving patterns, the Geico DriveEasy mobile app allows you to update your policy, pay your bills, track claims, and more.

As you drive, the DriveEasy mobile app tracks things like your stop and start patterns, braking patterns, the time of day you drive, and how quickly you accelerate.

All of this information allows Geico to curate a car insurance rate appropriate for your driving style. So, if you’re looking for a car insurance provider that rewards careful driving, Geico might be the one for you. To participate in the DriveEasy telematics insurance program, download the app.

There is also a good student discount for full-time students who maintain a GPA of 3.0 or better. Geico also offers a discount on auto insurance for active duty military and veterans, as well as members of the National Guard and Reserves.

Many of these discounts are automatically applied to your policy when you sign up. Others require documentation, such as proof of your grades for the good student discount. A Geico auto insurance customer service representative can double-check your account to ensure you get every savings possible.

Geico Auto Insurance Pros and Cons

What sets Geico apart is its direct-buy approach to car insurance, which eliminates the need for agents and their commissions. This results in lower rates for customers and a straightforward process for policy purchasing.

Geico is a strong choice for auto insurance, as it has affordable rates, strong ratings, and other perks. We found the best pros of Geico include:

  • Discount Opportunities: Geico has numerous discounts, such as multi-vehicle car insurance discounts, with savings up to 30%.
  • Customer Ratings: Geico has higher-than-average customer satisfaction ratings from J.D. Power, with a score of 692 / 1,000.
  • Financial Strength: Geico has the highest A++ rating from A.M. Best.

Geico also offers perks like the Geico Skill for Alexa, which allows you to ask questions about your policy and get instant answers.

What is Geico’s weakness? While Geico is an excellent choice for most customers, the company has some drawbacks. See the main cons we found below.

  • No Gap Coverage: New car owners may want to avoid Geico if they are looking for new car coverages to protect their assets.
  • Some Drivers Might be Rejected: Geico may not insure high-risk drivers, such as those with multiple DUIs.

For the most part, Geico is an excellent choice of provider for the majority of drivers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Geico have a mobile app?

Geico has a mobile app in both the Apple and Google playstores. The Geico auto insurance mobile app can start and manage claims, download digital IDs, update payments, and view policy information.

What is DriveEasy by Geico?

DriveEasy is Geico’s usage-based insurance program that tracks driving behaviors to offer safe drivers a discount. DriveEasy tracks behaviors like hard braking, rapid acceleration, and what time you drive through an app. Safe drivers can save up to 10% on their insurance with DriveEasy.

Can Geico refuse to pay a claim?

Yes, Geico can refuse to pay a claim if your policy doesn’t cover the damages (Read More: What to Do When Your Car Insurance Claim is Denied).

Could switching to Geico really save you?

Starting at $40 per month, Geico auto insurance rates are some of the cheapest in the country for the average driver. However, you’ll likely see much higher rates with Geico than at another company if you have speeding tickets, DUIs, or at-fault accidents.

How do you get a Geico auto insurance quote?

You have several options to get a Geico quote. The easiest is to complete the request form on the Geico site, which takes only a few minutes. Alternatively, you can call the Geico auto insurance phone number or visit a local office to speak with a representative.

Can you switch car insurance at any time?

Yes, you can switch auto insurance at any time, though you may have to pay a cancellation fee if you cancel before the end of your policy (Read More: Is it bad to switch insurance companies?). Shop for affordable auto insurance today with our free tool.

Is Geico good at paying out claims?

By any measurable standard, the Geico auto insurance claims process has slightly more satisfied customers than the average company.

What is Auto Repair Xpress?

Auto Repair Xpress is Geico’s network of vetted repair shops. While you can choose any repair shop you like, the process may be easier when choosing a qualified Auto Repair Xpress shop.

Which is better, Geico or Progressive?

Geico is better for affordability, as its rates start at $40 per month compared to Progressive’s $56 per month. Learn more about the company in our Progressive auto insurance review.

Is Geico owned by State Farm?

No, Geico is not owned by State Farm.

Is Geico insurance hard to deal with?

Some customers have complaints about claims with Geico, but J.D. Power rates Geico above average for customer satisfaction.

Is Allstate cheaper than Geico?

No, Allstate’s rates start at $87 per month, compared to Geico’s starting rate of $40 per month. Read our Allstate auto insurance review to learn more about the company.

Does Geico forgive the first accident?

Geico forgives the first accident of eligible drivers. You must have been insured with Geico for five or more years, and had no claims within that time period.

What is the Geico phone number?

The general customer service number for Geico is 1 (800) 207-7847.
