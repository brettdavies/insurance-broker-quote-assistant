Snapshot® by Progressive in Baldwin County, Alabama

At Heritage Insurance in Silverhill, we’re proud to represent Progressive Insurance, one of the most innovative carriers in the industry. One of Progressive’s most popular features is Snapshot®, a usage-based insurance program that rewards safe driving with real discounts.

 

What Is Snapshot®?

Snapshot® is Progressive’s telematics program, designed to personalize your insurance rates based on how you actually drive—not just general statistics like age or ZIP code. By tracking driving behavior through either the mobile app or a plug-in device, Snapshot can help lower your auto insurance premiums if you practice safe driving habits.

Drivers typically receive:

  • An initial participation discount just for enrolling.
  • The opportunity to earn larger savings at renewal based on performance.

 

How It Works

When you enroll through Heritage Insurance, you’ll choose either the Snapshot mobile app or a small plug-in device mailed to you. Snapshot monitors:

  • Time of day you drive (late-night driving may increase risk)
  • Hard braking and rapid acceleration
  • Speed and mileage
  • Phone usage while driving (mobile app only)

You’ll be able to see a driving score and trip details inside the app, helping you improve your driving habits while working toward discounts.

 

Do Other Carriers Offer Similar Programs?

Yes—many insurers now offer usage-based insurance programs like this, but Progressive’s Snapshot® is among the most established and widely used. Other companies may use similar systems (like National General’s DynamicDrive), but Progressive’s program is unique in offering both a plug-in device and an app option.

 

Things to Keep in Mind

While many drivers save with Snapshot®, not everyone will. If the program shows risky driving habits—like frequent hard braking, speeding, or late-night trips—it may impact your rate at renewal. Still, most safe drivers find that Snapshot® offers meaningful discounts and valuable feedback.

 

Is Snapshot® Right for You?

If you’re a safe, consistent driver, Snapshot® is a great way to turn your good habits into savings. At Heritage Insurance, we’ll explain how it works, help you get set up, and make sure your policy is customized for the best value.
