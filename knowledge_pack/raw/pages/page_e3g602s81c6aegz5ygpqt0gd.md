When it comes to saving on car insurance, knowing about available discounts can make a significant difference. GEICO, a popular choice for many drivers, offers a range of discounts designed to lower your premium. Let’s dive into the various discounts GEICO provides and how you can take advantage of them to maximize your savings.
## Multi-Policy Discount
**Details:** Bundling your car insurance with other types of insurance, such as homeowners or renters insurance, can save you up to 25%.
## Multi-Vehicle Discount
**Details:** If you insure more than one vehicle with GEICO, you could receive a discount of up to 25%.
## Good Driver Discount
**Details:** Maintaining a clean driving record for five years can [earn you a discount](https://ratefrog.com/how-do-i-get-the-geico-safe-driver-discount/) of up to 26% on most coverages.
## Seat Belt Use Discount
**Details:** Regular use of your seat belt can save you up to 15% on your medical payments or personal injury protection coverage.
## Good Student Discount
**Details:** Students who maintain a B average or better can save up to 15% on certain coverages.
## Driver’s Education Discount
**Details:** Completing a [defensive driving course](https://ratefrog.com/does-geico-offer-a-defensive-driving-course-discount/) or driver’s education can get you a discount, typically around 10%.
## Military Discount
**Details:** Active or retired military members can save up to 15% on their total insurance premium.
## Federal Employee Discount
**Details:** Federal employees are eligible for up to 8% off their auto insurance.
## Emergency Deployment Discount
**Details:** Military personnel deployed to imminent danger zones are eligible for significant savings.
## Membership and Employee Discounts
**Details:** Being a member of certain organizations or having specific occupations can qualify you for [additional discounts](https://ratefrog.com/how-can-i-get-a-geico-affiliation-discount/).
## Anti-Theft System Discount
**Details:** Having an anti-theft system installed in your vehicle can earn you a discount of up to 25%.
## New Vehicle Discount
**Details:** [Insuring a new car](https://ratefrog.com/does-geico-offer-a-new-car-discount/) can save you up to 15%.
## Daytime Running Lights Discount
**Details:** Vehicles equipped with daytime running lights can get a small discount, typically around 3%.
## How to Maximize Your Savings
  * **Combine Discounts:** Stacking multiple discounts can lead to substantial savings.
  * **Regularly Review Your Policy:** Annually review your policy to ensure you’re taking advantage of all the discounts you qualify for.
  * **Maintain a Clean Driving Record:** A clean driving record not only ensures safety but also keeps your good driver discount intact.
