## What Is the Progressive Mature Driver Discount?
The Progressive Mature Driver Discount is a rate reduction offered by Progressive Insurance to reward mature or older drivers who demonstrate safe driving behaviors. This discount acknowledges the experience, stability, and often safer driving habits of mature drivers.
Eligible drivers can enjoy a reduction in their auto insurance premiums. This discount serves as an incentive for older drivers to maintain their safe driving habits.
Ready to learn about the Progressive mature driver discount, including who qualifies, how to obtain the discount, how long it lasts, and more? Read on for all the details.

## What Are the Requirements for the Progressive Mature Driver Discount?
Here’s a detailed breakdown of the eligibility requirements for the Progressive mature driver discount:
  * **Age:** Typically, drivers who are 50 years or older might be eligible for this discount, although the exact age may vary by state.
  * **Driving record:** A clean driving record, with no recent at-fault accidents or major traffic violations, is required for eligibility.
  * **Mature driver course:** In some states, drivers may also be required to complete a mature driver improvement course to qualify for the discount (more on that below).


You’ll also need to have an active Progressive insurance policy on at least one vehicle.
### What Are the Requirements for the Progressive Mature Driver Discount in California?
![What Are the Requirements for the Progressive Mature Driver Discount in California?](https://www.trafficsafetyinstitute.com/progressive-mature-driver-discount/)
In California, the requirements to qualify for the Progressive mature driver discount are:
  * To be 55 years of age or older.
  * To complete a California Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) approved mature driver improvement course. These courses are designed to refresh driving knowledge, cover the effects of aging on driving, and provide tips for older drivers.
  * To maintain a clean driving record. A history of recent at-fault accidents or significant traffic violations could affect eligibility.
  * To have a valid California driver’s license.


### What Are the Requirements for the Nationwide Mature Driver Discount in Florida?
To qualify for the mature driver discount in Florida, you must:
  * Be at least 55 years old.
  * Have a valid Florida driver’s license.
  * You have not been involved in a crash for which you have been cited.
  * You have not pled guilty or been convicted of a moving violation.
  * Have a Nationwide insurance policy on at least one vehicle.

### How to Maintain the Progressive Mature Driver Discount
![How to Maintain the Progressive Mature Driver Discount](https://www.trafficsafetyinstitute.com/progressive-mature-driver-discount/)
The Progressive mature driver discount lasts for three years at a time. If you want to maintain it for longer than that, follow these steps:
**1. Take a renewal course.** You don’t need to take the full mature driver course again — but you do need to take a DMV-approved renewal course, which covers similar topics but is shorter. Once you pass the renewal course, you’ll receive another insurance discount certificate that’s good for another three years. This can be repeated every three years indefinitely for as long as you meet the other eligibility criteria for the program.
**2. Maintain a clean driving record.** Avoid at-fault accidents — being at fault in a car accident could jeopardize your discount. Traffic violations, especially severe ones like DUIs, can also disqualify you from the discount.
**3. Stay up-to-date with policy changes.** Insurance policies and discount requirements can change. Periodically review your policy and the terms of the discount to make sure you’re in compliance.
**4. Regularly review your insurance policies.** It’s a good idea to review your policy and discounts periodically to ensure you’re getting the maximum benefits and to check if there are other discounts you might qualify for.
