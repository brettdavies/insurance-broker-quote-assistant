## What Is the AAA Mature Driver Discount?
The AAA mature driver discount is a program offered by AAA that provides **discounted auto insurance rates to mature drivers who meet certain criteria**. The program is designed to recognize that older drivers have more experience behind the wheel — and are therefore less risky drivers.
To qualify for the AAA mature driver discount, you need to be at least 55 years old and have a valid driver’s license. Here we’ll take a look at the details of this program and how you can qualify.
![What Is the AAA Mature Driver Discount](https://www.trafficsafetyinstitute.com/aaa-mature-driver-discount/)
## What Are the Requirements for the AAA Mature Driver Discount?
To qualify for the AAA [mature driver discount](https://www.trafficsafetyinstitute.com/mature-driver-discount/) in California or Florida, you must:
  * Be at least 55 years old
  * Have a valid California or Florida driver’s license
  * Have AAA insurance coverage on at least one vehicle
  * Not have any at-fault accidents or DUIs on your driving record.
