## Associate Membership
### Organization Associate Membership
**Applies to** : All employees of non-insurers such as brokers, trade associations, consulting, and research firms.
### _Individual Associate Membership_
**Applies to** : Individual employees of non-insurers such as brokers, trade associations and consulting and research firms.
**Annual pricing** :
Revenue | Assessment  
---|---  
Organization | Individual*  
Less than $5M | $10,000 | $899  
$5M-$30M | $20,000 | $1,799  
Over $30M | $30,000 | $3,599  
_*Individual membership does not include complimentary presentations or confidential 1:1 support._
**Benefits** :
  * Triple-I’s extensive information services, research and publications.
  * Triple-I _Daily_ e-newsletter.
  * Access to the _Insurance Facts_ , continually updated throughout the year.
  * Exclusive members-only quarterly briefings on insurance economic and underwriting issues.
  * Permission to use Triple-I content for redistribution to management, employees, agents, and policyholders.*
  * Discount on event registration fees.
  * Potential to participate on Triple-I committees.*
  * Your company’s website listed on the [Industry Directory](https://www.iii.org/services/directory).*
