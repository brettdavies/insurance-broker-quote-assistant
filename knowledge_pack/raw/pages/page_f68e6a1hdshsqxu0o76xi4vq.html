Frequently Asked Questions

What is Progressive smart technology discount?

The Progressive smart technology discount rewards customers who use devices that monitor driving behavior, encouraging safe driving practices for potential savings on premiums.

Does Progressive accept defensive driving?

Yes, Progressive offers a defensive driving discount for policyholders who complete an approved defensive driving course, helping them save on their auto insurance premiums.

How much is Progressives one-time sign-online discount?

Progressives one-time sign-online discount typically provides a discount of around $50 when you manage your policy and sign documents electronically.

Does Progressive offer a referral program?

Progressive offers a referral program that lets current customers earn discounts or cash when they refer new clients. Its essential to understand this because knowing how to check if an auto insurance company is legitimate can help you ensure youre working with a reputable provider while maximizing your savings.

What is a Progressive Snapshot device?

The Progressive Snapshot device is a telematics tool that monitors your driving habits, including speed, braking, and time of day, to provide personalized feedback and potential discounts based on your driving behavior.

How does Progressive Snapshot work?

Progressive Snapshot works by collecting data from your driving habits over a set period. After this evaluation, you may receive a discount based on your safe driving practices. How long do I have to drive with Snapshot?

You typically need to drive with the Snapshot device for at least 30 days to gather enough data for an accurate assessment and potential discount.

Is Progressive deductible savings bank free?

Absolutely! The Progressive deductible savings bank is a no-cost program that enables policyholders to accumulate savings toward their deductible simply by keeping a clean driving record. If youre exploring the 10 cheapest auto insurance companies, this program could be a valuable way to reduce your out-of-pocket costs.

What discounts does Progressive offer for auto insurance?

Progressive offers various discounts, including the multi-policy discount, homeowner discount, sign-online discount, smart technology discount, defensive driving discount, and more, helping customers save on their premiums.

What is Progressive GPA?

Progressive GPA (Good Student Discount) is a discount available for young drivers who maintain a good academic record, typically requiring a GPA of 3.0 or higher to qualify for reduced premiums.
