Pennsylvania Mature Driver Improvement Course

Here is a driver improvement course designed specifically for Pennsylvania seniors 55 years of age an older. This opportunity allows you to refresh your driving techniques, regain lost confidence, and develop a positive driving attitude.

Upon completion of the seminar, you may qualify for a 5% or higher car insurance discount that lasts for 3 years; as mandated by PA State Law.

Seniors For Safe Driving is PennDOT approved and is accepted by all insurance companies in Pennsylvania (State Farm, Nationwide, Erie, Geico, Progressive, USAA, Travelers, etc.). We are the ONLY local PA based company who has been helping older citizens save money, while staying safe on the road, for over 30 years.

Our team of dedicated instructors and educators strive to provide the most comprehensive and enjoyable learning experience possible. Both our classroom and online programs are the number one insurance agent recommended PA highway safety education seminars statewide.

Why Take Our Online Pennsylvania Mature Driver Improvement Basic Course?

Save Money: PA seniors age 55 and older will receive a 5% discount on their auto insurance premium for a period of three years, for completing our driver education program. This discount is mandated by State Law and must be followed by all insurance companies in Pennsylvania.

Be Safe: The cars and the roads we drive them on are every changing. Our online Mature Driver Improvement Course is the easiest and most convenient way to be an informed driver and stay safe on the road.

Be Confident: Our Pennsylvania curriculum was designed specifically for older drivers 55 and up. All topics and lessons are geared towards making you a more confident driver.

Why Take Our Online Pennsylvania Mature Driver Improvement Refresher Course?

Maintain Your Discount: To maintain your senior car insurance discount, simply complete our PennDOT approved curriculum once every three years.

Stay Safe: As vehicle and driving technology continues to change and evolve, so will our online accident prevention lecture. We will do our best to keep you safe on the road!

Stay Confident: The next time you return to us, you will be three years older. That doesn’t mean you should be less confident in your driving skills. Our goal is to keep you a safe and confident driver for as long as possible.

Why Take Our Classroom Mature Driver Course?

Not everyone has access to the internet, nor does ever person learn best online. Some folks still enjoy being in a room filled with actual people, getting education from a real live person. This interaction with other drivers, who you share the road with, is something that you can only get with the classroom version of our seminar.

We are proud to announce that our award winning PennDOT-Approved In-Person Classroom Lecture is still available! With over 30 years of experience, SFSD is a name that you can trust.

Our team of dedicated educators work tirelessly year-round to keep our Pennsylvania senior citizens and our highways safe. They make every class a true learning experience. Their knowledge and experience is what continues to make Seniors For Safe Driving the statewide leader in highway safety education.

With over 150 locations to choose from, it is almost certain that we will have an event near you.
