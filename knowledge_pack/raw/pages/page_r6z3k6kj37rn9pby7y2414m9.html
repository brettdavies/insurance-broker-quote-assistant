What is the standard deduction in California?

The standard deduction reduces your taxable income,which results in a lower amount of taxes owed. For the 2024 tax year, the standard deduction in California is:

  • Married Filing Jointly, Head of Household, or Qualifying Surviving Spouse: $11,080 

Taking the standard deduction can simplify filing your California state income taxes. It automatically lowers your taxable income. This is beneficial if you are unable to itemize deductions.
