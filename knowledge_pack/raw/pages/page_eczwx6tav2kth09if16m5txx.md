## What are the benefits of Drive Safe & Save Business?
  * You’ll receive a one-time, participation premium decrease for each eligible vehicle you enroll and pair with a Bluetooth beacon.
  * At policy renewal, the premium adjustment is based on driving behaviors and may increase, decrease or remain the same.
  * Plus, the Drive Safe & Save Business portal lets you monitor your business vehicles with near real-time locations, detailed trip maps and driver feedback.


## What is the Drive Safe & Save Business portal?
The Drive Safe & Save Business portal gives you an inside look into your employee driving behaviors. Use the insights you gain from the portal to coach your team and help them build safer driving habits.
**The Drive Safe & Save Business portal lets you:**
  * Get near real-time vehicle location updates
  * See trip details and history
  * Monitor route efficiency
  * Review driver scores, trends and streaks
  * Share leaderboard rankings with your team for friendly competition

## How do I get started with Drive Safe & Save Business?
Contact your State Farm® agent to get started with Drive Safe & Save Business.
Here’s how the enrollment process works:
#### Step 1
Once you sign up through your agent, you’ll get an SMS text message with a link to download the Drive Safe & Save Business app.
#### Step 2
After you successfully log in to the app and give consent, we’ll send you a Bluetooth beacon for each vehicle you’re enrolling.
#### Step 3
Follow the in-app prompts to pair a Bluetooth beacon with each vehicle listed in your app. 
#### Step 4
The app also walks you through inviting other drivers to get started with the app.
## How is Drive Safe & Save Business different from Drive Safe & Save®?
![drive safe and save picture](https://www.statefarm.com/content/dam/sf-library/en-us/secure/legacy/small-business/pds/DSS.png)
#### Drive Safe & Save Business
This program is for small-to medium-sized commercial-type vehicles, like cargo vans, box trucks or flatbed trucks, that are used for business operations.
![state farm logo](https://www.statefarm.com/content/dam/sf-library/en-us/secure/legacy/small-business/pds/SFMA.png)
#### Drive Safe & Save
This program is for personal and private passenger-type vehicles, even some used for business purposes, like a real estate agent’s car.

## Frequently asked questions about Drive Safe & Save Business
* * *
Why should I enroll in Drive Safe & Save Business?
Enrolling in Drive Safe & Save Business helps you focus on driving safety and optimize your business operations.
Please talk to your [State Farm agent](https://www.statefarm.com/small-business-solutions/agent) for more information.
* * *
How does Drive Safe & Save Business affect my premium?
You’ll receive a one-time participation premium decrease for each eligible vehicle you enroll and pair with a Bluetooth beacon. At policy renewal, the premium adjustment is based on driving behaviors and may increase, decrease or remain the same.
* * *
How often is my premium adjustment calculated?
Your premium adjustment is calculated at each policy renewal (every six or 12 months).
* * *
Can I leave Drive Safe & Save at any time?
Yes. You can unenroll from Drive Safe & Save Business at any time.
If participation is voluntarily discontinued, a post program premium adjustment will remain for 12 months following the first renewal after discontinuation.
* * *
Will State Farm share my information with anyone else?
Your data is safe with us. State Farm won’t disclose information to third parties except as permitted by the [Notice of Privacy Policy](https://www.statefarm.com/customer-care/privacy-security/privacy).
* * *
How do employee drivers get started with the app?
The app walks you through inviting your employee drivers to join by sending a text with a link to download the Drive Safe & Save Business app.
* * *
How do I pair Bluetooth beacons with my vehicles?
If you’ve downloaded the app, you can follow in-app steps to pair a beacon. Once a beacon is paired with a vehicle, the app is ready to record trips for any user that drives that vehicle.
The app runs in the background, so you don’t need to log in every time you drive. But it’s a good idea to check the app often and make sure trips are recording.
* * *
Who is Cambridge Mobile Telematics?
State Farm has partnered with Cambridge Mobile Telematics (CMT) to bring you Drive Safe & Save.
You may see emails, texts or other communications from CMT, when you enroll in Drive Safe & Save Business.
* * *
What if my vehicles are already enrolled in Drive Safe & Save?
The same vehicle cannot be enrolled in both the Drive Safe & Save and Drive Safe & Save Business programs.
Only eligible commercially rated vehicles can be enrolled in Drive Safe & Save Business.
If you have vehicles already enrolled in Drive Safe & Save just keep your existing beacon in those vehicles and continue to use the State Farm app. Downloading the Drive Safe & Save Business app on the same phone should not interfere with the State Farm app.
