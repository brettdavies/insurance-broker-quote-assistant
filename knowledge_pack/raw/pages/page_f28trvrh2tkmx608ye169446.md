## State Farm Car Insurance Customer Reviews and Reputation
State Farm earns sixth place in overall customer satisfaction in MoneyGeek's comprehensive customer experience study. Based on customer feedback from the company's website and insurance forums, State Farm receives strong ratings for agent service and digital tools but faces more complaints related to claims processing than other major insurers. 
The following sections break down buying, policy management and claims process experiences based on MoneyGeek's research.
![find icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/find_nzkmut.svg)
Buying Process Experience
State Farm combines the personal touch of local agents with online quote capabilities, giving you flexibility in purchasing coverage. Agents are well-trained on State Farm's extensive product lineup and can explain coverage options clearly. The online process is user-friendly, though complex situations often benefit from agent consultation. As the largest auto insurer in the country, State Farm has agents in most communities, but service quality varies by location. The Drive Safe & Save program offers usage-based discounts during the quoting process.
