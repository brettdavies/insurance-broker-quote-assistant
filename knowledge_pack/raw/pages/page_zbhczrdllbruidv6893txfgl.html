Where Progressive stands out

Progressive’s car insurance offerings include a lot of bells and whistles. From accident forgiveness to loyalty rewards to deductible savings banks — all geared to widen the safety net of your policy.

Though the company doesn’t offer the cheapest rates, it doesn’t charge high rates, on average. Plus, Progressive offers many discounts to help you save, and its number of customer complaints is lower than average for companies of similar size, according to the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC).[2]

Where Progressive falls short

While Progressive offers the attractive features and benefits above, customer feedback suggests that customer service is just average. Progressive’s overall satisfaction and claims handling scores are both below the industry average.

Progressive Auto Insurance Coverages

Progressive’s policies are highly customizable, so you can choose between standard coverages and optional add-ons. Here’s an overview of the company’s coverages:

  • Liability: Covers bodily injury and property damage costs you cause to others in an accident.

  • Collision: Pays for damages a collision causes to your vehicle, regardless of fault.

  • Comprehensive: Covers non-collision damage to your car, including from vandalism, theft, weather, and animals.

  • Uninsured/underinsured motorist: Protects you if a driver with little to no coverage hits you.

  • Personal injury protection and medical payments: Pays for your or your passenger’s medical expenses, regardless of fault.

  • Loan/lease payoff: Gap insurance pays for the difference between your car’s value and the remaining car loan balance if it gets totaled in an accident.

  • Rental car: Helps cover the costs of a rental car while your car is in the shop getting repairs.

  • Roadside assistance: Provides towing and labor aid for flat tires, fuel delivery, lockouts, and more.

  • Custom parts: Covers aftermarket upgrades and accessories you’ve added to your vehicle.

  • Pet injury: Included with collision coverage, and helps pay pet bills if a car accident injures your pet.

Progressive Car Insurance Discounts

Car insurance discounts are an easy way to save money on premiums. Progressive offers the 12 discounts below that help lower your premium based on various eligibility factors.

  • Auto pay: Get a discount for setting up automatic payments.

  • Continuous coverage: You can earn a discount at renewal if you’ve avoided insurance lapses in the past.

  • Distant student: Earn a discount for student drivers living 100 miles or more away from home.

  • Good student: Students with good grades earn 5% (or more) off premiums.

  • Homeowner: Get a discount if you own your home, but savings vary.

  • Multi-car: Get an average discount of 12% for insuring more than one vehicle.

  • Multi-policy: Save an average of 7% for bundling car insurance with other insurance products.

  • Online quote: Get an average 7% discount when you get a quote online.

  • Paperless: You can save just by receiving your policy documents online.

  • Pay-in-full: Get a discount when you pay the whole policy term up front.

  • Sign online: Sign policy paperwork online and get an average 10% discount.

  • Teen driver: Teens earn a discount after one year of continuous insurance.

Filing a Claim With Progressive

Progressive makes it easy to file and manage claims 24/7 through its website, mobile app, or by phone. We’ve outlined the claims process in four steps.

  1. File your claim online, with the mobile app, or by calling the customer service line. Upload applicable pictures and detailed descriptions of the event. Progressive will assign a claims representative and estimator.

  2. The claims representative will schedule an adjuster to review the damage and provide an estimate for repairs.

  3. Choose your repair shop from anywhere, but Progressive also offers in-network shops where you can receive guaranteed repairs.

  4. Progressive manages the repair bills for you so you can pick up your car and go, and it guarantees in-network repairs for as long as you own or lease your car.

Snapshot Telematics program

Enroll in the Progressive Snapshot program to earn an automatic discount (in most states). Download the app, and allow it to monitor your driving habits. At renewal, Progressive rewards a discount based on your driving score. The company claims the average discount saves $322 per policy term.

Accident forgiveness

Progressive offers three ways for customers to get accident forgiveness:

  1. Small accident forgiveness: Locks your rate after a first claim totaling $500 or less, applied automatically. 

  2. Large accident forgiveness: Reserved for members of the Loyalty Rewards program, it forgives one accident for customers with a clean record who’ve stayed with the company for at least five years.

  3. Purchased accident forgiveness: Available to anyone for purchase and forgives up to one accident per policy period. 

Deductible Savings Bank

Add the Deductible Savings Bank, a diminishing deductible feature, to your car insurance policy. Progress will award every claims-free policy term period with a $50 reduction on your deductibles for a future claim. Over time, these savings can cover an entire deductible. Once used, the account resets, and you can continue to earn savings for claims-free driving.

Loyalty Rewards program

Progressive offers loyalty rewards to customers from day one, including minor-claim accident forgiveness. Benefits stack the longer you stay with the company, offering additional discounts and perks such as priority call routing and guaranteed renewal.

Progressive Auto Insurance FAQs

Here are quick answers to the most common questions drivers ask when considering a Progressive car insurance policy.

  • Is Progressive a good insurance company?

    Yes. Progressive is a good car insurance company with an AM Best financial strength rating of A+ (Superior).[3] But the company falls below average in claims handling and customer satisfaction (in all regions except California).

  • How much is Progressive car insurance?

    The average Progressive car insurance premium is $171 per month, according to our research. But rates can change dramatically depending on your driving profile, location, and vehicle. Always compare rates before buying.

  • Why is Progressive so expensive?

    Progressive can be expensive for some drivers, but whether your Progressive rates are high or low depends on how the company rates your unique risk factors. The higher it perceives your risk, the higher your rates will be.

  • What’s the best car insurance company?

    It depends. You won’t find one best car insurance company for every driver. The best car insurance company depends on your needs and budget. Progressive stands out for its customizable policies, wide range of discounts, and tech-friendly tools. 

    Other solid options include State Farm for its customer service and strong financial ratings, and Allstate for its range of coverages and strong nationwide availability.

  • Is Progressive known to deny claims?

    While Progressive isn’t known for denying claims, the company earns a low claims satisfaction rating from J.D. Power. It also received a low Trustpilot rating, with some online reviewers mentioning a pattern of claim denials.[4]
