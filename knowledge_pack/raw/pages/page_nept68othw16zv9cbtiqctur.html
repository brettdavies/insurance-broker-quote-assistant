    State Farm Claims Handling

    In our survey, 50% of State Farm customers were completely satisfied with the ease of filing a claim, and 28% were somewhat satisfied. Fifty percent were completely satisfied with how their claim was resolved, while 33% were somewhat satisfied.

    State Farm Customer Loyalty

    Seventy-one percent of survey respondents were either “likely” or “very likely” to recommend State Farm to someone shopping for car insurance. Eighty percent of respondents were either “likely” or “very likely” to renew their coverage with State Farm.

    State Farm’s sample annual rate of $2,204 per year is $192 per year higher than the national average. However, State Farm has sample premiums below the national average in a number of driver categories we analyzed. These include teen drivers, drivers with a speeding ticket, and drivers with a single accident or DUI on their record.

    Throughout this review, we use sample premiums for comparative purposes. These are based on specific driver profiles, vehicles, and coverage levels. Since car insurance rates are based on many individual factors, your actual costs will differ based on factors like your age, location, vehicle type, and coverage selections.

    Minimum coverage refers to state minimum liability requirements and other state-mandated coverage. Full coverage refers to $100,000/$300,000 bodily injury liability; $100,000 property damage liability; comprehensive and collision ($500 deductible); $100,000/$300,000 uninsured/underinsured motorist (UIM); and state-mandated coverage.

    State Farm’s Drive Safe & Save discount

    Your annual mileage and safe driving habits can save you up to 30% on your premium with the company’s Drive Safe & Save discount. State Farm’s app gathers data for each trip you take and provides feedback on elements like braking, acceleration, cornering, speed, and how often you’re distracted by your phone.

    “I haven't had to file any claims, but my husband and I switched to State Farm because we both purchased new vehicles, and our other insurance company greatly increased our rates. After comparison shopping, we received equal coverage at a much lower rate with State Farm. We then signed up for the UBI program, and after they tracked our driving habits for a few months, we both scored discounts because we're safe drivers.”
    Quiana Darden-King, Senior Insurance Editor for USN&WR

    State Farm offers the major coverage one expects, including liability (personal injury and property damage), collision, comprehensive, uninsured/underinsured motorist, medical payments (MedPay), and personal injury protection (PIP).

    In addition, you can customize your policy with add-ons like rental car reimbursement and emergency road service coverage. State Farm also offers rideshare coverage for policyholders who drive for transportation network companies like Uber or Lyft.
