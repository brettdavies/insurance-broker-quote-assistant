Discounts are one of the easiest ways to save money on your auto insurance. If you’re a current or former military member, you may be eligible for an exclusive discount from many car insurance companies.

Progressive offers a military discount only in Louisiana, where the law mandates insurers provide a discount for eligible military personnel. Active and retired military personnel in Louisiana can get up to a 25% discount on their Progressive auto insurance.

We’ll explain how to get Progressive’s military discount and other discounts the company offers.

What Is the Progressive Military Discount?

Progressive offers a military discount to the following military members in Louisiana:[1]

  • Active-duty military members

  • Military reservists

  • Louisiana Air and Army National Guard personnel

  • Retired military members

  • Veterans who are at least 50% disabled

Louisiana drivers who meet these qualifications can get up to 25% off their Progressive premium — but the discount may apply only to liability insurance coverage. Check with your Progressive agent to find out how much the military discount can save you.

How Much Can You Save With the Progressive Military Discount?

Military personnel in Louisiana may be eligible for up to 25% in savings on their Progressive auto insurance. But the amount you save can vary by policy, depending on your coverage and other discounts.

The military discount is less common than other car insurance discounts. Louisiana is the only state that requires insurers to offer a discount to military members. Progressive doesn’t provide much information about its military discount, most likely because the company only offers it in Louisiana.

How to Get the Progressive Military Discount

If you’re a Progressive customer, live in Louisiana, and are an active-duty military member or a veteran, contact Progressive to add the military discount to your auto insurance policy.

If you have insurance through another company, consider getting a car insurance quote from Progressive to see if it’s worth switching. Make sure you look at the same coverages, policy limits, and deductibles for an accurate price comparison.

Many Progressive customers find the insurer offers much cheaper rates than other companies. But price isn’t the only factor to consider. J.D. Power ranked Progressive below average in the Southeast region for customer service and claims satisfaction.

Other Progressive Discounts to Consider

Although Progressive offers a military discount only in Louisiana, the company offers other discounts in most states. Consider combining these discounts to save the most money on your car insurance policy:

  • Bundling discount: Progressive gives you a multi-policy discount if you combine your auto insurance with any other policy, including home, RV, boat, and motorcycle insurance. Your savings can vary depending on the policies in your bundle.

  • Continuous coverage discount: Depending on how long you’ve had continuous insurance, you could be eligible for a loyalty discount at your first policy renewal.

  • Teen driver discount: Insuring teens is expensive. But if you’ve been with Progressive for at least a year and add a driver aged 18 or younger, you can offset your premium’s increase with a teen driver discount.

  • Multi-car discount: Insuring at least two vehicles with Progressive can save you an average of 12% with the multi-car discount.

  • Online quote discount: Getting a Progressive insurance quote online can get you an average of 7% in savings — even if you complete your policy over the phone with a Progressive representative.

  • Sign online discount: Get an average of 9% savings by signing your policy documents online.

Progressive Military Discount FAQs

We answered the most common questions about Progressive’s military discount to help you get the most savings on your auto policy.

  • Does Progressive offer a discount for the military?

    Yes, Progressive offers a 25% car insurance discount for military members. But it’s available only in Louisiana, where the law requires all auto insurance companies to provide a discount to qualifying military members.

  • Which insurance company offers the largest military discount?

    USAA offers the largest military discount on car insurance. Deployed military members can save up to 60% on stored vehicles and up to 15% when parking their cars on a military base.

  • Is USAA cheaper than Progressive?

    Yes, USAA is cheaper than Progressive for car insurance, according to Compare.com data. USAA’s average monthly premium is $53 for liability-only coverage, while Progressive’s is $102.

  • Is car insurance cheaper for the military?

    Maybe. Car insurance can be cheaper for military members, but it depends on factors like your age, driving record, and ZIP code. USAA caters to military personnel and is one of the cheapest auto insurance companies, along with COUNTRY Financial, NJM, and Auto-Owners.
