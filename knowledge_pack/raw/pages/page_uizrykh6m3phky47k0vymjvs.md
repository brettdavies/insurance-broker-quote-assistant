### Progressive auto insurance coverage options
Progressive offers several coverage options you can add to your policy for an extra fee.
Rental car reimbursement
Progressive [rental reimbursement](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/rental-reimbursement-insurance-coverage "Rental Reimbursement Coverage: What Is It and Do I Need It?") pays for a rental vehicle after a  covered accident. Progressive will typically pay between $40 and $60 per day for a rental car similar to yours. 
Roadside assistance
[Roadside assistance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/roadside-assistance-car-insurance "Roadside Assistance: What Is It, and Do I Need It?") from Progressive pays for towing, a locksmith, gas and fluid delivery, flat tire changes and other emergency services if you're stuck on the side of the road. 
Rideshare coverage
[Rideshare insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-ridesharing-affects-your-auto-insurance "Rideshare Insurance: Why You Need It and What It Costs") is an essential add-on if you drive for a rideshare or delivery company like Uber or Lyft. It covers any gaps between your auto insurance coverage and the protection offered by the rideshare company.
Progressive offers rideshare insurance in  39 states and Washington, D.C. 
Gap insurance
Progressive [gap insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-gap-auto-insurance-works "Gap Insurance: How Does It Work and Do I Need It?") pays the difference between your balance on a loan or a lease and your car's value if it's totaled in an accident.
Progressive calls this coverage loan/lease payoff, and it's important if you have a new car. Your collision coverage will typically only cover the cost of your car up to its actual cash value. That means it factors in depreciation, wear and tear, which usually affects new cars the most.
**Without gap insurance, your insurance payout for a new car will likely be less than what you owe on a loan or lease.**
Progressive limits its gap insurance coverage to 25% over your vehicle's actual cash value. [Not all companies have this limit](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-gap-auto-insurance-works#companies "Where can you buy gap insurance?"). 
Custom parts and equipment coverage
Custom parts and equipment coverage from Progressive are a good idea if [your car has after-market parts](https://www.valuepenguin.com/special-auto-insurance-coverage-for-car-modifications "Do I Need Special Auto Insurance to Modify My Car?"). This can include stereos, navigation systems, wheels, grilles and other custom parts you've added to your car.
Progressive custom parts coverage typically has a $5,000 limit. 
Deductible savings bank
Progressive deductible savings bank lowers your deductible by $50 for every six months you don't make a claim. Other car insurance companies call this a [vanishing or diminishing deductible](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/vanishing-deductibles-what-it-and-should-you-get-it "Vanishing Deductibles: What Is It and Should You Get It?"). 
Mexico insurance
Progressive [Mexico insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/mexican-car-insurance "What Is Mexican Car Insurance, and Why Do You Need It?") protects people driving across the border. You'll need special insurance to drive legally in Mexico. This is the case whether you go there regularly for business or take a short vacation. 
Progressive also offers all the standard auto insurance coverages you need to drive legally in your state. You can choose [full coverage insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/what-full-coverage-car-insurance "What Is Full Coverage Car Insurance?") with comprehensive and collision to protect your car against damage.
Progressive customers now have access to a free accident response feature through the company's app. If you get in a crash, Progressive will automatically contact you to offer help. You can request emergency services or a tow through the app or by speaking to an agent, and Progressive will automatically file a claim on your behalf.
This service isn't available in California.
#### Does Progressive have accident forgiveness?
Progressive has a unique [accident forgiveness program](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/accident-forgiveness-what-it-and-who-offers-it "How Does Accident Forgiveness Work?"). It breaks accidents into two categories:
  1. Progressive includes **small accident forgiveness** for most new customers. Progressive considers an accident small if the claim is less than $500. 
  2. Progressive includes **large accident forgiveness** for customers who have had insurance with the company for more than five years. It only applies to drivers without a claim in five years or more. Large accident forgiveness will keep your rates the same even if the claim is more than $500. 


Some drivers can also buy accident forgiveness from Progressive when they get a policy.
**Progressive's accident forgiveness program can be very helpful if you cause a crash.** Without accident forgiveness, full coverage rates from Progressive go up by 49% after an at-fault accident. That's an average increase of $83 per month.
### Progressive auto insurance discounts
Progressive offers lots of discounts to drivers. Many of Progressive's discounts are easy to get, like a discount for getting a quote or buying your policy online.
Online quote discount
Save around 7% when you get a quote online and then buy on the phone. 
Sign online discount
Save around 9% when you sign your insurance documents online. 
Multicar discount
Get a discount of around 12% when you [insure multiple cars](https://www.valuepenguin.com/multi-car-insurance-discounts-quotes "How Much You Can Save With Multi-Car Auto Insurance") with Progressive. 
Continuous insurance discount
Get a discount based on how long you've been with your previous insurance company. 
Multipolicy discount
Save around 5% when you [bundle more than one policy](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-bundling-can-save-you-money-car-insurance "Bundling Insurance Policies Can Save 18% on Car Insurance") with Progressive. 
Homeowner discount
Get a discount of around 10% if you own a home, even if you don't have home insurance from Progressive. 
Paperless discount
Get a discount when you get policy documents and bills via email. 
Auto payment or pay-in-full discount
Earn a discount when you pay your entire bill upfront or sign up for automatic payments. 
Progressive also has three helpful discounts to lower the cost of car insurance for young drivers.
  * **Teen driver discount:** Add a child under 18-year-old to your policy. 
  * **Good student discount:** Save around 10% if you have a student on your policy with a "B" average or better. 
  * **Distant student discount:** Get a discount if you have a child on your policy who is away at college and doesn't have a car at school. 


**Progressive Snapshot discount**
[Progressive Snapshot](https://www.valuepenguin.com/progressive-snapshot-insurance-review "Progressive Snapshot") is a program that helps good drivers save money by tracking their driving habits using a device or mobile app and rewarding safe driving behavior.
If you sign up when you buy a new Progressive policy, you'll get a discount of around $25. Drivers who show safe habits over six months will get a discount. Progressive says drivers save an average of $146 per year with Snapshot.
**However, your rate can also go up if you have unsafe habits, like hard braking or driving late at night.** Progressive says that two out of every ten drivers end up with a higher rate rather than a discount.

### Progressive car and motorcycle insurance reviews and ratings
Progressive auto insurance has mixed customer service reviews.

### Progressive motorcycle insurance coverage
Progressive is one of the [best motorcycle insurance companies](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-motorcycle-insurance-companies "The Best Motorcycle Insurance Companies") for riders who need extra coverage.
Progressive's motorcycle insurance comes with the [basic coverages you'd expect](https://www.valuepenguin.com/what-does-motorcycle-insurance-cover "What Does Motorcycle Insurance Cover?"), along with extra features.
For example, all Progressive motorcycle policies include full replacement cost for parts. That means Progressive will pay to fix your bike with brand-new parts after an accident, regardless of any wear and tear beforehand.
**Progressive's generous coverages included on all policies**
If you buy a full coverage policy with comprehensive and collision coverage, you'll also get useful extras that most insurance companies don't include.
  * $3,000 of protection for custom parts and accessories, like a custom saddle or CB radio 
  * Full replacement cost for parts
  * Original manufacturer parts (OEM) coverage will fix your motorcycle with parts from the original manufacturer after an accident, as opposed to using aftermarket parts. 


Besides the extras that come with every policy, Progressive also offers a broad range of add-ons that cost more money, though they provide extra protection if you've customized your bike or depend on it for commuting or travel.
Total loss coverage
[Total loss coverage](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance/new-car-replacement-insurance "New Car Replacement Insurance") will replace your bike with a brand new model if it's totaled in an accident. This coverage is only available for a bike less than one model year old. 
Roadside assistance
[Roadside assistance and trip interruption](https://www.valuepenguin.com/motorcycle-roadside-assistance "A Guide to Motorcycle Roadside Assistance and Towing") will pay to tow your bike if it breaks down on the side of the road.
If you're on a road trip, this coverage will also pay up to $500 for your hotel, meals and transportation while your bike is in the repair shop. 
Carried contents coverage
Carried contents coverage will replace any personal belongings damaged in an accident, like your phone or camping gear. 
Custom parts coverage
[Extra accessories and custom parts coverage](https://www.valuepenguin.com/special-auto-insurance-coverage-for-car-modifications "Do I Need Special Auto Insurance to Modify My Car?") protects aftermarket upgrades to your bike up to $30,000. 
Disappearing deductible
[Disappearing deductible](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/vanishing-deductibles-what-it-and-should-you-get-it "Vanishing Deductibles: What Is It and Should You Get It?") lowers your deductible by 25% for each policy period that you remain claim-free. 
Enhanced injury protection
Enhanced injury protection pays up to $250 per week for two years if you're hurt on your bike and can't work. It will also pay your family $25,000 if you're in a fatal crash.
