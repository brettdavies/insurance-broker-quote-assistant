Geico's DriveEasy program

GEICO offers a usage-based insurance program called DriveEasy that can help low-mileage drivers save money. The program tracks mileage and other driving habits, saving policyholders up to 20%.

To qualify for GEICO's low-mileage discount, you typically need to drive fewer miles than the average driver. The exact mileage threshold can vary, but it generally applies to those who drive less than 7,500 miles per year. GEICO may require verification of your annual mileage to apply the low-mileage discount. This can be done through self-reporting or odometer readings. GEICO may ask for periodic odometer readings to verify your mileage.

When you select "Commute" as the vehicle's primary use, GEICO will ask you a few questions to determine your annual mileage estimate. You can also choose "Pleasure" as the primary use if your vehicle is rarely driven or only used for occasional errands.

In addition to the low-mileage discount, GEICO offers a variety of other discounts that can help lower your overall car insurance bill. For example, drivers can get a discount of up to 15% if they insure a car that's three years old or younger. You can also save up to 25% on your premium if you insure more than one car with GEICO. Opting for a higher deductible on any of your insurance policies from GEICO can also lower your premium. However, it's important to choose a deductible amount that you can still afford if you need to file a claim.

Practicing safe driving habits and avoiding moving violations can also help you qualify for lower GEICO insurance rates long-term. GEICO even offers a maximum 15% discount to drivers who take an approved defensive driving course.

Discounts for insuring multiple cars

GEICO offers a discount of up to 25% on most car insurance coverages when you insure more than one car with them. This multi-car discount is automatically applied when a customer insures two or more vehicles on the same GEICO policy. To be eligible for the discount, all vehicles must be:

  • Private passenger vehicles such as cars, SUVs, pickup trucks, or minivans.
  • Registered in the same state.
  • Located at the same address.
  • Equipped with only factory equipment (no aftermarket modifications).
  • Registered to an owner who is listed on your policy.

In addition to the multi-car discount, GEICO offers other ways to save on your car insurance premium. These include:

  • Discounts for safe drivers with a clean driving record.
  • Discounts for completing a defensive driving course.
  • Discounts for insuring a new vehicle that is 3 model years old or newer.
  • Discounts for vehicles with extra safety features such as anti-lock brakes, airbags, and advanced collision avoidance systems.
  • Discounts for bundling your auto coverage with other insurance policies such as home or renters insurance.
  • Discounts for signing up for Auto Pay.
  • Discounts for military personnel, including active duty, retired, and National Guard or Reserve members.

It is important to note that discount availability, amount, and eligibility criteria may vary between states and GEICO companies. To confirm your eligibility for any discounts, it is recommended to contact GEICO directly or visit their website for more information.

Discounts for insuring a new car

GEICO offers a range of discounts for customers insuring a new car. Firstly, if your new car is 3 model years old or newer, you could get up to a 15% discount on certain coverages. Safe drivers with a clean driving record can also save up to 22% on most GEICO coverages. This includes drivers who have completed a defensive driving course.

GEICO also offers a multi-vehicle discount of up to 25% on each car's premium when a customer insures two or more vehicles on the same policy. This discount also applies to motorcycles, RVs, and boats. Additionally, GEICO offers a multi-policy discount when you bundle your auto policy with other insurance policies such as homeowners, renters, motorcycle, and RV insurance.

If you're a student, you can also qualify for a good student discount by maintaining a B or B+ average or better. GEICO also offers discounts for government employees.

Furthermore, GEICO's DriveEasy program is a usage-based insurance program that tracks your driving habits and mileage, offering savings of up to 20%.

By taking advantage of these various discounts, you can significantly reduce your insurance premiums when insuring a new car with GEICO.
