## The Trusted Voice of Risk and Insurance
**For more than 60 years, the Insurance Information Institute (Triple-I) has been the trusted voice of risk and insurance, providing an understanding of what insurance is and how it works. Triple-I delivers unique, data-driven insights to educate, elevate, and connect industry professionals and the customers they serve.**
## Why Become a Triple-I Member
[Triple-I](https://www.iii.org/sites/default/files/docs/pdf/triple-i_2024_annual_report.pdf) promotes the essential value of insurance and risk management and addresses the issues challenging insurers, agents and brokers. As a Triple-I member, you can draw on a distinguished team of economists, actuaries, researchers and communications experts for impartial, fact-based information, analysis, and guidance.
  * Access insightful research and analysis of the insurance industry’s performance based on historical trends and key economic drivers for the current and coming years.* [

  * Elevate your expertise with insights based upon original research and comprehensive data analytics.
  * Benefit from knowledgeable industry spokespeople to address industry groups, testify before Congress, and educate regulatory bodies.


## Exclusive Member Benefits
Triple-I provides member-only research on vital insurance and finance-related topics to members:
  * Confidential economic profile reports provide you with key insights into the factors impacting financial performance.*
  * Bespoke presentations for your board, staff, agents, and brokers, on issues such as The State of the Industry, Legal System Abuse, and Economic Outlook Forecast.*
  * Experienced communications support to assist with media inquiries, provide earned media recommendations and thought leadership support.*
  * Member-only briefings keep you informed on weekly industry media issues and quarterly economic and underwriting forecasts.
  * Rapid response to your industry research requests


## Apply Now: Find the Membership for You
As part of its mission, Triple-I supports a variety of stakeholders. There are four types of Triple-I membership categories, learn more and apply:
  * [**Insurance Carrier**](https://www.iii.org/carriermember) – _available to property/casualty, life/health, and reinsurance companies. Dues are based on a company’s direct written premiums._
  * [**Associate**](https://www.iii.org/associatemember) – _available to brokers, associations, and companies providing services to the insurance industry. Dues are based on annual revenue._
  * [**Academia**](https://www.iii.org/academiamember) – _available to university professors, teachers, staff, and students._
  * **[Independent Agent Pro TM](https://www.iii.org/independentagentpro)** – _independent insurance agents can subscribe to access Triple-I resources._
