## Quick steps to cancel Progressive
### 1) Line up your replacement
Quote and bind your new policy with a future effective date. That way, you can cancel Progressive the same day your new policy starts.
### 2) Pick your cancellation date
Choose an end date that matches your new policy start. Avoid even a one-day gap—DMVs and lenders can penalize lapses.
### 3) Send a written request
Most carriers accept phone or app cancellations, but a short _written_ request is cleanest. Use our letter generator.
### 4) Confirm & save proof
Ask for written confirmation showing the cancellation date. Save it with your ID cards and lender/HOA docs.
## Best timing & how to avoid lapses
Cancel on the **same day** your new policy takes effect. If you’re selling a car, cancel on the sale date—after you’ve transferred title and removed plates if your state requires it. Moving to a new state? Start the replacement policy with your new garaging address first; then cancel the old policy.
Situation | When to cancel | What to check  
---|---|---  
Switching to a new insurer | Same day your new policy begins | Verify ID cards active before cancelling  
Selling a vehicle | Day of sale/plate return (state rules apply) | Bill of sale, plate/DMV requirements  
Moving states | After new policy is bound at new address | DMV registration, lender garaging requirements  
Vehicle in storage | Consider comp-only or temporary garaging change | Keep coverage for theft, fire, weather  
## How to cancel Progressive (channels & documents)
Progressive typically allows cancellation _by phone, through the app/online account, by letter_ , or via your independent agent. The table summarizes what you’ll usually need.
Channel | What you’ll need | Tips  
---|---|---  
App / Online account | Policy number, effective cancellation date | Download confirmation page or email  
Phone (number on ID card) | Policyholder authentication; date you want to end | Request written confirmation by email  
Written letter (mail or upload) | Name, policy #, VIN/address, desired end date, signature | Use our generator for a clean, signed request  
Agent-assisted (us) | Same details as above | We’ll help time it with your new policy & lender needs  
Mailing addresses and steps vary by policy type and state. Use the address shown on your declarations page or cancellation instructions inside your account/app.

## Refunds, fees & billing cleanup
Your final bill depends on billing plan, earned premium to date, and state rules. Many policies refund any _unearned premium_ when you cancel mid-term—especially if you prepaid. Auto-pay? Turn it off after the final invoice posts and your refund (if any) hits.
Item | What to expect | What to do  
---|---|---  
Prorated refund | Unused days returned after cancellation | Confirm amount & method (card, ACH, check)  
Minimum earned | Some policies keep a minimum % when cancelling early | Check declarations or billing schedule  
Fees | Installment or policy fees may be non-refundable | Review your last invoice for fee lines  
Auto-pay | Future drafts should stop after cancel posts | Verify “paid in full” status before disabling  
## After you cancel: what to do next
### DMV & plates
Some states require plate return or proof of continuous insurance. Check your DMV guidance to avoid fines.
### Lienholder/lessor
Auto lenders and mortgage companies want uninterrupted coverage. Share your new ID cards/evidence of insurance.
### Proof of prior insurance
Save your Progressive declarations and cancellation confirmation—handy for future underwriting and discounts.

## Cancel Progressive — FAQs
Can I cancel Progressive mid-term?
Yes, most policies can be cancelled at any time. Your final bill accounts for earned premium and any fees in your state/policy.
Will I get a refund after cancelling?
If you paid ahead, you’ll typically receive a prorated refund of the unused premium, minus non-refundable fees. Timing and method vary by billing plan.
Do I need to send a cancellation letter?
Phone or app may work, but a short written request creates clean documentation. Use our generator and keep the confirmation email.
What if my state requires plate surrender?
Follow your DMV’s plate and proof-of-insurance rules to avoid fines. Cancel on the date you return plates or transfer ownership.
How do I avoid a coverage lapse?
Bind your new policy first and set your Progressive cancellation for the same day. We’ll help you match dates and deliver new ID cards.
