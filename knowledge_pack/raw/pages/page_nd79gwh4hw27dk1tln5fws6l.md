Overall, Geico offers cheaper car insurance than Esurance. However, some drivers may find rates from Esurance more affordable.

### Cheapest for drivers with prior incidents
For drivers with a speeding ticket on their record, Esurance offers cheaper rates than Geico. A full-coverage policy costs $1,752 per year, which is $406 cheaper than Geico.
If you've been involved in an at-fault accident, Geico's rates are much more affordable. At $1,380 per year for full-coverage, Geico costs $781 less than Esurance. ## Geico vs. Esurance: compare coverage options
Most coverages offered by Esurance and Geico are the same. The main differences are that Geico provides accident forgiveness and mechanical breakdown coverage, whereas Esurance does not. On the other hand, Esurance offers gap insurance, which is not available at Geico.
Both Esurance and Geico offer full-coverage car insurance policies that include the [standard coverages required or recommended in most states](https://www.valuepenguin.com/minimum-car-insurance-required-your-state "What Is the Minimum Car Insurance Required in Your State?"), plus additional types of coverage.

## Esurance vs. Geico: customer service reviews
Geico has better customer service scores and a higher financial strength rating than Esurance.

Geico receives fewer complaints than expected for a company of its size, according to the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC). Esurance, on the other hand, scored higher than the national median, which indicates the insurer received more complaints than expected.
The most common complaints against Esurance were related to claims processing delays and unsatisfactory settlements.
Geico also earned a much higher rating from our editors. Our rating takes into account a company's rates, coverage offerings and customer service scores. So we believe it's a better company overall.
## Frequently asked questions
### Is Esurance part of Allstate?
Esurance is owned by [Allstate](https://www.valuepenguin.com/allstate-auto-insurance-review "Allstate Auto Insurance Review"), but until 2023, the two insurers operated independently. Rates, coverage options, discounts and availability vary between the two, so drivers should consider getting a quote from both companies when comparing car insurance.
### Is Esurance cheaper than Geico?
Esurance is cheaper than Geico in some instances. A full-coverage policy from Esurance costs $1,372 per year, which is $8 less than Geico. Drivers with [a recent DUI](https://www.valuepenguin.com/compare-esurance-vs-geico-car-insurance#incidents "#incidents") will also find cheaper rates from Esurance. However, Geico offers more affordable rates for young drivers and those looking for a minimum-coverage policy.
### Is Esurance good auto insurance?
[Esurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/Esurance-insurance-review "Esurance review") is a good auto insurance company. It offers all of the standard coverages you would expect from a major insurer and has a solid financial stability score from A.M. Best. However, drivers may be able to find cheaper rates and better customer service elsewhere.
## Methodology
This analysis used car insurance quotes from both Esurance and Geico across thousands of ZIP codes in California.
Our sample driver is a 30-year-old man with a clean record who drives a 2015 Honda Civic EX, unless otherwise noted. Young driver quotes are based on an 18-year-old man.
Quotes are based on the following coverage levels:
Coverage type | Minimum coverage | Full coverage  
---|---|---  
Bodily injury liability | $15,000/$30,000 | $50,000/$100,000  
Property damage liability | $5,000 per accident | $25,000  
Uninsured/underinsured motorist bodily injury | waived | $50,000/$100,000  
Comprehensive and collision | waived | $500 deductible  
ValuePenguin's analysis used insurance rate data from Quadrant Information Services. These rates were publicly sourced from insurer filings and should be used for comparative purposes only — your own quotes may be different.
