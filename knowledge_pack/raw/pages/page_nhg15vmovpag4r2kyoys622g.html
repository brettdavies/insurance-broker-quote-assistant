Got More Questions?
Can I take this course if I’m not a GEICO customer? 

Yes! While this page was created for GEICO customers to access a special discounted rate, anyone can enroll and complete the course. 

Will I be able to use the course certificate if I switch insurance carriers? 

Yes. The certificate is valid with any licensed auto insurer in New York. By state law, all carriers must honor the discount.  The certificate is valid for 3 years since the day of completion. 

Can my spouse or another family member take the course and get a discount? 

Yes, any driver in your household can take the IMPROV Defensive Driving course and save 10% off their vehicle’s premium for three years.

How much can I save on my auto insurance? 

New York drivers are eligible for a 10% discount on their liability and collision premiums for three years after completing a DMV-approved defensive driving course.

Can I reduce points on my license with this course? 

Yes. If you have points on your record, you may be able to reduce up to 4 points to help prevent suspension. The DMV applies this automatically once you complete the course.

Is this course approved by the New York DMV? 

Yes. This is a fully approved Internet Point and Insurance Reduction Program (IPIRP) accepted by the NY DMV and all insurance carriers.

How do I get my certificate after I finish? 

We’ll mail your original certificate and electronically notify the DMV—both at no extra cost. We will notify Geico on your behalf and mail you an original copy of your certificate.
