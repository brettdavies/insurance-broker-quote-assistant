Progressive has the best motorcycle insurance for most people. It offers extra coverage and reliable customer service at an affordable rate. A full coverage policy from Progressive costs around $23 per month. That's $10 per month cheaper than average.

All of Progressive's motorcycle insurance policies pay to fix your motorcycle without factoring in wear and tear, called full replacement cost coverage.

Progressive includes $3,000 of coverage for custom parts, upgrades and customizations when you get collision and comprehensive coverage. This means your policy covers things like a custom saddle or stereo. And you can pay extra to raise the limit up to $30,000 if needed.

In addition, Progressive will replace any original or custom parts on your bike with the same or better parts. This is called original equipment manufacturer (OEM) coverage.

Progressive gets 35% fewer complaints compared to an average company of the same size. . That means you can count on Progressive to fix your bike quickly after an accident.

However, Progressive doesn't offer rental reimbursement coverage for motorcycles. You may want this coverage if you don't have another vehicle or if you ride your bike every day.

Dairyland has the best motorcycle insurance rates in the country.

On average, policies cost $25 per month, with rates as low as $14 per month in some states. That makes Dairyland a great choice for low-cost motorcycle insurance.

Dairyland customers can add on roadside assistance and rental reimbursement coverage at an extra cost. These coverages make it a great choice for riders who use their motorcycle as their main vehicle.

  • Emergency roadside assistance includes service for flat tires, bad batteries, mechanical or electrical problems, fuel, fluid, oil or water refills and tow service.
  • Rental reimbursement helps cover the cost of a rental vehicle while your motorcycle is in the shop after a crash. This is an important coverage to have if you ride your bike to get to work or for everyday activities.

In addition, Dairyland will pay to fully restore or replace your motorcycle after a crash like it's brand new if it's less than three years old. This is usually called new vehicle replacement or replacement cost coverage. Many other companies have a mileage limit or set a one-year cap on full replacement coverage.

Riders aren't thrilled with the service they get from Dairyland, though. It gets 60% more complaints than an average insurance company its size. If you're in an accident, it may take longer to settle your claim with Dairyland compared to other companies.

The Harley Davidson insurance company offers lots of ways for riders to save on its already affordable motorcycle insurance.

Full coverage Harley-Davidson motorbike insurance costs just $24 per month, on average. That's $9 per month cheaper than the national average, making it one of the most affordable motorcycle insurance companies. In addition, it offers 13 discounts to help you lower your motorcycle insurance rates.

  • Anti-theft discount for riders who install an audible alarm or LoJack system on their bike
  • Bundled protection discount if you buy multiple insurance products from Harley-Davidson , like a planned maintenance policy
  • Claim-free renewal discount if you avoided making a claim during the last policy period
  • Experienced rider discount based on the number of years you've had a motorcycle license
  • H-D Riding Academy discount when you complete the H-D Riding Academy New Rider Course
  • H.O.G. membership discount for members of the Harley Owners Group
  • Homeowner discount for riders who own a home and have homeowners insurance from any company
  • Loyalty discount for riders who have Harley-Davidson insurance for more than one year
  • Military and law enforcement discount for active or retired military or law enforcement members
  • Motorcycle endorsement discount for riders with a motorcycle endorsement on their license
  • Multi-cycle discount when you insure more than one motorcycle with Harley-Davidson
  • New bike discount for riders with a brand-new motorcycle
  • On-time payer discount for making your motorcycle insurance payments on time

Harley-Davidson also has lots of coverage add-ons to help you customize your motorcycle insurance policy. Options include roadside assistance, rental reimbursement coverage, vacation rental coverage and custom equipment coverage.

The main downside to Harley-Davidson insurance is that it doesn't sell car, home or cheapest renters insurance policies. If you need to protect another vehicle, your home or your belongings, you'll have to get a policy from a different company. That can be inconvenient, especially if you have a major emergency, like a fire that damages both your home and bike.

Geico has good motorcycle insurance for young riders because it lets you pay your bill in installments.

Young bikers on a budget may find this helpful. In comparison, many companies require you to pay your annual rate in full upfront. However, you'll probably have to pay Geico a small fee for each installment.

Geico's motorcycle insurance isn't typically the cheapest option. The average cost of a full coverage policy is $36 per month. That's $3 per month more than the national average. But Geico lets you lower your insurance costs with a number of discounts. For example, young riders who take a Motorcycle Safety Foundation or Military Safety Course get a 10% discount.

Geico motorcycle insurance allows you to customize your coverage to meet your needs. That means policies don't automatically include extra coverage young riders may not need. However, it doesn't offer some popular upgrades, like original equipment manufacturer (OEM) coverage.

USAA doesn't offer motorcycle insurance directly. It refers its members to Progressive #progressive , one of the best companies for motorcycle insurance.

Progressive offers a 5% discount to USAA members, making its coverage even more affordable. On average, a full coverage Progressive policy costs $22 per month after the USAA discount.

Many bikers can't buy USAA motorcycle insurance, though. It's only available to military members, veterans and their families.

USAA also offers motorcycle loans and financing for up to 100% of the value of your new bike. That makes it a one-stop shop for people looking to buy a new motorcycle.

Besides motorcycles, USAA also sells insurance for ATVs, mopeds and scooters.

Choosing a company with reliable customer service is just as important as finding the cheapest rate. Consider Progressive, Nationwide, Allstate or Geico motorcycle insurance quotes, since these companies get a low number of complaints.

Great customer service can make the claims process less stressful after an accident. Companies with poor service reviews may take longer to process your claim. You may also end up paying more out of pocket.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best motorcycle insurance company?

The best motorcycle insurance depends on your coverage needs, the type of motorcycle you have, your riding habits and where you live. For example, Dairyland #dairyland has the best rates for most riders. But Progressive #progressive is the best company for custom bikes because of its OEM and custom parts coverage.

What's the cheapest motorbike insurance?

Dairyland has the cheapest motorbike insurance, with an average rate of $22 per month. That's $17 per month cheaper than the national average.

Is Geico or Progressive better for motorcycle insurance?

Progressive has better motorcycle insurance for most people. Full coverage motorcycle insurance from Progressive costs around $23 per month. That's $13 per month less than a Geico motorcycle insurance policy. In addition, Progressive's basic policy includes more coverage than a standard Geico motorcycle insurance policy.

How much is good bike insurance?

The average cost of full coverage motorcycle insurance is $33 per month in the U.S. However, you may be able to get good bike insurance for less. The best motorcycle insurance company for most people, Progressive, charges an average of $23 per month.

Methodology

To find the best insurance for a motorcycle, ValuePenguin collected thousands of quotes from top insurance companies across the country. Rates are for a 45-year-old single man who owns a 2018 Honda CMX500 Rebel.

Quotes are for a full coverage policy, which includes higher liability limits than the state requirement, along with comprehensive and collision coverage.

  • Bodily injury liability: $50,000 per person and $100,000 per accident
  • Property damage liability: $25,000 per accident
  • Uninsured and underinsured motorist bodily injury: $50,000 per person and $100,000 per accident
  • Uninsured and underinsured motorist property damage: $25,000 per accident
  • Medical payments: $5,000
  • Comprehensive and collision deductible: $500

To choose the best motorcycle insurance company in each category, our experts considered the coverages offered, rates, available discounts and customer service reputation.

Insurance company ratings from ValuePenguin editors and the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) Complaint Index were evaluated to determine the best companies.

  • ValuePenguin editor's ratings are based on customer service reviews, coverage availability and the overall quality a company provides its riders.
  • The NAIC Complaint Index scores insurance companies based on the number of complaints they receive compared to other similar-sized companies. Scores are based on all private passenger auto insurance complaints, which includes motorcycle insurance complaints.
