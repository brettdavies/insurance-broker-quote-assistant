## State Farm Profile/Overview
State Farm was well known for being labelled as a “good neighbor” by “being there” for the customers. It was initially founded in 1922 by a retired farmer and insurance salesman named George Jacob “G.J.” Mecherle. Humble beginnings indeed became the origins of the State Farm. Currently, the company insures more cars and homes than any other insurer in the United States mutual company without compromising the focus on their policyholders.
Stories traced and began with the founder’s vision for the company. This is, “operate fairly and do the right thing for our customers.” Mecherle’s vision became the lighthouse of the company and became the core guidance up to this day.
From a humble single-line auto insurance company, State Farm now offers to close 100 products and service in five comprising, extensive service lines. Estimating to 35,000 claims per day, State Farm has established a reputable name to the US market. Certain involvement to other institutions and organization is their next step onward.
In terms of ranking in the Fortune 500 list, State Farm is currently ranked number 35 which is a pretty solid spot to be in. Employee directory leads to a number of 70,425 and going up as the operation continues.

  1. Car insurance
  2. Home, condo, and renter insurance
  3. Life insurance
  4. Health insurance
  5. Disability insurance
  6. Annuities
  7. Business

  1. Checking

  4. Home loans
  5. Savings, CDs and more
  6. Pocket Agent Mobile App

State Farm’s **financial services** span the following lines of services:

  7. Mutual funds
