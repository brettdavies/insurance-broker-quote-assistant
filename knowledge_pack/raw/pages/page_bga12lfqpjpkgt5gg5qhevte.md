Progressive is an American insurance company that offers homeowners insurance, among other types of coverages.   
  
The company was founded in 1937 and today has the 15th largest market share of all American homeowners insurers. It sells homeowners policies in 19 states and the District of Columbia.

## Financial Strength
### Financial Strength - 5 / 5
Progressive enjoys high ratings from Standard & Poor's, Moody's, and AM Best.   
  
The company appears to be on a sound financial footing. Though they only write homeowners policies in 19 states, these have enough geographic diversity to protect the company against heavy and localized claims experience resulting from a natural disaster or other event.

## Progressive Coverage
### Coverage Options - 4.5 / 5
Progressive offers the usual [suite of homeowners insurance coverages](https://www.consumersadvocate.org/home-insurance/home-insurance-basics) - structures, personal property, and liability.   
  
It also offers the most commonly available optional coverages, in addition to an unusual inflation coverage, which automatically adjusts insurance levels to keep up with rising building and repair costs.

## Pricing & Discounts
### Pricing and Discounts - 4.5 / 5
Many companies claim that automating their policy initiation service reduces costs and saves consumers money.   
  
As evidenced by Progressive's above-average rating for pricing, however, this is one company that appears to have lived up to its claims. Discounts are available for customers that already hold other Progressive policies, have home security systems, or are retired.
