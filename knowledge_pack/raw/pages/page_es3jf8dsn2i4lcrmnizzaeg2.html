Liberty Mutual RightTrack vs. Progressive Snapshot

Liberty Mutual and Progressive both offer discounts based on how you drive. You'll save by having good driving habits. The usage-based programs track your driving habits using a cellphone app or plug-in telematics device.

The RightTrack usage-based program from Liberty Mutual is better than Progressive Snapshot.

If you're unsure about your driving habits, Liberty Mutual's program is a better choice for you. That's because Liberty Mutual won't raise your rates if you have bad driving habits. On the other hand, 2 out of 10 drivers who sign up for Progressive Snapshot see an increase in their rates. Liberty Mutual also doesn't track handheld phone use.

Progressive doesn't currently have a pay-per-mile program.

Progressive vs. Liberty Mutual home insurance coverage

Liberty Mutual home insurance gives you better coverage for your belongings than Progressive.

Frequently asked questions

Is Liberty Mutual cheaper than Progressive?

Liberty Mutual's home insurance is cheaper than Progressive #home-quote . But its auto insurance is more expensive. You can use Liberty Mutual's many home and auto insurance discounts #discounts to lower your rates.

Does Progressive have 24-hour customer service?

Yes, Progressive's call center is open 24 hours a day. You can speak to Progressive customer service by calling 888-671-4405. Or you can chat or email with Progressive through its website.

Does Progressive offer accident forgiveness?

Yes, Progressive automatically gives most drivers accident forgiveness for small claims less than $500. You can buy large accident forgiveness if you want protection for accidents that cost more than $500. You can also get large accident forgiveness for free after having Progressive for five years without any claims.

Does Liberty Mutual offer roadside assistance?

Yes, Liberty Mutual offers roadside assistance #coverage as an optional add-on. Its 24-hour program includes towing, battery jump-start, fuel delivery, flat tire replacement and locksmith services.

Does Progressive offer rental car discounts?

Yes, Progressive's PerkShare program includes discounts to rental car companies, including Enterprise, National and Alamo.
