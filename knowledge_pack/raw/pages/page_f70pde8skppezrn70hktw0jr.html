Pricing & Discounts — FAQs

Is Progressive affordable for a young driver in California?

Often competitive with good student, multi-car, and continuous insurance factors… Snapshot can help careful drivers.

Which discounts are common?

Multi-policy, multi-car, homeowner, paperless, EFT, Snapshot, and safe driver. Availability varies by address and profile.

What are fast ways to lower my rate?

Raise deductibles, bundle home or renters, and compare quotes. See instant quotes.

Do vehicles matter a lot?

Yes… modest sedans like Honda Civic or Toyota Camry usually price better than luxury or sports models.

Do married drivers always pay less than single?

Sometimes… but violations, credit, and vehicle often matter more. We test all combos.

Are motorcycle or ATV discounts available?

Cycle insurance can be paired for value… riders on Harley and Yamaha often see savings when bundled.

Policy & Coverage — FAQs

Full coverage versus liability only?

We quote liability only or full coverage with comprehensive and collision. Start with our full coverage explainer. If you are researching other states, compare cheap full coverage options in Florida.

Which liability limits should I pick?

Many choose 100/300/100 with UM and UIM matched… we show step-up costs so the choice is simple.

Does comprehensive cover glass?

Usually yes… sometimes with a separate glass deductible. See the comprehensive vs collision guide.

What about roadside and rental reimbursement?

Optional add-ons… easy to price… helpful if you commute or travel often.

Is it MedPay or PIP in California?

California uses MedPay… we can price with and without so you compare.

How do classic or collector vehicles work?

Progressive has options… for agreed value or special needs we compare partners too.

Liability

Protects others you injure… common pick 100/300/100… add UM and UIM where offered.

Comprehensive

Theft, hail, glass, animals… typical deductibles 500 to 1000.

Collision

Crash with a vehicle or object… higher deductibles can reduce premium.

Add-ons

Roadside assistance… rental reimbursement… gap or new-car options.

Snapshot & Telematics — FAQs

What is Progressive Snapshot?

A telematics program in the app or device that evaluates driving habits… safer patterns may earn a discount. Neutral background at Wikipedia.

How long is the Snapshot review?

Usually a few weeks… we confirm your exact terms when we quote.

Can Snapshot ever raise my rate?

In some profiles it can… we quote with and without Snapshot so you decide.

Do all drivers in my household need the app?

Often yes for the best read on risk… we help set it up for each driver.

What about privacy concerns with telematics?

We walk through data use and opt-out choices so you are comfortable.

Claims & Payments — FAQs

How do I file a claim?

File with us or with Progressive… we help with estimates and rental arrangements. See shopping and timing tips.

Will a comprehensive claim raise my premium?

It can… usually less than an at-fault collision claim. Frequency matters more than one event.

Do I pay my deductible if the other driver is at fault?

You might at first… your insurer may recover it later through subrogation.

How fast can I get proof of insurance?

Same day in most cases… digital ID cards after bind.

What payment plans are common?

Paid-in-full or monthly with EFT or card… paperless and EFT can help pricing.

Can I change deductibles mid-term?

Usually yes… premium adjusts pro-rata.

Can I exclude a driver?
