## Progressive — Los Angeles FAQs
**Is Progressive the cheapest in LA?**
No single carrier is cheapest for everyone. Rates depend on drivers, vehicles, and ZIP. We quote Progressive and competitors side-by-side to find the best _value_ for your profile.
**Do I need uninsured/underinsured motorist (UM/UIM)?**
LA traffic density makes UM/UIM an important part of protecting income and assets. We’ll recommend limits aligned to your risk tolerance and budget.
**Can I get same-day proof of insurance?**
Yes. Most policies issue digital ID cards immediately after binding. We can also send certificates to lenders or fleet managers.
**How does rideshare coverage work?**
Personal policies can exclude periods when the app is on. Rideshare endorsements (where available) help bridge gaps between personal and TNC coverage. We’ll tailor coverage to your platform and driving pattern.
**How do I lower my premium?**
Bundle lines, keep continuous insurance, consider telematics/usage-based programs, raise deductibles you can afford, and right-size coverage. We’ll show savings for each choice before you buy.
