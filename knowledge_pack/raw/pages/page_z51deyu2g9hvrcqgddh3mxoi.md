##  Types of Geico car insurance
[Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163) offers car insurance in all 50 states and Washington, D.C.
### Basic coverage
A [full coverage policy](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-full-coverage-car-insurance/) is typically required if you leased or financed your car.
  * **Liability car insurance:** Minimum coverage allowed in most states,[liability ](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-liability-car-insurance/)pays for damages if you or someone else driving your car is at fault for an accident.
  * **Collision coverage:** Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident.
  * **Comprehensive coverage:** Pays for damages not caused by a collision, including theft, [weather damage](https://www.cnbc.com/select/does-car-insurance-cover-hail-damage/) or hitting an animal.


### Additional coverage
These policies are required in some states**.** Note:
  * **Personal injury protection insurance:** Also known as no-fault insurance,
  * **Medical payments insurance:** Covers medical bills for you, your passengers and any pedestrians, regardless of fault.
  * **Uninsured/underinsured motorist insurance:** Covers repairs and medical bills if you are in an accident with a driver with little or no insurance.


### Add-ons
Geico offers a selection of coverage options.
  * **Accident forgiveness:** Geico won't raise your rates after your first at-fault collision. Accident forgiveness is free if you remain accident-free for five years or it can be bought as an upgrade when you purchase or renew your policy.
  * **Emergency road service:** Helps with towing, winching, jump-starting, tire changing, lockouts, and fuel delivery through Geico's mobile app. It starts at $14 per year.
  * **Mechanical breakdown insurance:** This coverage can cover repairs after a manufacturer's warranty expires.
  * **Rental car reimbursement:** Pays for a rental while your car is being repaired

##  Geico car insurance discounts
There are plenty of opportunities to lower your [Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163) premiums, with discounts based on driving history and habits, affiliation, education and customer loyalty. Not all discounts are available in all states and some discounts may not be combined
  * **Anti-theft system:** A car alarm, tracking system, VIN etching and other security features can save you up to 23% on comprehensive coverage.
  * **Defensive driving:** Save on your premiums when you complete an approved defensive driving course.
  * **Driver's education.** Young drivers who complete a driver's ed course can earn a discount on most policies.
  * **Eagle discount:** Current or retired federal employees may qualify for up to 12% off.
  * **Good driver:** If you remain accident-free for five years, you can save up to 22% off your rate.
  * **Good student:** Full-time students with a good academic record could get a discount of up to 15% on certain policies.
  * **Military discount:** Active duty or retired military are eligible for a discount of up to 15%. If you're part of an emergency deployment, you could receive a rate reduction of up to 25% 
  * **Membership discount:** Receive rate discounts and other benefits if you're a member of one of Geico's 500-plus partner organizations, including alumni associations, fraternities and sororities, and professional organizations
  * **Multiple vehicles:** Get 25% off most coverages when you insure more than one vehicle with Geico
  * **Multiple policies:** Save when you [bundle auto and homeowners insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/best-home-auto-insurance-bundles/) from Geico.
  * **New vehicle:** If your car model is three years or newer, you can get a discount of up to 15%.
  * **Safe driving:** Eligible drivers can save up to 25% using Geico's DriveEasy app.
  * **Seat belt use:** Always buckle up and get a discount on the personal injury protection and medical payments portion of your premium
  * **Safety equipment:** Airbags, anti-lock brakes, daytime running lights and other safety devices can save you as much as 23%

## FAQs
### Why is Geico so much cheaper?
Because it sells insurance directly to consumers and doesn't have to pay commissions to brokers, Geico can offer some of the lowest rates in the market. Its many discounts allow policyholders to save even more.
### Who is better: Geico or Progressive?
Both Geico and Progressive are solid choices for auto insurance. Geico stands out for low rates and high customer satisfaction, while Progressive tends to offer lower premiums to high-risk drivers.
### Does Geico cover rental cars?
A Geico auto insurance policy typically extends coverage to a rental car but only to the limits you have for your vehicle. In addition, Geico will pay for a rental if your car is being repaired after a covered incident.
### Who owns Geico?
Geico is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, which also issues property and casualty reinsurance policies.
### Does Geico offer gap insurance?
Geico doesn't offer [gap insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-gap-insurance/). Although it's usually more expensive, you may be able to buy coverage from your dealership.
