# State Farm® Names Joe Park as Executive Vice President and Chief Digital & Information Officer
[ ![Post this release on X](https://content.presspage.com/assets/8f5a482826e7b872907d81ba224126ea7d62b233/images/x-post-flat.png) ](https://x.com/intent/post?text=State%20Farm%C2%AE%20Names%20Joe%20Park%20as%20Executive%20Vice%20President%20and%20Chief%20Digital%20&%20Information%20Officer&url=https%3A%2F%2Fnewsroom.statefarm.com%2Fstate-farm-names-joe-park-as-executive-vice-president-and-chief-digital--information-officer%2F)
State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company announced today that it has named Joe Park as Executive Vice President and Chief Digital & Information Officer. Park will join State Farm in October.
Park joins State Farm from Yum! Brands where he is Chief Digital & Technology Officer and President of Byte by Yum!.
“State Farm is committed to creating exceptional experiences for customers,” said State Farm President and Chief Executive Officer Jon Farney. “Joe brings a blend of customer focus, technical expertise and strong leadership that will help us deliver great service and competitive prices.”
Park will report to Farney and oversee State Farm’s technology, data and innovation functions, which include several thousand associates.
