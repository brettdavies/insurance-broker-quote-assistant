At a glance

Bankrate's take

Arrowhead is an insurance broker that provides specialty solutions to agents seeking coverage for clients, including those in the standard and nonstandard market. While the companys product line is extensive, personal auto and life are not currently available.

  • No longer offers personal auto or life insurance
  • Home insurance currently only offered in NJ
  • Commercial and specialty insurance available
  • Flood and earthquake insurance available for homeowners in certain states
  • Offers personal umbrella and wedding insurance

Arrowhead car insurance review

Founded in 1983, the Arrowhead General Insurance Agency began its focus on providing solutions for agents looking to insure hard-to-place risks. While the broker previously offered personal auto insurance through third-party underwriters, you cant currently get car insurance through Arrowhead. Our insurance editorial team has not assigned Arrowhead a Bankrate Score, as personal auto policies are no longer offered. Existing policies may still be in force in some states.

Pros and cons of Arrowhead car insurance

If youre gathering car insurance quotes, making a list of perks and drawbacks may be a helpful way to compare companies. However, Arrowhead car insurance isnt currently available for personal vehicles, so youll need to look elsewhere for coverage.

Arrowhead car insurance cost

Arrowhead does not currently offer car insurance policies, so average rates are not available. However, you can still get an idea of what your premium might be with other carriers by analyzing average car insurance rates. As of September 2025, the national average cost of car insurance is $2,671 per year for full coverage and $806 for state minimum coverage. Keep in mind that numerous factors make up your price, so your rate will be different based on your unique circumstances.

Arrowhead customer satisfaction

Arrowhead itself doesnt write insurance; its a general agency that facilitates the sale of specialty insurance products. As a result, its difficult to find third-party customer satisfaction ratings. Because it isnt an insurance carrier, Arrowhead doesnt have any third-party ratings from J.D. Power or the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC). It also lacks financial strength ratings from companies like AM Best.

Arrowhead car insurance discounts

Discounts may be a great way to save on car insurance and get a cheaper auto insurance policy. Because personal auto coverage is not currently available through Arrowhead, discount information is also not available.

Arrowhead home insurance review

Arrowhead has not consistently offered home insurance, so like its auto insurance product, the company does not have a Bankrate Score due to the limited data available. While the company previously offered home insurance in nine states, Arrows HO-3 coverage is currently only available in New Jersey and is underwritten by DB Insurance Company. Just like with its car insurance product, Arrowhead is not actually a home insurance company but rather a broker.

Pros and cons of Arrowhead homeowners insurance

Comparing home insurance quotes and other aspects of each carrier youre interested in may be an important step in finding the right option for you. However, Arrowheads home insurance is currently only available in one state New Jersey which means you wont be able to buy coverage if you live outside the state.

Arrowhead home insurance cost

As Arrowheads home insurance offerings and availability have changed over time, average rates for its home policies are unavailable from Quadrant Information Services. If you live in New Jersey where its home coverage is offered, you can request a quote and compare it to other companies and the national average cost of home insurance, which is $2,408 per year for $300,000 of dwelling coverage as of September 2025.

Generally, the more coverage you need, the higher your premium will be. Youll also pay more for added protections such as sewer backup coverage or service line coverage.

Arrowhead customer satisfaction

Because Arrowhead home insurance is currently widely unavailable for a majority of the U.S., its impossible to give an accurate estimate of the companys customer satisfaction based on third-party ratings. However, its home insurance is currently underwritten by DB Insurance Co., Ltd., which has an AM Best financial strength rating of A+ (Superior), a boost from its previous A (Excellent) rating.

Previously, coverage was underwritten by Universal North America Insurance Company. Universal North America has an AM Best rating of B+ (Good), but an outlook description of negative, indicating that its financial strength may be headed downhill. The NAIC gave Universal North America a complaint index of 3.13 for its homeowners insurance product in 2023. The baseline rating is 1.00, which means that the NAIC recorded three times more complaints than average for Universal North America's home insurance.

Arrowhead home insurance discounts

Neither Arrowhead nor Universal North America Insurance Company lists home insurance discounts online, possibly since new policies arent available right now. However, in general, discounts could be a good way to get cheaper home insurance, so it may be worthwhile to look into discounts when researching other companies.

Compare Arrowhead to other insurers

If youre shopping for a new car insurance carrier and home insurer (if you live outside of NJ), Arrowhead wont be an option right now. That doesnt mean youre out of luck, though. There are plenty of options out there for a variety of different insurance needs.

Arrowhead vs. Geico

If youre looking for plenty of car insurance discounts, Geico could be a good choice. The company offers lots of savings opportunities that may help you lower your premium. However, just like Arrowhead, Geico doesnt underwrite its own home insurance policies.

Learn more: Geico Insurance review

Arrowhead vs. SafeAuto

Arrowhead started by offering nonstandard auto insurance coverage to high-risk drivers, but since it doesnt currently write personal auto coverage, SafeAuto could be a good choice if you need insurance as a high-risk driver. Coverage is available in 20 states and SR-22 forms are available if you need one.

Learn more: SafeAuto Insurance review

Arrowhead vs. Erie

If Arrowheads former array of home insurance endorsements is appealing to you, you might want to check out Erie. The company is only available in 12 states and Washington, D.C., but it offers a long list of add-ons for robust home insurance coverage.

Learn more: Erie Insurance review

Is Arrowhead a good insurance company?

Arrowhead may be a good insurance company if youre looking for commercial or specialty insurance products, but it simply isnt an option for personal auto or home insurance for most customers at this time. If you are shopping for new insurance, you may want to get quotes from several carriers so you can compare rates, coverage offerings, discounts and third-party scores from different companies. Doing so might help you find the best company for your needs.

Because Arrowhead does not currently offer new personal auto policies anywhere or home insurance policies outside of New Jersey, its difficult to find reviews online from current customers.   • Customer support: Contact numbers vary by state and product
  • Address: 701 B. Street, Suite 2100, San Diego, CA 92101
  • Website: arrowheadgrp.com 

State availability

Arrowhead does not currently offer personal auto or life insurance in any states. Home insurance is currently available in New Jersey.

Arrowhead tools and benefits

Arrowhead provides solutions for numerous specialty products. These offerings include:

  • Personal umbrella: This is one of Arrowheads only personal products that is currently being offered. Umbrella policies provide additional liability coverage beyond the underlying limits of your auto, home and other policies.
  • Residential earthquake: Another personal product that is still available is Arrowheads earthquake coverage. This could help protect your finances in the aftermath of earthquake damage to your home.
  • Workers compensation: A common commercial insurance product, this policy helps cover the expenses of job-related injuries sustained by employees.
  • Commercial vehicle insurance: Arrowhead offers insurance for commercial vehicles, such as tow trucks and ambulances.

Various other business and specialty insurance products are also available. However, remember that Arrowhead isnt an insurance company, its a general agency. This means that your insurance wont be underwritten by Arrowhead itself. Your policy may come from one of several underwriting companies that Arrowhead uses, depending on the product you choose and the state you live in.

Frequently asked questions about Arrowhead insurance

  • Visit Arrowheads claims page for product-specific information on filing a claim. To contact the company for general inquiries, you can send mail to 701 B. Street Suite 2100, San Diego, CA 92101 or call 1-800-669-1889 or 619-881-8600.

  • Arrowhead may be a good insurance company if you have commercial or specialized insurance needs. However, it isnt an option at the moment if you need a personal auto or a home insurance policy outside of New Jersey it is not currently offering those product lines.

  • Arrowhead does not underwrite policies itself, so it does not have an AM Best score. Instead, it helps connect people with other insurance companies that underwrite certain types of policies. If you want to learn about the financial strength of your insurance, you can look into the AM Best score of the company underwriting the policy rather than the broker.

  • Arrowhead doesnt publish details about any corporate social responsibility or sustainability practices. If sustainability is important to you, consider researching the practices of the insurance companies that Arrowhead helps match you with.

  • Arrowhead General Insurance Agency doesnt have a Bankrate Score because Bankrates insurance editorial team only assigns Bankrate Scores to insurance carriers. Because Arrowheads insurance coverage is underwritten by multiple third parties, its impossible to give a universal evaluation of how well its underwriters meet customer needs.
