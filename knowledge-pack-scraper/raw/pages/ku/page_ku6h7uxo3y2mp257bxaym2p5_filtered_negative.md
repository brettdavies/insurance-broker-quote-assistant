GEICO offers two student discounts: the good student and student away at school discounts. The company awards students who maintain at least a B average a [good student discount](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/good-student-discount). You can prove this by submitting official documentation, like a transcript or report card. The discount typically expires when you reach age 23.[[1]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/geico-student-discount#cite-1)
![Smiling girl with curly hair driving a car, wearing a white shirt, looking forward through the windshield on a sunny day.](https://a.storyblok.com/f/295508/1200x675/7123dea4c5/teen-driver.jpg/m/1200x675/smart/filters:quality\(90\))
Few GEICO customers have reported their experience with the company’s student discounts, so it’s difficult to determine the average savings. But at least one student earned $40 off their monthly premium with a good student discount.[[2]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/geico-student-discount#cite-2) And a parent was able to reduce their costs by $600 annually with the student away at school discount.[[3]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/geico-student-discount#cite-3)
Combining good student and student away at school discounts can [save you the most money](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/resources/car-insurance-hacks).[[4]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/geico-student-discount#cite-4)
