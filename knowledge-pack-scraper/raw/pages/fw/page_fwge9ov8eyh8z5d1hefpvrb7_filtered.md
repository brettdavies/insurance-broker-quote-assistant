## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's Progressive's average payout for flood damage claims?

Based on recent data, Progressive pays an average of $15,000-$35,000 for flood damage claims, though amounts vary significantly based on damage extent. The 75% approval rate indicates most valid claims are covered. Your deductible (typically $1,000 - $2,500) will be subtracted from the payout.

### How quickly does Progressive process flood damage claims?

Progressive typically processes flood damage claims within 6-12 days. Emergency situations may receive expedited service within 24-48 hours. You can track your claim status through progressive.com or by calling 1-800-PROGRESSIVE.

### What documentation does Progressive require for flood damage?

Progressive requires: 1) Detailed photos/videos of damage, 2) Official reports (police, fire dept, etc.), 3) Receipts for damaged items, 4) Proof of ownership for high-value items, 5) Contractor estimates for repairs. Submit via the Progressive mobile app for fastest processing.

### Will my Progressive premium increase after a flood damage claim?

Premium increases vary by state and claim history. First-time flood damage claims typically result in 10-20% increases. Multiple claims within 3 years may lead to 25-40% increases or non-renewal. Progressive's Claim Rate Guard (if purchased) can protect against first claim increases.

### What should I do immediately after flood damage occurs?

1) Ensure safety and call 911 if needed, 2) Document everything with photos/video, 3) Prevent further damage if possible, 4) Call Progressive at 1-800-PROGRESSIVE, 5) Keep all receipts for emergency repairs, 6) Don't dispose of damaged items until adjuster approves.

### Does Progressive cover temporary living expenses during flood damage repairs?

Yes, if your home is uninhabitable due to covered flood damage, Progressive's Additional Living Expense (ALE) coverage typically pays for temporary housing, meals above normal cost, and other necessary expenses. Coverage limits are usually 20-30% of your dwelling coverage.
