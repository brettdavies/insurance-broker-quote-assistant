# Insurance Information Institute

Updated: 11 November 2024

The Insurance Information Institute (III) is an organization dedicated to helping the public understand [insurance](https://www.insuranceopedia.com/definition/1/insurance). It achieves this by educating people about what insurance is, how it works, and how individuals can benefit from it.

The IIIâ€™s educational initiatives cover a broad range of insurance types, including [auto insurance](https://www.insuranceopedia.com/definition/249/automobile-insurance), [homeowners insurance](https://www.insuranceopedia.com/definition/4976/homeowners-insurance), and [life insurance](https://www.insuranceopedia.com/definition/2655/life-insurance).

The Insurance Information Institute (III) is a valuable resource for anyone looking to learn more about insurance. Its website, iii.org, offers a wealth of information, including articles and references.

Each year, the III contributes to approximately 3,700 news stories related to insurance. It is widely recognized by the media, governments, universities, and the public as a credible and reputable source of insurance information.

To maintain its objectivity, the III refrains from engaging in lobbying activities.
