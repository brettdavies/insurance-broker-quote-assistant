# State Farm Seeks 39% Rate Hike for Umbrella Insurance in California
[Share Article to LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/sharing/share-offsite/?url=programbusiness.com/news/state-farm-seeks-39-rate-hike-for-umbrella-insurance-in-california/&t=TITLE)
State Farm General is pursuing a substantial 39% rate hike for its Personal Liability Umbrella policies in California, according to a filing with the California Department of Insurance.
Published on April 4, 2025
![State Farm](https://programbusiness.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/iStock-1411996937.jpg) Bloomington, Illinois, USA - March 26, 2022: State Farm corporate headquarters in Bloomington, Illinois, USA. State Farm Insurance is a large group of mutual insurance companies throughout the USA.
The [_Los Angeles Times_](https://www.latimes.com/business/story/2025-04-02/troubled-state-farm-seeking-yet-another-rate-hike) has reported that State Farm General is pursuing a substantial 39% rate hike for its Personal Liability Umbrella policies in California, according to a filing with the California Department of Insurance. The request follows a series of recent increases and comes amid significant financial pressures related to recent catastrophic wildfires.
