**Erie has the** [**cheapest SR-22 insurance**](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/cheapest-sr-22-insurance/#cheapest-sr22-companies) with minimum coverage starting at $133 monthly and full coverage at $252 monthly. GEICO and Progressive have the lowest cost nationwide rates starting at $144 and $153 monthly for minimum liability coverage.
**Travelers earns the** [**highest MoneyGeek score**](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/cheapest-sr-22-insurance/#best-cheap-sr-22-companies) of 96 for most drivers, combining quality coverage with affordable rates of $40 monthly for minimum coverage and $50 monthly for full coverage. **USAA** scores 97 but is only available to military members and their families.
[**SR-22 costs vary by violation type**](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/cheapest-sr-22-insurance/#average-cost-of-sr-22)**.** Drivers with hit-and-run violations pay the highest premiums at $188 monthly for minimum coverage and $371 monthly for full coverage. DUI and racing violations result in similar high-cost increases.
[Our SR-22 insurance ratings](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/cheapest-sr-22-insurance/#our-methodology) are based on 529,578 quotes from 67 insurers across all 50 states to find high-risk drivers the lowest rates for required coverage. We prioritize customer satisfaction (35%) and affordability (30%) to ensure you get quality service that balances your need for a low premium. This data-driven approach uses real-world driver profiles and stability metrics from J.D. Power, AM Best, and online review forums.
**Erie is the cheapest SR-22 insurance provider**, with [minimum coverage premiums](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/cheapest-sr-22-insurance/#cheapest-minimum-liability) averaging $114 monthly or $1,368 annually.
