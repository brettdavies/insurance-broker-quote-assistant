Progressive claim time limits can significantly impact whether you’re able to receive a settlement or not, making it crucial to start the insurance claims process as soon as possible. If you’ve recently been in a car accident, you should hire a [Chicago car insurance claim attorney](https://news.rosenfeldinjurylaw.com/updates/chicago-car-insurance-claim-lawyer/) who can guide you through the process and ensure that Progressive Insurance follows state laws.
![A Chicago car accident lawyer helping a client with a Progressive claim time limit case.](https://news.rosenfeldinjurylaw.com/static/2025/06/chicago-car-accident-lawyer-progressive-claim-time-limit.jpg)
![Attorney discussing car insurance claim options with an accident victim in Chicago.](https://news.rosenfeldinjurylaw.com/static/2025/06/insurance-company-attorney-chicago-car-accident-claim.jpg)
[If your Progressive claim is denied](https://news.rosenfeldinjurylaw.com/updates/chicago-car-insurance-claim-progressive-denied/), the company must explain the reasons for the denial in writing, whether they’re related to insurance policy coverage, insufficient evidence, or something else. You can either accept the settlement or dispute it with the help of a personal injury attorney.
