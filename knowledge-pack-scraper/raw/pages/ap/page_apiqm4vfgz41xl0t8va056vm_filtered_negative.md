Independent agents, on the other hand, represent multiple insurance companies and may earn higher commission rates since they don't have allegiance to a single company. This means their _commission rates can vary depending_ on the company and policy they sell.
---|---
The _commission rate can vary significantly depending_ on the type of insurance policy. Life insurance policies often offer higher commission rates than other types of policies.
Agents can also _earn bonuses and incentives based_ on their performance, the volume of policies sold, or when they _achieve specific milestones set by the insurance company_.
