3. Homeowners Insurance Explained
![Homeowners insurance protects your biggest investment](https://graystoneig.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Homeowners-Insurance-Explained_1000x563.jpg)
Your dwelling coverage should be high enough to completely rebuild your home in the event of a total loss. This requires estimating the home’s _replacement cost value_ (RCV), which includes current labor and material costs. Do not confuse this with market value, tax assessment value, or the remaining balance on your mortgage.
