## Average Cost of Home Insurance in California by Coverage Amount

In California, the average homeowners insurance premium is $1,148 for policies with $250K in dwelling coverage, $125K in personal property coverage and $200K in liability coverage, less than the [national average](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/average-cost-of-home-insurance/) of $2,423.

[Determine how much coverage you need](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/how-much-home-insurance-do-you-need/) to adequately rebuild your home and replace personal property in the event of a total loss. A policy with $100K in dwelling coverage costs an average of $602 annually, while a policy covering $500K in dwelling coverage costs an average of $2,081 annually. These premiums show the correlation between coverage amount and policy cost.

### Bundling Home Insurance With Auto Insurance in California

In California, homeowners save on insurance by [bundling their home and auto policies](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/best-home-auto-bundle-in-california/). On average, policyholders save $97 to $478 per year, about a 15% discount on the combined cost of both premiums.

### Home Insurance Discounts in California

California offers a variety of homeowners insurance discounts to help lower premiums.

* **Loyalty discount** for staying with the same insurer for several years.
* **Protective devices discount** for homes with security systems or smoke detectors.
* **Claims-free discount** for homeowners who haven't filed a claim in a certain period.

To maximize your savings, [ask about all available discounts](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/#how-to-save-with-discounts) when requesting a quote.

## FAQ: Homeowners Insurance in California

### On average, what is the cheapest insurer in California?

Allstate offers the lowest average premium at $727 per year. For more options, explore [cheap national providers](https:/www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/cheap-homeowners-insurance/).

### Are wildfires in California covered by homeowners insurance?

Wildfires, a common natural disaster in California, are [covered by home insurance](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/does-homeowners-insurance-cover-natural-disasters/).

### Is flood insurance included in standard homeowners insurance in California?

Flood insurance isn't included in a standard home policy but can be purchased through the National Flood Insurance Program, a private flood insurer or a home insurer that offers coverage as an endorsement.

All areas of California are prone to flooding, especially from atmospheric rivers in the winter months and Pacific tropical systems in the summer. California has one of the lowest flood insurance take-up rates in the U.S. (than 2% of homeowners have flood insurance).

### What is the biggest factor that impacts the price of homeowners insurance in California?

The insurance provider is the biggest factor, with premiums ranging from $727 to $1859.

### In California, how much, on average, can I save bundling my homeowners insurance with auto?

You can save an average of 9% per year in California when you [bundle your home and car insurance](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/best-home-auto-bundle-in-california/).
