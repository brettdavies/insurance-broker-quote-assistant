What is SR22 Insurance in Tennessee?

An SR22 is a certificate of financial responsibility required by the state for drivers who’ve had serious violations. It proves you carry the minimum liability coverage required by Tennessee law.

Affordable Solutions: Get the Cheapest Broadform or Non-Owner Car Insurance with Progressive in Tennessee

What is Non-Owner Car Insurance?

Non-owner car insurance provides liability coverage when you drive a vehicle you don’t own. It’s ideal for:

  • Drivers who rent or borrow cars frequently
  • Individuals needing SR22 insurance to reinstate a suspended license
  • People who want continuous coverage to avoid rate increases

What is Broadform Insurance?

Broadform insurance is a unique option that covers you as a driver, no matter what car you operate. It’s a budget-friendly solution for:

Meeting Tennessee’s minimum liability insurance requirements

One-person policies (no additional drivers)

Coverage across multiple vehicles (owned or borrowed)
