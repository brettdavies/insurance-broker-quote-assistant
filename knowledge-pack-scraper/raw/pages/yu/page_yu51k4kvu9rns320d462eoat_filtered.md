## New York Auto Insurance Requirements & What They Cover

New York law requires drivers to carry minimum auto insurance of 25/50/10 for liability. $25,000 per person, $50,000 per accident, and $10,000 for property damage. It also mandates $50,000 in no-fault (PIP) coverage to pay for medical expenses and lost wages, no matter who caused the accident.

PIP covers medical bills and lost wages after an accident, regardless of who is at fault. New York requires $50,000, but many drivers choose higher limits. The New York PIP statute mandates this coverage to ensure drivers are financially protected after an accident.
