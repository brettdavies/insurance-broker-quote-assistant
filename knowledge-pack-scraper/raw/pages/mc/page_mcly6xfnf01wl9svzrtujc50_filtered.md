To ensure [Texas Windstorm Insurance Association](/) and [Texas FAIR Plan Association](https://www.texasfairplan.org/) systems remain able to best serve policyholders and agents, we perform routine maintenance.

### **Systems Unavailable During Maintenance**

The following systems are unavailable for use during our scheduled maintenance:

* TWIA Policyholder Portal
* TWIA Agent Portal
* TFPA Policyholder Claim Center & ePayments
* TFPA Agent Portal
