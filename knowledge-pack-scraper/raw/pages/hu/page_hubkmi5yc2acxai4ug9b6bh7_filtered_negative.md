Progressive homeowners policies in Indiana cost an average of $135.84 per month or $1,630.12 per year in 2024.*Read the associated disclosure for this claim. Your cost depends on several factors, including the type of home and roof construction. Learn more about [how much homeowners insurance costs](https://www.progressive.com/answers/average-homeowners-insurance-cost/).
The amount of coverage you need can also impact your rate for homeowners insurance. Use our [homeowners insurance calculator](https://www.progressive.com/homeowners/insurance-coverages/calculator/%20) to help you estimate coverages and limits for your policy.
