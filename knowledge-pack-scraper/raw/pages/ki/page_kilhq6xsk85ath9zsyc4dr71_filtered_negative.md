GEICO is owned by Berkshire Hathaway, one of the five largest commercial auto insurers in the U.S.[[1]](https://insurify.com/business-insurance/commercial-auto/companies/geico/#cite-1) That standing reflects the company’s proven ability to pay for claims if your business truck gets sideswiped or your delivery van needs major repairs after an accident.
One of GEICO’s strengths is its DriveEasy Pro program, which uses technology to track employee driving and offers discounts to business owners for safe driving. Although this program isn’t available in every state, GEICO’s strong financial foundation makes it a company worth considering, especially if you’re shopping for [**cheap commercial insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/cheap-commercial-insurance/).
###![GEICO logo](https://insurifycdn.com/images/carrier/high-definition/geico.svg)GEICO
GEICO’s commercial auto insurance offers the same straightforward approach to vehicle insurance coverage as its [**personal auto policies**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/companies/geico/). You can buy liability, collision, comprehensive, and personal injury protection for fleets of any size. Commercial auto policies also have higher liability limits than standard personal auto insurance and include access to DriveEasy Pro to monitor driving behavior.
GEICO’s competitive rates, financial stability, and digital tools for fleet management make it one of the [**top commercial auto insurance companies**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/commercial-car-insurance/). It’s well suited for small and midsize companies prioritizing financial strength and practical features over white-glove service.
You can also access specialized coverage tailored to your [**business needs**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/commercial-insurance-small-business/). For example, freight haulers may need cargo insurance, businesses that rent or borrow vehicles can benefit from hired and non-owned vehicle coverage, and trucking companies using trailers they don’t own may require trailer interchange insurance.
Discounts can affect your business insurance costs significantly, especially when insuring multiple vehicles.[[2]](https://insurify.com/business-insurance/commercial-auto/companies/geico/#cite-2) GEICO offers several ways to trim your commercial auto premiums. While the company outlines a few discounts specifically for commercial policies, it may adapt other discounts from its personal auto offerings.
Remember that potential savings and eligibility requirements may vary depending on your state, vehicle types, and business operations. It’s best to call GEICO directly for personalized policy info and [**discount information**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/discounts/).

GEICO [**commercial auto coverage**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/do-i-need-commercial-car-insurance/) offers higher liability limits than typical personal auto insurance to protect businesses from greater risks.
Compared to industry standards, GEICO offers a wide range of core and specialized commercial coverages. The following coverages are available:[[4]](https://insurify.com/business-insurance/commercial-auto/companies/geico/#cite-4)
[**Liability insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/liability-insurance/) covers bodily injury (medical expenses, lost wages, legal fees) and property damage to vehicles, buildings, and other property. GEICO’s combined single-limit policy offers more flexibility.
[**Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/uninsured-motorist-coverage/) pays for injuries and property damage caused by drivers without insurance. It can also pay for damage after a hit-and-run.
[**Medical payments**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/medical-payments-coverage/) insurance helps cover medical expenses if your drivers or their passengers are injured in a company vehicle, regardless of who’s at fault.
[**Comprehensive insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/comprehensive-insurance/) pays for non-collision damage, such as from theft, vandalism, and fire. [**Collision insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/collision-insurance/) pays to repair or replace a vehicle after an accident.
GEICO’s emergency [**roadside assistance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/what-is-roadside-assistance/) provides emergency towing, tire changes, jump-starts, fuel delivery, and lockout assistance. Its expanded towing option lets business owners choose which repair shop to use, regardless of location.
A business owners policy (BOP) covers business equipment, inventory, and lost income if a disaster forces you to close temporarily.[[5]](https://insurify.com/business-insurance/commercial-auto/companies/geico/#cite-5) It also provides liability protection if someone is injured on your property. GEICO also offers workers’ compensation insurance to cover lost wages and medical expenses for injured employees.
