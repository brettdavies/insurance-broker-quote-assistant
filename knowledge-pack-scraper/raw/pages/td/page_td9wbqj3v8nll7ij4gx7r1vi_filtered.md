## How to find the best insurance for 50-year-olds

Your car insurance will probably be cheaper at age 50 than when you were younger, but there are still things you can do to get a lower rate.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/value-penguin/image/upload/w_70/icons/compare-2)

### Shop around

Car insurance companies set their rates differently, which means rates can differ by hundreds of dollars per month, depending on the company you choose. Shopping around and [comparing car insurance rates](/auto-insurance "Compare Car Insurance Rates") can help you find the lowest price for the coverage you need.

While you're comparing quotes, it's a good idea to compare customer service, too. Choosing a company with good service can make a big difference, especially if you have to [file a car insurance claim](/filing-auto-insurance-claim "A Guide To Filing An Auto Insurance Claim").

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/value-penguin/image/upload/w_70/icons/piggy-bank)

### Review your discounts

While you're shopping for a new car insurance policy, it's a good idea to look at the discounts a company offers. Even if you already have a policy that you're happy with, reviewing the discounts you're getting and the discounts your company offers can help you find additional savings.

The biggest discount is usually for [bundling your car and home](/best-home-car-insurance-bundle "Best Companies for Home and Auto Insurance Bundles"), condo or renters insurance together. You might also save by paying your full six-month or annual rate in full, going paperless and not having claims on your record.

## Frequently asked questions

### How much is car insurance for a 50-year-old per month?

On average, 50-year-olds pay $183 per month for full coverage car insurance and $133 per month for minimum coverage. Rates change depending on the car you drive, where you live, the coverage you need and more.

### What is the cheapest car insurance for people over 50?

State Farm tends to have the cheapest rates for most people age 50 and older. If you're a military member or a direct family member of someone in the military, you should get quotes from USAA, too. USAA often has cheap rates but it's not an option for most people.

### Does car insurance go down at 50 years old?

Car insurance rates stay fairly steady through adulthood. Your rate might go down a bit, especially if you haven't filed any claims or gotten any tickets. But you probably won't see a big drop in your rate for turning 50.

### What's the best car insurance for a 50-year-old?

State Farm is the best major insurance company for most 50-year-olds. It's usually cheap and you get to work with a local agent who can offer you personalized advice. But the best insurance for you depends on your personal situation. That's why it's important to shop around and compare companies.
