  * Get a free homeowners insurance quote

What does a standard homeowners insurance policy cover?
A standard homeowners insurance policy from Plymouth Rock keeps you well protected:
  * **Your Property** – We’ll protect your home and other structures from damage caused by fire, lightning, hail and other covered events.
  * **Your Belongings** – If damaged or stolen, we’ll arrange for them to be repaired or replaced.
  * **Your Liability** – We’ll protect you against certain claims for which you are responsible. For example, if someone is injured in your home on your property, you could be held legally responsible for the damages.


Can I add more protection to my homeowners insurance policy?
Absolutely! If you want the best possible protection for your home and belongings, we also offer several optional coverages. How much you add is up to you. For example, you can customize your home insurance by adding the following:
  * [Home Systems Breakdown](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/home-systems-breakdown)
  * [Cyber and Identity Protection](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/identity-theft-monitoring)
  * [Utility Service Line](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/utility-service-line)
  * Guaranteed Replacement Coverage
  * [Green Upgrades](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/green-upgrades)


If you have pets and want to add [pet insurance](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/pets), you can do that too. However, pet insurance must be purchased as a separate policy altogether.
Who needs homeowners insurance and why?
If you have a home, you definitely need homeowners insurance. There are two main reasons:
  * Mortgage lenders usually require proof that you have a certain amount of homeowners insurance before issuing a loan.
  * As unlikely as disasters are, they can and do happen. You want the best possible homeowners insurance for you and your family.
