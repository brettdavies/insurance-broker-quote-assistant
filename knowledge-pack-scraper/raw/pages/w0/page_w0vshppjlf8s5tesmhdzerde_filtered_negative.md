## Optional coverages in New York
If you want to maximize your protection, we also offer several optional coverage features. As a New York resident, you can customize your home insurance coverage by adding the following:
[Home systems breakdown](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/home-systems-breakdown) – Protects you if important equipment breaks down in your home, such as your furnace, stove or air conditioning unit.
[Flood insurance](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/flood) – Neptune Flood provides everything in FEMA’s National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP)—and more.
[Cyber & identity protection](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/identity-theft-monitoring) – Offered through CyberScout, our cyber and identity protection services keep you one step ahead of identity thieves.
[Utility service line](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/utility-service-line) – Provides payment if physical damage results in a leak, break, tear, rupture, collapse or arcing of a covered service line.
[Pet health insurance](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/pets) – Pet insurance lets you seek the best possible treatment for your pet’s unexpected injuries and illnesses.
[Green upgrades](https://www.plymouthrock.com/insurance/home/coverage-policy-options/green-upgrades) – Pays for the increased cost of repairing damaged property using materials and methods that are better for the environment.
