## Coverages & Policies
Openly specializes in homeowners insurance, offering flexible policy options for primary residences, secondary homes, and properties rented-to-others.
![Modern well-maintained home](https://openly.com/hs-fs/hubfs/ModernHome.jpeg?width=2000&name=ModernHome.jpeg)
### HO-5
### Primary & Secondary Homes
Whether it's a primary residence or a vacation home, Openly offers personalized coverage for your clients. Our policies include such superior coverage limits as:
  *     * Guaranteed replacement coverage up to $5M*
    * Broad liability coverage up to $1M
    * Blanket personal property coverage up to $100K per category


*In CT, GA, KS, MS, MO, NH, OH, SC, TN and WI, coverage amount is subject to Coverage A and conditions listed in the policy.
### HO-3
### Homes Rented-to-Others
Protect your clients' rental properties with our specialized landlord insurance. Unlike a typical landlord policy, our rented-to-others homeowners insurance policy is a modified HO-3, providing protection for: 
  *     * Property damage
    * Landlord’s personal property 
    * Liability claims
    * Loss of rental income due to a covered loss
