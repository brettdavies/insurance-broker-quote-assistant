CR shares personal information with third parties through the use of third party tracking technologies (or cookies) for online users and through sharing certain information about print members for marketing purposes. We do this to help support our nonprofit mission and do not accept third party advertising. But we understand that you may prefer that we not share your information in these ways. Here is how you can opt out of this type of data sharing:

### 

#### Functional and User Enhancement Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember you, your preferences and settings to help enhance your user experience. They cannot be switched off via this tool. If you do not allow these cookies, some features of our websites may not function properly.
  
  
You can learn more about managing cookies on popular browsers by clicking on any of these links: [Apple Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac), [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en), [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences), [Microsoft Edge](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-10-microsoft-edge-and-privacy), [Microsoft Internet Explorer](https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies)
  
  
Follow these directions to [Opt Out of Facebook Commenting](https://www.facebook.com/help/211829542181913)

#### Strictly Necessary/ Essential Cookies

These cookies are essential for our digital services to function correctly and cannot be switched off via this tool. They include cookies necessary to enable members to log in to CR.org, access account information, and use certain features and services available to CR members.
  
  
You can learn more about managing cookies on popular browsers by clicking on any of these links [Apple Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac), [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en), [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences), [Microsoft Edge](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-10-microsoft-edge-and-privacy), [Microsoft Internet Explorer](https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies). If you set your browser to block these cookies some part of our CR.org website may not be accessible to you.

#### Share My Personal Information: Digital

Share My Personal Information: Digital

We use online technologies (or cookies) to track the pages you visit on the Consumer Reports website and serve you offers of Consumer Reports products and services after you leave our website. This is known as “targeted advertising” or “cross-context behavioral advertising”. We do this to support our mission and do not accept third party advertising. If you prefer not to receive these targeted offers, you can turn off these cookies by sliding the toggle button to "Off" and clicking the “Confirm My Choice” button below. Once you have confirmed your selection, you will no longer receive these targeted offers, but you may still receive other Consumer Reports offers. You can also use this toggle to turn off the tracking of certain types of affiliate offers and analytics (please see our Cookie Policy in the link below). Please note that users who are not logged in to their CR account will need to renew this choice each time you clear your cookies or use a new browser. Users will need to renew this choice when you use a new device.  
  

#### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for our digital services to function correctly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They include cookies necessary for members to access their accounts and use certain site features. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of the site will not work. To learn more and to manage your cookies, please visit our Cookie Policy [**here**.](/customerservice/privacy-policy/cookies/) You can also visit our Privacy Policy located  [**here.**](/customerservice/privacy-policy/highlights)   
  

#### Share My Personal Information for Direct Mail: Print

  
  
  
If you are a Print or All-Access Member and receive Consumer Reports magazine or Consumer Reports on Health through the mail, we may share your name and mailing address for direct mail purposes with selected companies offering products or services that we believe will be of interest to you. We do this to improve our customer list and help support our mission. If you prefer not to receive these offers, please click the Opt Out link  [**here**.](https://subs.consumerreportsmagazine.org/servlet/CSGateway?cds_mag_code=CNS&cds_page_id=254584) Please note that you will need to return to the Consumer Reports website to set your online preferences.
