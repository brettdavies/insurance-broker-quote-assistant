    We use online technologies (or cookies) to track the pages you visit on the Consumer Reports website and serve you offers of Consumer Reports products and services after you leave our website. This is known as “targeted advertising” or “cross-context behavioral advertising”. We do this to support our mission and do not accept third party advertising. If you prefer not to receive these targeted offers, you can turn off these cookies by sliding the toggle button to "Off" and clicking the “Confirm My Choice” button below. Once you have confirmed your selection, you will no longer receive these targeted offers, but you may still receive other Consumer Reports offers. You can also use this toggle to turn off the tracking of certain types of affiliate offers and analytics (please see our Cookie Policy in the link below). Please note that users who are not logged in to their CR account will need to renew this choice each time you clear your cookies or use a new browser. Users will need to renew this choice when you use a new device.

    Strictly Necessary Cookies

    These cookies are essential for our digital services to function correctly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They include cookies necessary for members to access their accounts and use certain site features. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of the site will not work. To learn more and to manage your cookies, please visit our Cookie Policy here. You can also visit our Privacy Policy located here.

    If you are a Print or All-Access Member and receive Consumer Reports magazine or Consumer Reports on Health through the mail, we may share your name and mailing address for direct mail purposes with selected companies offering products or services that we believe will be of interest to you.
