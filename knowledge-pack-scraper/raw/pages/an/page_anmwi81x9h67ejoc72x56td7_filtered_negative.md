##  Average Auto Insurance Expenditure in Nevada by Age Group
It’s a fact of life: [as you age](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/driver/age/), your auto insurance prices shift. As a general rule, car insurance is more costly for [teenage drivers](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/driver/age/cheap-car-insurance-teens/), as insurers view inexperienced motorists as riskier.
In Nevada, auto insurance costs $8,947 annually for a [16-year-old motorist](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/driver/age/car-insurance-16-year-olds/), versus $1,388 annually for a client in his or her [50s](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/discounts/senior-citizen-discount/).

##  Average Car Insurance Rates in Nevada by Marital Status

##  Average Car Insurance Costs by Coverage in Nevada
The amount you pay every month for auto insurance depends on how much [coverage](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/coverage/) you purchase. Liability-only coverage usually costs less, while low-deductible comprehensive coverage costs more. In Nevada, the price gap between state-minimum liability-only coverage and comprehensive coverage with a $500 deductible is $598.
