Yes, GEICO offers several mature driver discounts for drivers who are over 50 years old. Discounts are available for defensive driving course completion, multiple car insurance policies, retired government employees, and more.

## GEICO Auto Insurance Discounts Available for Drivers Over 50

* **Defensive driving discount:** Completing a defensive driving course could qualify you for a discount. Eligibility rules and requirements vary by state.
* **Five-year good driving discount:** If you’ve been accident free for 5 years, you may be able to save up to 22% on most coverages.
* **Retired government & military discount:** You may be eligible for a discount of up to 15% on certain car insurance coverages.
* **Multi-car discount:** Insure more than one car and you could get a discount of up to 25% on most of your car insurance coverages.
* **Membership & employee discount:** You may be eligible for a special auto insurance membership discount if you’re a member of a [partner organization](https://www.geico.com/save/discounts/organization-member/).

Plus, GEICO has a guaranteed renewal program for policyholders who are at least 50 years old. The Prime Time contract is available in 30 states and can be applied automatically when you first buy a policy with GEICO, or at the time of renewal. Certain restrictions and eligibility requirements may apply.
