1. **How does the Mental Health Services Tax affect high-income earners in California?****
**An additional 1% tax applies to taxable income over $1 million under the Mental Health Services Tax.
2. **Are there any recent changes to California's tax brackets for 2025?****
**As of now, California's tax brackets remain similar to previous years, with annual adjustments for inflation.
3. **How can I reduce my taxable income in California?****
**You can lower taxable income through deductions, tax-advantaged accounts (401(k), IRA), charitable contributions, and tax credits like the CalEITC.
4. **When are California state income tax returns due for the 2025 tax year?****
**The deadline for filing California state tax returns is**April 15, 2026**, unless extended.
