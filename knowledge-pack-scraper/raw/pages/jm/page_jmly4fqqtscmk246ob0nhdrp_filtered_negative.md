###  Texas home insurance and flood damage coverage
* **A standard home insurance policy usually won’t cover flood damage.** You need to buy a separate [flood insurance](https://www.lendingtree.com/home-insurance/best-flood-insurance/) policy for that.
* If you have flood insurance, **contact your insurance company or agent as soon as you can** after flooding damages your home.
* Be patient after you file your flood claim. **It can take four to eight weeks for your insurer to finalize and pay your claim.**
* If you don’t have flood insurance, **consider buying it even if you don’t live in a high-risk flood zone**. Most of Texas’ major cities have a high flood risk.
