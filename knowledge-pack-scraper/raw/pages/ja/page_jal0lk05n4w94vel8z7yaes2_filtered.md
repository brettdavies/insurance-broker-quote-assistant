## What Does Progressive Homeowners Insurance Cover?

Progressive provides all [six standard home insurance coverages](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/what-does-homeowners-insurance-cover/) to financially protect your home and assets.

![house icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/house_f1rmru.svg)

**Dwelling Coverage**

[Dwelling coverage](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/what-is-dwelling-coverage/) pays for repairs or rebuilding when your home's structure suffers damage from covered perils like fire, wind or hail. This includes your walls, the floor, the ceiling, built-in fixtures and more.

![garage icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/garage_ttngpd.svg)

**Other Structures Coverage**

[Other structures coverage](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/does-homeowners-insurance-cover-other-structures/) handles damage to detached garages, sheds, fences and other structures on your property. It also covers gazebos, pool houses and detached guest houses when damaged by covered events.

![bookshelves icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/bookshelves_nsuojr.svg)

**Personal Property Coverage**

Your [personal property insurance](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/what-is-personal-property-coverage-how-much-do-you-need/) covers items like clothing, gadgets, and furniture that are lost, stolen or destroyed. Usually, coverage is applicable whether the damage occurs while traveling or at home.

![scale icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/scale_dhas7a.svg)

**Personal Liability Coverage**

If you're found responsible for injuries to others or damage to their property, [personal liability coverage](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/personal-liability-homeowners-insurance/) will pay for legal fees and damages. For example, if a guest falls on your stairs or your dog bites a neighbor, you'd be covered.

![hospital icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/hospital_l5afnp.svg)

**Medical Payments Coverage**

Coverage for medical payments addresses small medical costs incurred by guests injured on your premises, regardless of who is at fault. It includes immediate expenses such as ambulance transportation and emergency room visits, up to the limits set by your policy.

![hotel icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/hotel_tab8pv.svg)

**Loss of Use Coverage**

If your home gets damaged and becomes unlivable, [loss of use coverage](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/homeowners-insurance-loss-of-use/) helps out by covering costs like hotel stays, meals at restaurants, and storage fees. It also takes care of extra transportation expenses and any additional living costs that go beyond what you usually spend while the repairs are being done.

### Optional Home Insurance Coverages from Progressive

Progressive offers two endorsements to customize your policy. Endorsements are optional add-ons that expand your standard policy to cover specific risks not included in basic homeowners insurance.

Consider the options below to enhance your protection with Progressive. Note that availability varies by state, so ask your agent if there are other coverages available to you.

| Coverage | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Water Back-up and Sump Discharge or Overflow | Covers damage when water backs up, overflows or discharges from sewers, drains, sump pumps or related equipment, including water removal and property damage from basement flooding |
| Personal Injury | Covers legal fees and damages from lawsuits involving slander, libel, malicious prosecution, wrongful eviction, wrongful entry, false arrest, detention or imprisonment |

### What Does Progressive’s Home Insurance Not Cover?

Progressive's home insurance policies don't cover certain natural disasters or intentional damage. Understand what's excluded from a standard Progressive home insurance policy below:

![flood icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/flood_kua1hg.svg)

**Earthquakes and Floods**

Floods, earthquakes, landslides and sinkholes require separate policies or specific endorsements for coverage.

![injury icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/injury_ykcx1b.svg)

**Intentional or Negligent Damage**

Damage you cause deliberately or damage from lack of maintenance, wear and tear, pest infestations and mold growth aren't covered.

![hammer icon](https://res.cloudinary.com/moneygeek/image/upload/v1585171420/assets/components/icon/hammer_yk2psa.svg)

**War & Nuclear Hazards**

Military actions, war, nuclear hazards and government seizure of property fall outside standard coverage.

## How to Buy Progressive’s Homeowners Insurance

Progressive sells homeowners insurance online and through independent agents who can help you [buy home insurance](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/how-to-buy-homeowners-insurance/) tailored to your situation. Follow the five steps below to secure your policy.

1. 1

   Determine How Much Coverage You Need

   Calculate [how much home insurance you need](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/how-much-home-insurance-do-you-need/) by assessing your dwelling's replacement cost and creating a [home inventory](https://www.moneygeek.com/living/home/how-to-make-a-home-inventory/) of your belongings. Review your assets to set appropriate coverage limits for dwelling, personal property and liability protection.
2. 2

   Get a Quote Online or Through an Agent

   Visit Progressive's website or contact an independent agent to receive your personalized quote. Be ready to provide accurate information about your home's features, location and your insurance history for the most precise pricing.
3. 3

   Compare Policy Options

   Review your chosen coverage levels, deductibles and optional endorsements to build appropriate protection. Consider adding water back-up or personal injury coverage based on your specific risks and budget.
4. 4

   Apply for Discounts

   Ask about all eight available discounts including bundling, safety features and new home construction savings. Maximize your savings by combining multiple discounts where eligible.
5. 5

   Review and Finalize Your Policy

   Carefully read your policy documents to understand coverage limits, exclusions and your deductible. Confirm all information is accurate before making your first payment to activate coverage.

### How to File a Homeowners Insurance Claim with Progressive

Progressive accepts claims through phone calls to their claims line or online submissions via their website. Complete these four steps to [process your home insurance claim](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/how-does-a-homeowners-insurance-claim-work/) with Progressive.

1. 1

   Report the Claim

   Call Progressive immediately or submit your claim through their website to start the process. Provide details about the damage, when it occurred and any immediate repairs needed to prevent further loss.
2. 2

   Document the Damage

   Take photos of all damaged areas and create an inventory of affected belongings with receipts when available. Progressive may request this documentation or schedule an adjuster inspection to assess your loss.
3. 3

   Schedule an Inspection

   Work with Progressive's adjuster to review the damage estimate and understand your deductible responsibilities. The adjuster explains repair costs and what portion your policy covers after your out-of-pocket expenses.
4. 4

   Review the Settlement and Repair Process

   Progressive will send the payment for the covered amount so you can hire any contractor for repairs. If workers discover additional damage during repairs not included in the original estimate, Progressive may issue supplemental payments.

### Progressive Home Insurance: FAQ

These frequently asked questions provide quick answers to help you understand Progressive's home insurance..

### How much does Progressive homeowners insurance cost?

Progressive charges an average of $5,445 annually for $250,000 in dwelling coverage based on MoneyGeek's sample profile. Your actual rate depends on factors including your location, credit score, claims history and selected coverage limits. Premiums range from $3,461 yearly for basic coverage to $13,060 for policies with $1 million in dwelling protection.

### What discounts does Progressive offer for homeowners insurance?

Progressive provides eight discount opportunities including bundling home and auto policies, safety and alarm system installations, and paying your premium in full.

### How do I file a claim with Progressive?

Report claims immediately by calling Progressive or using their website to start the process online. Document all damage with photos, provide receipts for damaged items if available, and work with the assigned adjuster to complete your estimate. Progressive sends payment for covered damages so you can hire contractors to complete repairs.
