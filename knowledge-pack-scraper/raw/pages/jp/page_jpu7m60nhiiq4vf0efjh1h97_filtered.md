## What Information Do You Need When You Call?

To ensure a smooth and efficient quoting process when you call the **Progressive Insurance Phone Number for Home Quotes**, it’s beneficial to have certain information readily available. ### About Your Home: Key Property Details

When speaking with a Progressive agent, you’ll be asked a series of questions about the property you wish to insure. These details are critical for assessing risk and determining your premium. Having this information at your fingertips will streamline the conversation.

* **Property Address:** The full address of the home, including zip code.
* **Year Built:** The construction year of the house.
* **Type of Home:** Single-family, multi-family, condo, townhouse, etc.
* **Square Footage:** The heated living area of the home.
* **Construction Materials:** What your home is primarily built from (e.g., brick, wood frame, stucco).
* **Roof Type and Age:** The material of your roof (shingle, tile, metal) and its approximate age. This is a significant factor in premiums.
* **Specific Features:** Details about any unique features like a swimming pool, detached structures (garage, shed), fireplaces, wood-burning stoves, or security systems.

### About Your Household: Occupants and History

Beyond the structural aspects of your home, insurers also consider information related to the occupants and the property’s claims history. This helps them understand the overall risk profile.

* **Number of Occupants:** How many people reside in the home.
* **Prior Claims History:** Any past home insurance claims filed at this property or by you at a previous residence. Be prepared with dates and details.
* **Pets:** Information about any dogs, as certain breeds may impact liability coverage.
* **Smoking Status:** Whether there are smokers residing in the home.

#### Essential Home Details for Progressive Home Insurance Quotes

Delving deeper into your home’s specifics ensures the most precise quote. Agents will often inquire about the age of major systems and safety features.

* **Heating System:** Type of heating (electric, gas, oil) and its age.
* **Plumbing System:** Type of pipes (copper, PEX, galvanized) and age.
* **Electrical System:** Amperage of the electrical panel and wiring type.
* **Security Features:** Presence of smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, fire extinguishers, alarm systems, or sprinkler systems. These can often lead to discounts.

#### Personal Information for Accuracy

Finally, you’ll need to provide some personal information to verify your identity and help Progressive access relevant records.

* **Your Name and Date of Birth:** For identification purposes.
* **Social Security Number:** Often required for credit-based insurance scores, which impact premiums.
* **Current Insurance Provider:** If applicable, knowing your current insurer helps with comparisons and potential discounts.
* **Desired Coverage Start Date:** When you need the policy to become active.
