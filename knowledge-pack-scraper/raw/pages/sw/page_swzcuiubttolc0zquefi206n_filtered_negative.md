# Uber Driver Requirements
* [Rideshare Accident](https://accidentnetwork.com/category/rideshare-accident/)
[We are here to help, schedule a free consultation now](https://accidentnetwork.com/uber-driver-requirements/#text-2)
Being aware of driver prerequisites also aids in handling situations like accidents, where legal support becomes important. Those involved in such incidents may seek assistance from experienced [rideshare accident attorneys](https://accidentnetwork.com/rideshare-accidents/) to address injury claims or insurance matters. This knowledge helps new drivers prepare fully before entering the ridesharing field.
Drivers in California must meet certain criteria to provide rides on Uber’s platform. They need to be [at least 21 years old](https://accidentnetwork.com/latest-car-age-limits-for-lyft-and-uber-updated-requirements/) and have a minimum of one year of driving experience if they are over 25; younger drivers may face additional rules. A valid U.S. driver’s license is mandatory.

Uber insists on a four-door car in good condition with [up-to-date registration and insurance](https://accidentnetwork.com/uber-and-lyft-driver-requirements/#:~:text=Automobile%20Insurance%20Standards%20for%20Uber%20and%20Lyft). Some companies may demand newer models or additional inspections.
