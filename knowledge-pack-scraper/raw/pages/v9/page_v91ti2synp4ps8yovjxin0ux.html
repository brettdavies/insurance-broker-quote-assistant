Commercial Insurance Protects You From False Advertising Lawsuits

Posted on March 28, 2025March 27, 2025 by admin

Sometimes, a person may feel that your business didn’t deliver on a promise you made through an advertisement, and they initiate a lawsuit for false advertising. You then have to defend yourself against the claim with the help of a lawyer. If you have a commercial insurance policy, your insurer will provide one if you have a valid defense. At Amerisent Insurance in Las Vegas, NV, our goal is to ensure our business customers have the right type of insurance to cover themselves in the event of a lawsuit.

How Commercial Insurance Defends Your Business Against a False Advertising Lawsuit

Getting sued for false advertising initiates a legal process that puts you on the defensive. When someone takes legal action against your business, they have to serve you with a notice. Once you receive the notice, contact your commercial insurance company and tell them you need assistance.

The insurer assigns you a lawyer who can handle the plaintiff’s claim that you willingly put out a false advertisement for a product or service. Once the lawyer takes over the case, you can continue operating your business knowing that the lawsuit is in capable hands.

When Commercial Insurance Won’t Cover Your Lawsuit

If you knowingly and willingly engaged in false advertising, the insurance company won’t cover you in the event of a lawsuit. The insurer considers this a deliberate act, and you’ll have to retain an attorney on your own.

Our team is here to help you learn more about how commercial insurance provides you with a legal defense, and what happens when you make a claim. Get in touch with us today to learn more.
