If you have a bad driving record, you may have to spend more time shopping around to find affordable car insurance. Some insurance companies give bad drivers very high rates, so [compare online quotes](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance-quotes "Get Free, Cheap Car Insurance Quotes Online") from multiple companies to find the best deal for you.
* * *
If your bad driving record means you need a form to show the state you have car insurance, called an [SR-22](https://www.valuepenguin.com/sr-22-auto-insurance "SR-22 Insurance") or sometimes an FR-44, you may not be able to get insurance from some major companies.
Instead, you may have to consider [nonstandard auto insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance/nonstandard-auto-insurance "Non-Standard Auto Insurance") companies that specialize in bad driver car insurance. Two good nonstandard car insurance companies are [The General](https://www.valuepenguin.com/the-general-insurance-review "The General Insurance Review") and [SafeAuto](https://www.valuepenguin.com/safe-auto-insurance-review "SafeAuto Insurance Review").
* * *
Try to [find as many discounts as possible](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance-discounts "Car Insurance Discounts") to make insurance more affordable. Many companies offer discounts that are easy to get. You can usually save by buying a policy online, setting up automatic payments, bundling multiple insurance policies or paying in full.
You can also take a [defensive driving course](https://www.valuepenguin.com/defensive-driver-training-car-insurance-discount "Defensive Driver Courses: What's Covered, and How to Get an Insurance Discount") to save around 10% to 15%.
* * *
If your poor driving record is because of bad luck or you're committed to driving safer, you could lower your rates by as much as 30% or 40% by letting your insurance track your driving habits. This is called a [usage-based discount program](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-usage-based-car-insurance "Best Usage-Based Car Insurance Companies").
The [best usage-based car insurance programs](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-usage-based-car-insurance "Best Usage-Based Car Insurance Companies"), like State Farm Drive Safe & Save, won't raise your rates for bad driving habits.
