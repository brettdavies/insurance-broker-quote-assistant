    Drive Safe & Save® Business

    With Drive Safe & Save Business, you can help your team focus on driving safely with driving insights, detailed trip maps and location awareness.

    • Drive Safe & Save Business
    • Getting started with DSSB
    • DSSB FAQ
    Looks like we’re missing some information. Select your state and try again.

    What are the benefits of Drive Safe & Save Business?

    • You’ll receive a one-time, participation premium decrease for each eligible vehicle you enroll and pair with a Bluetooth beacon.
    • At policy renewal, the premium adjustment is based on driving behaviors and may increase, decrease or remain the same.
    • Plus, the Drive Safe & Save Business portal lets you monitor your business vehicles with near real-time locations, detailed trip maps and driver feedback.

    What is the Drive Safe & Save Business portal?

    The Drive Safe & Save Business portal gives you an inside look into your employee driving behaviors. Use the insights you gain from the portal to coach your team and help them build safer driving habits.

    The Drive Safe & Save Business portal lets you:

    • Get near real-time vehicle location updates
    • See trip details and history
    • Monitor route efficiency
    • Review driver scores, trends and streaks
    • Share leaderboard rankings with your team for friendly competition

    How do I get started with Drive Safe & Save Business?

    Contact your State Farm® agent to get started with Drive Safe & Save Business.

    Here’s how the enrollment process works:

    Step 1

    Once you sign up through your agent, you’ll get an SMS text message with a link to download the Drive Safe & Save Business app.

    Step 2

    After you successfully log in to the app and give consent, we’ll send you a Bluetooth beacon for each vehicle you’re enrolling.

     

    Step 3

    Follow the in-app prompts to pair a Bluetooth beacon with each vehicle listed in your app. 

    Step 4

    The app also walks you through inviting other drivers to get started with the app.

     

    How is Drive Safe & Save Business different from Drive Safe & Save®?

    Drive Safe & Save Business

    This program is for small-to medium-sized commercial-type vehicles, like cargo vans, box trucks or flatbed trucks, that are used for business operations.

    Drive Safe & Save

    This program is for personal and private passenger-type vehicles, even some used for business purposes, like a real estate agent’s car.

    Comparing Drive Safe & Save Business and Drive Safe & Save

    Drive Safe & Save Business Drive Safe & Save
    Vehicle eligibility Eligibility is based on each vehicle’s gross vehicle weight, body type and how the vehicle is used during business operations. Private passenger vehicles are eligible, except motorcycles, golf carts and antique/classic vehicles.

    Premium adjustments

    You’ll receive a one-time, participation premium decrease for each vehicle you enroll and pair with a Bluetooth beacon.

    At policy renewal, the premium adjustment is based on driving behaviors and may increase, decrease, or remain the same.

    You’ll receive a 10% initial participation discountfootnote 1 when you enroll.

    At renewal, a discount of up to 30%footnote 2 is based on driving behaviors.

    Leaving Drive Safe & Save If participation is voluntarily discontinued, a post program premium adjustment will remain for 12 months following the first renewal after discontinuation. When you remove Drive Safe & Save, the discount is removed right away.
