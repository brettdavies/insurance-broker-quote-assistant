### Auto insurance for I-4 and tourist traffic

Progressive’s breadth of auto endorsements and competitive pricing can suit commuters who deal with stop-and-go traffic and frequent visitors on I-4 and around attractions. Look at options like accident forgiveness tiers, rideshare coverage, and roadside assistance. Telematics programs can reward smooth, low-distraction driving—use a phone mount and leave more following distance to reduce hard-brake events.

### Homeowners in a storm-aware market

Central Florida’s wind/hail and water exposure means coverage details matter more than headline premium. Progressive-placed homeowners policies may offer water backup, ordinance or law, and equipment breakdown add-ons. Ask us to model hurricane/wind deductibles (percentage vs flat), replacement cost options, and roof age documentation—photos and invoices can unlock credits and faster underwriting.

### Renters & condo owners near downtown and UCF

Renters can bundle with auto for big savings, while condo owners should verify the HOA master policy (walls-in vs walls-out) and add loss-assessment coverage. For high-rise or short-term rental buildings, we’ll review liability language, personal property sub-limits (jewelry, cameras, bicycles), and whether you need scheduled property.

### Toys & lifestyle: motorcycles, boats, RVs

Progressive is often strong on specialty lines. For weekends on the Butler Chain or coastal trips, consider boat liability limits that mirror your auto/umbrella strategy and review uninsured boater coverage. For motorcycles and RVs, we’ll cover total-loss settlement options, custom parts/equipment limits, and roadside/coach-towing specifics.
