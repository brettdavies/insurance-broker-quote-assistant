## What actually changes your price in Orlando
Compare apples-to-apples: match limits, deductibles, and key endorsements before deciding which quote is truly cheaper for you.
Factor | How it moves your rate | Pro tip
---|---|---
Location & storm exposure | Wind/hail, flood zones, and theft rates influence base premiums | Ask about wind/hurricane deductibles and mitigation credits (roof shape, shutters).
Roof age & home updates | Older roofs/plumbing/electrical price higher | Provide photos and invoices; consider secondary water damage protections.
Driver & vehicle profile | Violations, mileage, garaging, and repair costs affect auto pricing | Enroll in telematics only if your habits will score well; use a phone mount.
Coverage & deductibles | Higher limits cost more; higher deductibles reduce premium | Pair a reasonable deductible with an umbrella for cost-effective liability.
Bundle & payment | Multi-policy and paid-in-full/EFT can lower cost | Price monthly vs paid-in-full to see true net savings.
Claims & credit | Recent losses and credit-based scores (where allowed) affect tiers | Have us model “file vs self-pay” for small losses to avoid surcharges.

## Frequently asked questions
Is Progressive the cheapest in Orlando?
Sometimes—pricing depends on your ZIP, drivers, vehicles, roof age (for home), and discount eligibility. The only reliable answer is a matched comparison with identical limits and endorsements.
Should I use a telematics program?
If you drive mostly daytime miles, avoid phone handling, and brake smoothly, telematics can help. If you work nights or face heavy congestion, compare both telematics and non-telematics pricing before you commit.
Can I bundle auto and home with Progressive?
Yes, bundling can reduce overall cost, but we still validate coverage details like wind deductibles, water backup, and ordinance or law so you don’t trade away critical protection for a small savings.
What documents speed up quoting?
Driver license numbers, VINs, current declarations pages, and home update invoices/photos. For condos, include your HOA master policy summary; for boats or motorcycles, list accessories and values.
Can I switch mid-term?
Yes—just coordinate effective dates to avoid gaps and handle mortgagee/lienholder updates. We’ll time cancellations and new binders so proof of insurance is seamless.
