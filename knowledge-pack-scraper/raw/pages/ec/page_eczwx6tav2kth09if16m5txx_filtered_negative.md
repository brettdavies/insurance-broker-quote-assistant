Drive Safe & Save Business |  Drive Safe & Save
---|---|---
Vehicle eligibility |  Eligibility is based on each vehicle’s gross vehicle weight, body type and how the vehicle is used during business operations. |  Private passenger vehicles are eligible, except motorcycles, golf carts and antique/classic vehicles.
Required smartphone app |  Drive Safe & Save Business app |  State Farm app
Viewing driving feedback, trip maps and location awareness |  Driving feedback, trip maps, and location awareness are displayed in the app and the Drive Safe & Save Business portal.
The Drive Safe & Save Business portal also provides near real-time vehicle locations. |  Driving feedback, trip maps, and location awareness are displayed in the app.
There is no online portal.
Setup requirements |  You have 60 days to complete setup.
Once you download the app, log in and give consent, we’ll ship a Bluetooth beacon for each enrolled vehicle.
Employee drivers also need to download the app and log in.
A beacon needs to be paired with each enrolled vehicle. |  You have 30 days to complete setup.
A named insured downloads the app, logs in, and tells us where to send beacons.
When beacons arrive, they need to be paired with each enrolled vehicle.
Premium adjustments |  You’ll receive a one-time, participation premium decrease for each vehicle you enroll and pair with a Bluetooth beacon.
At policy renewal, the premium adjustment is based on driving behaviors and may increase, decrease, or remain the same. |  You’ll receive a 10% initial participation discount[footnote 1](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/small-business/commercial-auto/business-drive-safe-and-save#footnote-1) when you enroll.
At renewal, a discount of up to 30%[footnote 2](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/small-business/commercial-auto/business-drive-safe-and-save#footnote-2) is based on driving behaviors.
Leaving Drive Safe & Save |  If participation is voluntarily discontinued, a post program premium adjustment will remain for 12 months following the first renewal after discontinuation. |  When you remove Drive Safe & Save, the discount is removed right away.
