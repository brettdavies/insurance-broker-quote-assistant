## Why compare against big-brand quotes

Home insurance is priced uniquely for each property and household. Roof age, construction updates, claims history, local weather, and fire protection class can shift which insurer is best value *for you*. Independent comparison reveals when alternatives beat a renewals-only strategy.

## What a standard home policy covers

### Core coverages (typical HO-3/HO-5)

* **Coverage A — Dwelling:** Rebuilds the structure after a covered loss.
* **Coverage B — Other Structures:** Fences, sheds, detached garage.
* **Coverage C — Personal Property:** Belongings at home and often worldwide.
* **Coverage D — Loss of Use:** Extra living expenses while you’re displaced.
* **Coverage E — Personal Liability:** Injury/property damage to others.
* **Coverage F — Medical Payments:** Limited no-fault guest medical.

### Deductibles & settlement

* **All-Peril vs. Wind/Hail/Hurricane:** Some markets use separate % deductibles.
* **RC vs. ACV:** Replacement Cost pays new-for-old (subject to limits); ACV subtracts depreciation—crucial for roofs and contents.
* **Sub-limits:** Jewelry, art, firearms, collectibles, cash—consider scheduling.

## Popular endorsements & add-ons

* **Water Backup/Sump Overflow:** Drain/sewer backup (capped).
* **Service Line:** Buried utility lines (street to home).
* **Equipment Breakdown:** Mechanical/electrical failure of systems/appliances.
* **Ordinance or Law:** Brings rebuilds up to current code.
* **Extended/Guaranteed RC:** Cushion if rebuild costs surge (where offered).

* **RC on Personal Property:** Avoids depreciation on contents.
* **Scheduled Items:** Jewelry, watches, art, collectibles—broader causes of loss.
* **Identity Theft/Cyber:** Response services and limited coverage.
* **Loss Assessment (Condos):** HOA assessments after covered events.
* **Home-Based Business/Rental:** Side-hustle or STR exposures.

## What drives price (and ways to save)

### Rating factors

* **Roof age/material:** Newer & impact-resistant rate better.
* **Construction & updates:** Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, foundation.
* **Location risks:** Hail/wind/wildfire, crime, distance to hydrant/station.
* **Claims history:** Yours and sometimes property CLUE.
* **Coverage & deductibles:** Higher limits/low deductibles cost more; % wind/hail affects price.

### Ways to save

* **Bundle home + auto:** Often the biggest credit.
* **Mitigation:** Monitored alarms, leak sensors; roof/fortification upgrades.
* **Deductible strategy:** Balance out-of-pocket vs. premium.
* **Right-size contents:** Don’t insure items you no longer own; schedule what matters.
* **Annual review:** Re-shop after updates or when claims fall off.
