Progressive Insurance’s 3-digit code is 24260. This code is also known as the company’s NAIC code, which stands for National Association of Insurance Commissioners. The NAIC assigns these codes to authorized insurance providers to track customer complaints and ethics violations across state lines.
You can use the NAIC number to verify a company’s financial data, check fraud cases, and close and open complaints against the company in the past. You can find the NAIC code on your insurance card.
