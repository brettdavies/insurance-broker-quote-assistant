What makes a car a luxury model?

A true luxury car comes from a brand focused on providing a premium driving and owning experience. Buyers can expect the latest advancements in comfort, performance, safety, and technology in a luxury model. Many innovations start on upscale models before migrating to mainstream vehicles.

Buying a luxury vehicle is just the start for the added costs owners will experience. Often, luxury cars experience more depreciation because they can be expensive to maintain.
