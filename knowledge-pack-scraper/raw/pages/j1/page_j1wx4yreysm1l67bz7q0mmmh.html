GEICO Insurance was founded in 1936 by Leo and Lillian Goodwin. It was originally designed to provide federal employees and military personnel with lower-cost, high-quality insurance. 

GEICO is actually an anagram that stands for G- Government E-Employees I-Insurance Co-Company.

According to their market share, ratings, and the total number of policyholders, GEICO is currently ranking as the second-largest auto insurance provider in the nation. 

If youve been thinking about switching to Geico or youre considering purchasing coverage with them, then this article is for you. Below, were going to break down coverage policies, discounts that policyholders can apply for, and Geico insurances average cost.

GEICO Car Insurance Cost

So how much does geico car insurance cost. According to our  average car insurance cost per month  research, GEICO is actually a very affordable option. Monthly premiums are significantly more affordable than those of competitors.

The study mainly compared safe drivers and GEICO policyholders from around the USA and created an average premium amount based on different factors, such as the drivers age.

 The study showed that GEICOs average  full coverage insurance cost is $98 per month, or $1,175 per year.

 The lower end of the spectrum primarily consisted of older drivers (over age 65), while those who had higher premiums were younger drivers (around 25-years-old or older).

The average GEICO car insurance cost for minimum coverage is $40 per month or $480 per year, which is among the  cheapest car insurance quotes  you can find, especially among the larger and more well known companies.

GEICO typically offers six, nine, or twelve-month policy plans for auto insurance. Youll typically pay a downpayment to start your coverage and then pay off the rest of your vehicle insurance policy over several months.

How To Lower Your GEICO Car Insurance

Here are some of the best methods that you can use to lower your premium.

Maintain A Safe Driving Record

The safer you drive, the lower your insurance will be. Suppose you can avoid any accidents and avoid traffic tickets that put points on your license for several years at a time. In that case, your car insurance premiums will likely decrease naturally over time.

Raise Your Credit Score

One of the easiest ways to lower your GEICO car insurance rates is to raise your credit score, make all of your payments on time, and avoid any liens on your vehicle at all costs. Once you raise your credit score a bit, give your agent a call and talk to them about any discounts.

Take A Defensive Driving Course

GEICO offers a low-cost online defensive driving class that you can easily complete within a couple of hours on your next day off. After taking the course, youll receive a brief virtual test. If you pass the test, you may be able to save between 5% and 10% on your premiums.

Get A More Economical Car

If youre driving a fancy sports car, then expect to pay extra for it. These cars are statistically more likely to be involved in dangerous accidents. Consider switching to a more economical option with a standard 4-cylinder engine. Our cheapest cars to insure guide might be handy as well.

Move To Another City

The closer you live to a big city, the more youll pay. By moving out to the suburbs or into the country, you could receive a considerable discount on your monthly auto insurance rates.

Apply For Umbrella Insurance Policies

In addition to auto insurance, GEICO also offers life insurance, home insurance, renters insurance, motorcycle insurance and more. You can save on your total policy by bundling all of your policies together under one GEICO policy.

GEICO GAP Insurance Cost

GEICO GAP insurance cost cannot be measured since the company currently does not offer  GAP insurance  as part of its services.

How To Cancel GEICO Car Insurance

Cancelling your GEICO car insurance policy is pretty simple. All you need to do is call them up and let them know that youre interested in cancelling your policy. Its important to notify them before your next payment; otherwise, it will be automatically debited from your account. GEICO has a no-cancellation-fee policy , so you wont have to pay anything extra should you decide to cancel your coverage!
