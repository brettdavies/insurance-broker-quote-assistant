##  Progressive car insurance: Pros and cons
Pros
  * **Widespread availability:** Progressive issues auto insurance in all 50 states and Washington, DC
  * **Accident forgiveness:** Rates don't go up for claims under $500 and, if you've been accident-free for five years, even a larger accident won't raise your premiums.
  * **Good for high-risk drivers:** Progressive's rates for motorists with speeding tickets and at-fault accidents are below average.


Cons
  * **Poor customer service scores:** Progressive is ranked below average by J.D. Power for the claims process and overall customer satisfaction.
  * **Limited discounts:** Many competitors lower rates for installing anti-theft devices or taking a defensive driving course.
  * **Safe-driving program could raise rates:** According to Progressive, 20% of drivers who enrolled in its Snapshot usage-based insurance program saw their premiums increase, rather than decrease.


##  Types of Progressive car insurance
[Progressive](https://secure.money.com/pr/v68db5884625) offers auto insurance in all 50 states and Washington, DC. 
### Basic coverage
These are typically required if you leased or financed your car.
  * **Liability car insurance:** Minimum coverage allowed in most states,[liability ](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-liability-car-insurance/)pays for damages if you or someone else driving your car is at fault for an accident.
  * **Collision coverage.** Part of[ full coverage car insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-full-coverage-car-insurance/), collision coverage pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident.
  * **Comprehensive coverage.** Pays for damages not caused by a collision, including theft, [weather damage](https://www.cnbc.com/select/does-car-insurance-cover-hail-damage/) or hitting an animal.


Progressive automatically includes small accident forgiveness, which means your **r** ates won't go up for claims under $500. If you've been accident-free for at least five years, even a larger accident won't raise your premium.
### Additional coverage
These policies are required in some states in addition to full coverage**.**
  * **Personal injury protection insurance:** Also known as no-fault insurance,
  * **Medical payments insurance:** Covers medical bills for you, your passengers and any pedestrians, regardless of fault.
  * **Gap insurance:** Pays up to 25% over the value of your car if it's totaled to cover the balance of a loan or lease. Available to Progressive customers with collision and comprehensive insurance.
  * **Uninsured/underinsured motorist insurance:** Covers repairs and medical bills if you are in an accident with a driver with little or no insurance.


### Add-ons
Optional features offered by Progressive.
  * **Additional accident forgiveness:** Forgives one accident per policy period.
  * **Deductible Savings Bank:** $100 is subtracted from your deductible for each policy period you go without an accident or violation.
  * **Rental car reimbursement:** If your vehicle is damaged in a covered accident, this optional policy will reimburse the cost of a rental car, up to $60 per day for up to 30 days.
  * **Roadside assistance:** Customers can request assistance through the Progressive app and get help with jump starts, fuel delivery, lockouts, flat tires, towing and winching.
  * **Trip interruption:** Pays up to $500 for food, lodging, transportation and related costs if your car breaks down more than 100 miles from home.
  * **Vehicle protection:** Covers major systems, minor dings and dents and lost keys for newer cars.
  * **Custom parts and equipment value** : Pays up to $5,000 to fix or replace custom accessories.
  * **Rideshare insurance:** Expands coverage to any time the app is on, including when waiting to match with a rider.
  * **Veterinary expense coverage:** Policyholders with collision coverage are eligible for up to $1,000 in veterinary expenses if their pet is injured in a covered accident.

## FAQs
### How much is Progressive full coverage auto insurance?
In May 2024, a 40-year-old male with good credit and a good driving record will pay an average of $1,988 a year for full coverage insurance with Progressive, compared to the industry average of $2,314.
### Does Progressive cover a rental car during repairs?
Progressive offers optional rental car reimbursement if your vehicle is damaged in a covered accident. It will reimburse up to $60 per day for up to 30 days, though exact limits depend on your policy. 
### Does Progressive offer gap insurance?
Progressive's gap insurance pays up to 25% above the value of your car if it's totaled, to cover the balance of a loan or lease.
### How do I cancel Progressive auto insurance?
Progressive customers can cancel by phone, mail or email, but not online. You can stop coverage immediately or set a date for it to end. If you already paid for the current policy period, you'll receive a pro-rated refund.
