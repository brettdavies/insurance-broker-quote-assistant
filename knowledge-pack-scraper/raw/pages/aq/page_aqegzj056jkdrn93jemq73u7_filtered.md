Yes, State Farm does offer a defensive driving course discount. Eligible customers can save up to 15% on their State Farm car insurance bill by completing an approved driver safety course, depending on their state, age, and other factors.

Some defensive driving courses are taught online, while others are taught in a classroom. All the defensive driving courses that can result in a discount on your State Farm bill will teach you how to improve your skills behind the wheel and to be a safer, less aggressive driver.

## How To Get a State Farm Defensive Driving Course Discount

1. Request a list of qualified defensive driving courses and their requirements from a State Farm agent.
2. Register and pay for a course approved by State Farm. Defensive driving courses typically cost between $15 and $100.
3. Pass the defensive driving course. Courses typically take 4-8 hours to complete.
4. Present State Farm with your defensive driving course certificate of completion.
5. Make sure State Farm is applying your discount for completing a defensive driving course by checking your bill or contacting the company directly at [800-STATE-FARM](tel:+18007828332) (800-782-8332).

The State Farm defensive driving discount can last for as long as three years in most states. After it expires, customers can take another training course to get the discount again.
