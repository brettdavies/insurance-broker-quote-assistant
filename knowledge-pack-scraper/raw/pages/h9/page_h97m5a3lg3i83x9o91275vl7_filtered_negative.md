Progressive doesn’t advertise the defensive driver discount on its website, and the discount amount varies by state and[ ](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/resources/coverage)coverage. But you can typically get up to 15% in 34 states and Washington, D.C., after completing a defensive driving course.
For example, [Delaware](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/by-state/delaware) and [New York](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/by-state/new-york) drivers of all ages can save 10% on:[[1]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/progressive-defensive-driver-discount#cite-1)
In Delaware, the discount lasts for three years, and you can recertify within 180 days of the end of the three years to receive a 15% discount.[[2]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/progressive-defensive-driver-discount#cite-2)
Savings vary, and the discounts you already have factor into how much you can save, as several Reddit users found out.[[3]](https://www.compare.com/auto-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/discounts/progressive-defensive-driver-discount#cite-3) A few pointed out that many insurers cap how much you can save with discounts. So you may not save much with the defensive driving discount if you already have multiple discounts on your car insurance policy.
