Does Geico cover rental cars?

Yes, it does. Geico rental car insurance is an extension of coverage from your insurance policy. In other words, Geico car insurance provides the same coverage limits to rental cars as if you owned the vehicle.

If you want to make sure youre covered, buy the car rental insurance from the company. Geicos rental insurance will cover you, but purchasing more rental coverage guarantees that youre covered for complicated situations.

How does my insurance cover rental cars?

Geicos insurance for rentals expands your auto insurance policy to your rental vehicle. This can save you from out-of-pocket expenses that involve rental insurance fees.

How do I find the best rental auto insurance policy?

Rental insurance policies vary by company. Some car insurance companies may not extend your insurance policy to a rental vehicle.

Therefore, its wise to buy insurance with a rental company. This prevents you from getting into situations where a car insurance policy may not cover specific incidents.

How much is rental insurance?

Rental car insurance is between $5 and $60 per day. However, the price of your rental insurance depends on the vehicle youre renting.

Can rental car reimbursement cover auto insurance rental fees?

Yes, it can. Geicos insurance for rentals doesnt cover refunds. But you can add rental car reimbursement to your policy to get money back that you spent on a rental vehicle.

Since rental car insurance comes with a fee, you could be reimbursed.

Rental car reimbursement coverage is an option that provides a refund from the auto insurance company after youve paid for rental car service while your vehicle undergoes repairs.

Is rental insurance included in full coverage auto insurance?

Yes, it is. Full coverage car insurance is a combination of liability, collision, and comprehensive coverage. Your full coverage insurance also comes with other insurance options, such as rental car reimbursement, personal injury protection, medical payments, and emergency roadside assistance.

How much does rental car reimbursement cost per month?

Rental car reimbursement is between $2 and $15 a month, but you could pay more in your state. Auto insurance companies are known to issue more expensive rates for different states.

How do I save money on Geico auto insurance?

If youre concerned about the cost of Geico car insurance, look for available discounts. Geico is known for its insurance deals to customers, but did you know you can save money by bundling Geico homeowners insurance with auto insurance?

Adding other insurance products to your insurance bill can save at least 10% on Geico car insurance. Drivers who rent an apartment should also consider Geico renters insurance. You can use renters insurance to protect personal items stolen from your home or vehicle.

But the fastest way to cheap Geico insurance is through a clean driving record and excellent credit. You can save up to 35% when both of these factors are part of your car insurance policy.

Does Geico Cover Rental Cars? The Bottom Line

Geico is one of the few companies that cover your rental car, but you should also buy the rental coverage from the rental car company. This provides coverage from various angles.

You wont get rental insurance from Geico with liability-only coverage, so youll need to get full coverage insurance for extra benefits. Check out our Geico car insurance review to learn more about the company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you want to know more about car rental coverage? Lets look at some answers to frequently asked questions to learn more.

#1 Do I need auto insurance to rent a car?

No, you dont. But youll need a drivers license in addition to purchasing rental car insurance from the rental company.

#2 Do credit card companies provide rental insurance?

It depends on the credit card network. Visa and Mastercard are two credit networks that provide car rental insurance as a perk to customers.

#3 Is auto insurance higher on a rental car?

Yes, it is. On average, car insurance is $94 a month. Meanwhile, rental car insurance is between $5 and $60 a day.

The rental vehicle insurance model is designed for short-term use, and youll get a better deal through privately owned car insurance.

#4 Does travel insurance cover rental cars?

It depends on your travel insurance policy. Travel insurance policies with rental car collision and comprehensive coverage provide insurance for rentals. However, you could still face expensive out-of-pocket costs.
