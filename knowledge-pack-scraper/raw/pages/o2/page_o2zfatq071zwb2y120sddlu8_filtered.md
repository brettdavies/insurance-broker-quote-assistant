## Does Geico cover rental cars?

Yes, it does. Geico rental car insurance is an extension of coverage from your insurance policy. In other words, Geico car insurance provides the same coverage limits to rental cars as if you owned the vehicle.

If you want to make sure you’re covered, buy the car rental insurance from the company. Geico’s rental insurance will cover you, but purchasing more rental coverage guarantees that you’re covered for complicated situations.

### How does my insurance cover rental cars?

Geico’s insurance for rentals expands your auto insurance policy to your rental vehicle. This can save you from out-of-pocket expenses that involve rental insurance fees.

### How do I find the best rental auto insurance policy?

Rental insurance policies vary by company. Some car insurance companies may not extend your insurance policy to a rental vehicle.

Therefore, it’s wise to buy insurance with a rental company. This prevents you from getting into situations where a car insurance policy may not cover specific incidents.

### How much is rental insurance?

Rental car insurance is between $5 and $60 per day. However, the price of your rental insurance depends on the vehicle you’re renting.

## Can rental car reimbursement cover auto insurance rental fees?

Yes, it can. Geico’s insurance for rentals doesn’t cover refunds. But you can add rental car reimbursement to your policy to get money back that you spent on a rental vehicle.

Since rental car insurance comes with a fee, you could be reimbursed.

Rental car reimbursement coverage is an option that provides a refund from the auto insurance company after you’ve paid for rental car service while your vehicle undergoes repairs.

## Is rental insurance included in full coverage auto insurance?

Yes, it is. [Full coverage](https://www.insurantly.com/coverage/full-coverage/ "Full coverage") car insurance is a combination of liability, collision, and comprehensive coverage. Your full coverage insurance also comes with other insurance options, such as rental car reimbursement, personal injury protection, medical payments, and emergency roadside assistance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Do you want to know more about car rental coverage? Let’s look at some answers to frequently asked questions to learn more.

### #1 — Do I need auto insurance to rent a car?

No, you don’t. But you’ll need a driver’s license in addition to purchasing rental car insurance from the rental company.

### #2 — Do credit card companies provide rental insurance?

It depends on the credit card network. Visa and Mastercard are two credit networks that provide car rental insurance as a perk to customers.

### #3 — Is auto insurance higher on a rental car?

Yes, it is. On average, car insurance is $94 a month. Meanwhile, rental car insurance is between $5 and $60 a day.

The rental vehicle insurance model is designed for short-term use, and you’ll get a better deal through privately owned car insurance.

### #4 — Does travel insurance cover rental cars?

It depends on your travel insurance policy. Travel insurance policies with rental car collision and comprehensive coverage provide insurance for rentals. However, you could still face expensive out-of-pocket costs.
