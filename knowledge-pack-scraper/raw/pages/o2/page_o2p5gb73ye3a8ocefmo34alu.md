## GEICO Auto Insurance Review [2025]: What Does It Offer Other Than Funny Commercials?

### A Brief History of GEICO
Founded in 1936 by Leo Goodwin and his wife, the Government Employees Insurance Company (GEICO) initially focused on insuring federal government employees and military personnel. Over time, GEICO expanded its reach and services, becoming a household name in the auto insurance industry. As of 2025, GEICO is a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, demonstrating its financial strength and stability.

GEICO offers a user-friendly website and a mobile app that allow customers to manage their policies effortlessly. This technology-driven approach includes features like quoting, policy management, claims filing, and an informative blog to educate customers about their coverage options.

GEICO offers 24/7 customer support through various channels, including phone, chat, and the mobile app. Customers appreciate the accessibility and responsiveness of the service agents, which enhances the overall experience. Regular surveys indicate that GEICO customers report high satisfaction with the quality of support provided.
