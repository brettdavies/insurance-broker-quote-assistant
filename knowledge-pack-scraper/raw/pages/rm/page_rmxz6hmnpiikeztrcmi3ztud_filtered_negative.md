##  Key Metrics for Success:
* **Monitor your CTR regularly:** If it's consistently low, consider revising your headlines or visuals to make them more captivating.
* **Track your conversion rates:** Track conversion rates to evaluate ad effectiveness. Use A/B testing on copywriting styles or CTAs to optimize for higher conversions.
