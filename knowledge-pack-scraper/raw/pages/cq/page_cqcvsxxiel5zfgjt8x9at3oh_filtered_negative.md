Thirty-four states and Washington, D.C., require insurers to offer 5% to 15% [government-mandated discounts](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/senior-citizen-discounts-for-car-insurance/#government-mandated-discounts) to seniors who complete approved defensive driving courses, typically starting at age 55. Age requirements vary, with Connecticut and West Virginia setting them at 60 and D.C. at 50. Discounts must be renewed every two to three years.
