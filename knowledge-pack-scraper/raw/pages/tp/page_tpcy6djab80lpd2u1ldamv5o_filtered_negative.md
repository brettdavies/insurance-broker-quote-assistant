* Standing: Florida courts have not always been consistent when deciding who may bring a claim under FDUPTA despite the otherwise broad statutory definition of consumer. Some courts have decided FDUPTA only includes people who were deceived when buying or selling goods or services. On the other hand, some courts allowed FDUPTA claims when any person was injured by a deceptive or unfair practice, _regardless_ of whether goods or services were bought or sold.
