    Steer Clear - A State Farm Program

    Steer Clear is an app that allows you to help young drivers reinforce positive driving behavior and earn a discount on their State Farm insurance. You may be eligible for a discount on your car insurance when you complete this program.

    Drivers between the ages of 15 and 25, who successfully complete this program will be given an account key that they can use to track their progress, as well as drive safely on their routes.

    Steer Clear is designed to be a part of the overall program that helps young drivers reinforce positive driving behavior. The program consists of three phases that you can use to help teach young drivers the basics of driving in a safe manner.

    Steer Clear is a State Farm program that offers a discount on your car insurance if you complete this program. If you are interested in the discounts you may be eligible for, please contact your State Farm insurance agent or an agent representative.

    In order to become a member of the Steer Clear program, all you need to do is download this app and complete the program.

    You may need to complete an in-app registration to get started on this program. This app will guide you through this process and you can cancel your account from this app at any time.

    Steer Clear is a State Farm program and you will receive a discount on your insurance. This discount will be reflected in the total cost of your insurance. If you have any questions, please contact your State Farm insurance agent.
