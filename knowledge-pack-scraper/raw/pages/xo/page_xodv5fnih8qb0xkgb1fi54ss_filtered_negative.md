Texas requires drivers to prove financial responsibility, most commonly by buying **liability insurance**. The minimum limits are **30/60/25** —$30,000 per injured person, $60,000 per crash total for injuries, and $25,000 for property damage. These are **minimums**, not recommendations. [Texas Department of Insurance+1](https://www.tdi.texas.gov/pubs/consumer/cb020.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
Texas insurers must also **offer** two important protections—**Personal Injury Protection (PIP)** and **Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist (UM/UIM)** —and you can decline them **only by rejecting in writing**. [Texas Department of Insurance+1](https://www.tdi.texas.gov/commercial/pcckautp.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
