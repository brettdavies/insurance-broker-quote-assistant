For example, State Farm [endorsement](https://www.valuepenguin.com/homeowners-insurance-endorsements-scheduling "/homeowners-insurance-endorsements-scheduling") covers $15,000 in combined cyber extortion and cyber attack damages for $25 per year. This endorsement, named Cyber Event, Identity Restoration and Fraud Loss Coverage, also covers policyholders for up to $50,000 for identity restoration expenses and fraud losses. This provides individuals with more than enough protection for the average cyber crime cost at an affordable price.
Coverage for financial loss as a result of identity theft, stolen bank funds or fraudulent use of credit cards or checks can either come from aa standalone identity theft coverage policy or an [identity theft endorsement](https://www.valuepenguin.com/homeowners-insurance-coverage "/homeowners-insurance-coverage") for a homeowners insurance policy. Not allidentity theft coverage protects against the wider range of risks posed by cyber security threats.
One of the most prominent insurers to offer comprehensive cyber protection is State Farm, with its Cyber Event, Identity Restoration and Fraud Loss coverage. This coverage can be purchased as an add-on to State Farm home or [cheapest renters insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/cheapest-renters-insurance-companies "Cheapest Renters Insurance Companies").
Otherwise, personal cyber insurance is available as a stand-alone policy or as add-on coverage from a set of smaller insurers, or from a select group of major insurers who sell [high-value home insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/high-value-home-insurance "/high-value-home-insurance"). This is insurance only for those who have homes approaching $1 million in value or more.
---|---|---|---
[Cincinnati Insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/cincinnati-financial-insurance-review "Cincinnati Financial Insurance insurance review") offers basic coverage for cyber extortion, cyber attack and online fraud. It also offers the option of up to $1 million in protection for certain policyholders, including those with what it calls Executive or Capstone coverage.
[Safety's](https://www.valuepenguin.com/safety-insurance-review "Safety insurance review") coverage includes protection from data breaches, computer and home systems attack, cyber extortion and online fraud. Coverage limits top out at $50,000, with a $500 deductible.
In addition to many of the standard coverages, [Arbella](https://www.valuepenguin.com/arbella-auto-insurance-review "Arbella insurance review") also offers data breach protection. That covers review from forensic IT or legal representation when personal data entrusted to a policyholder is stolen, lost or published.
[Selective](https://www.valuepenguin.com/selective-insurance-review "Selective insurance review"), which has multiple offices in the Northeast, says it provides cyber attack, cyber extortion, online fraud protection and identity theft coverage. It covers computers, tablets, smartphones and other connected technology.
[Plymouth Rock's](https://www.valuepenguin.com/plymouth-rock-insurance-review "Plymouth Rock insurance review") coverage includes cyber attack, cyber extortion, online fraud, cyber bullying and data breach coverage.
A Wisconsin-based company, [Acuity](https://www.valuepenguin.com/acuity-insurance-review "Acuity insurance review") offers a full range of coverages that includes cyber protection legal expense and damages reimbursement. It can be added to a renters, condo or homeowners policy.
