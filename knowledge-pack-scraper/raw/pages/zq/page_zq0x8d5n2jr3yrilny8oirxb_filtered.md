## Who is Eligible for the Steer Clear Discount

To be eligible for the Steer Clear discount, drivers must meet the following criteria:

* Be under the age of 25
* Have a clean driving record with no at-fault accidents or moving violations in the past three years
* Have completed a driver education course or have a minimum of 30 hours of supervised driving experience
* Be able to provide proof of completion of the Steer Clear Safe Driver Program

If you meet these criteria, you may be eligible for a discount on your car insurance premiums through the Steer Clear program.

### What is the State Farm Steer Clear car insurance discount?

The State Farm Steer Clear car insurance discount is a program that rewards young drivers for safe driving habits. The program consists of completing a set of online modules and driving for a specified amount of time without any accidents or violations. Upon completion, eligible drivers can receive a discount on their State Farm car insurance premium.

### Who is eligible for the State Farm Steer Clear car insurance discount?

The State Farm Steer Clear program is designed for young drivers between the ages of 16 and 25 who have a clean driving record or a limited driving history. However, the program may not be available in all states and eligibility requirements can vary.

### How much can I save with the State Farm Steer Clear car insurance discount?

The amount of savings you can receive from the State Farm Steer Clear car insurance discount can vary depending on several factors, including your driving record, age, location, and the insurance coverage you select. On average, drivers can save up to 15% on their car insurance premium after completing the program.

### How long does the State Farm Steer Clear program take to complete?

The State Farm Steer Clear program consists of five online modules that cover various driving topics, such as defensive driving and risk management. The program typically takes between 4 and 5 hours to complete, but drivers can work at their own pace.

### Can I participate in the State Farm Steer Clear program if I’m not a State Farm customer?

No, the State Farm Steer Clear program is only available to State Farm customers. If you’re interested in participating, you’ll need to purchase a State Farm car insurance policy first.
