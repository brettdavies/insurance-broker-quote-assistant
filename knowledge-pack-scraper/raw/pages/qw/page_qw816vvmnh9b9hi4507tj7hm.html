Benefits and Claims

Key Facts

  • Life insurance benefits and claims in 2024 totaled $965.6 billion, including life insurance death benefits, annuity benefits, disability benefits and other payouts, up from $831.8 billion in 2023.
  • The largest 2024 payout category was surrender benefits and early terminations or cash withdrawals from life insurance contracts for $484.2 billion.
