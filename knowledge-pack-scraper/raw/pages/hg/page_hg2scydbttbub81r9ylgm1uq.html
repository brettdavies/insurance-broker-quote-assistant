What Is the AAA Mature Driver Discount?

The AAA mature driver discount is a program offered by AAA that provides discounted auto insurance rates to mature drivers who meet certain criteria. The program is designed to recognize that older drivers have more experience behind the wheel — and are therefore less risky drivers.

To qualify for the AAA mature driver discount, you need to be at least 55 years old and have a valid driver’s license. Here we’ll take a look at the details of this program and how you can qualify.

What Are the Requirements for the AAA Mature Driver Discount?

To qualify for the AAA mature driver discount in California or Florida, you must:

  • Be at least 55 years old
  • Have a valid California or Florida driver’s license
  • Have AAA insurance coverage on at least one vehicle
  • Not have any at-fault accidents or DUIs on your driving record.

How to Get the AAA Mature Driver Discount

Here’s how to qualify for the mature driver discount, step-by-step.

1. Make sure you meet the criteria for the program. You must be at least 55 years old with a valid California driver’s license and AAA insurance coverage for your vehicle(s). You also must not have any at-fault accidents or DUIs on your driving record.

2. Sign up for a DMV-approved mature driver course. At Traffic Safety Institute, we offer both a California Mature Driver Course and a Florida Mature Driver Course. Both are approved and licensed by the DMV and accepted by all insurance carriers. And, if you don’t get at least $100 back over three years, we’ll give you your money back.

3. Complete your course and receive your insurance discount certificate. At Traffic Safety Institute, and can complete the course at your own pace, and you receive your insurance discount certificate as soon as you pass the course.

4. Submit your insurance discount certificate to AAA and start saving money on your insurance premiums.

FAQ About the AAA Mature Driver Discount

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions about the AAA mature driver discount program and how to save money on your insurance coverage.

1. What’s the AAA Mature Driver Discount Age?

In California and Florida, you need to be at least 55 to qualify for the AAA mature driver discount. 99% of drivers 55 and older are eligible for the program.

2. How Long Is the AAA Mature Driver Discount Valid?

The discount remains valid for three years of insurance discounts. Every three years, you’ll need to take a renewal course if you want to keep your discount.

3. How Much Can I Save With the AAA Mature Driver Discount?

Discounts are offered at the discretion of individual auto insurance providers, so you should always check with your insurance provider for exact savings.

The typical discount for mature drivers who submit their insurance discount certificate to AAA is 5%.

Considering the average cost of car insurance premiums in California is $2,291 per year, a 5% discount over the three years the mature driver discount is valid would equal $343.65 in savings.

If you take our Mature Driver Course through Traffic Safety Institute, we guarantee at least $100 in savings over 3 years — no other course providers do that!

4. How Long Is the Mature Driver Course?

The California Mature Driver Course consists of 13 modules with easy-to-follow material. California requires a minimum of 400 minutes of in-class instruction for the initial course, and 240 minutes for the refresher course. Our Florida Mature Driver Course includes 7 modules and a 40-question final exam.

If you choose an online course (like the one we offer), you can complete the course at your own pace from the comfort of your home. Many drivers finish the course in a single sitting — but you can take your time if you’d rather.

5. How Do I Get the AAA Mature Driver Discount?

If you qualify for AAA’s mature driver discount, all you need to do is complete a Mature Driver Course and pass the final exam. Your insurance discount certificate will be mailed to you, and you can present it to your AAA insurance agent for your discount.

6. Does AAA offer a Mature Driver Improvement Course?

Yes. In addition to getting a discount for completing a mature driver course, AAA also offers their own course. AAA’s RoadWise Driver course is available online. The course costs $19.95
