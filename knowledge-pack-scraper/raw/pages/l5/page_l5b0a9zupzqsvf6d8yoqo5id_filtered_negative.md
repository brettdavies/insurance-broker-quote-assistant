* * *
[State Farm](https://www.valuepenguin.com/state-farm-insurance-review "State Farm Insurance Review") isn't the cheapest insurance company for seniors, but it offers many discounts that could make it a lot more affordable. You may get a discount for safe driving, having a safe car and being a loyal customer. You might even find State Farm more affordable than other providers.
State Farm offers excellent customer service along with a wide range of products, making it a great choice for seniors looking to [bundle policies](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-bundling-can-save-you-money-car-insurance "Bundling Insurance Policies"). It has a discount for those who also buy a home, condo, renters, farm, life or health insurance policy.

[Geico](https://www.valuepenguin.com/geico-insurance-review "Geico Insurance Review") has the most affordable car insurance rates for seniors. The company also offers a guaranteed renewal program called the Prime Time contract for drivers over the age of 50 who meet  certain criteria. It guarantees that your policy won't be canceled, even if you get multiple tickets.
Seniors should expect average customer service from Geico. The company earned average scores on both the J.D. Power claims satisfaction study and the  NAIC complaint index. Geico does not underwrite its homeowners insurance policies and offers fewer kinds of insurance than its competitors, which means that seniors looking to bundle policies may want to consider other companies.

[AARP auto insurance from The Hartford](https://www.valuepenguin.com/aarp-auto-insurance-review "Review of AARP Auto Insurance From The Hartford") includes extras that can be especially useful for seniors. For example, if you're hurt in an accident, AARP car insurance includes RecoverCare. This program helps pay for things like transportation, dog walking, cleaning and lawn mowing while you recover. The company will also help you find a repair shop after an accident, and if you use one of its more than 1,600 approved shops, the repairs are guaranteed for as long as you have the vehicle.
However, the cost of AARP auto insurance can go up quickly in your 70s and 80s, so [compare car insurance quotes](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance "Compare Car Insurance Quotes Online") to make sure you don’t overpay.
