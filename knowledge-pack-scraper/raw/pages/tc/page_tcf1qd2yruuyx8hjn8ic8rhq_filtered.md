State Farm Drive Safe & Save will not raise your rates due to poor driving habits like distracted driving or speeding, though you may not be able to get the maximum discount. State Farm Drive Safe & Save users are eligible for an initial discount of 10% just for signing up, as well as a discount of up to 30% based on their driving habits.

## Key Things to Know About State Farm Drive Safe & Save

* State Farm Drive Safe & Save tracks things like acceleration, hard breaking, fast cornering, speeding, and distracted driving
* Drivers can use the Drive Safe & Save app to track their habits
* State Farm Drive Safe & Save has a 4.7/5 rating from WalletHub’s editors
* Not available in CA, MA, & RI
* Customer reviews of the app average 4.4 out of 5
* Minimum coverage from State Farm costs an average of $60 per month without Drive Safe & Save

Regardless of whether you use Drive Safe & Save, State Farm can raise your rates for things like at-fault accidents, speeding tickets, and other moving violations. State Farm learns of such things from your [driving record](https://wallethub.com/edu/ci/drivers-license-check/12553) and [CLUE report](https://wallethub.com/edu/ci/clue-report/10595), rather than through Drive Safe & Save.
