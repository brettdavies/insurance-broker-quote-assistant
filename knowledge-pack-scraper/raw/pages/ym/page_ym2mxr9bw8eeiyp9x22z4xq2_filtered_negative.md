State Farm’s homeowners insurance includes these [**standard coverages**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/knowledge/what-does-homeowners-insurance-cover/):
* **Personal liability:** [**Personal liability coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/personal-liability/) protects you if someone sues you because of an injury or property damage that you cause or that happens on your property.
* **Dwelling:** [**Dwelling coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/knowledge/dwelling-insurance/) pays to repair or rebuild your home and attached structures, such as a garage, if it’s damaged or destroyed by a covered peril. It might also cover sheds and other stand-alone structures.
* **Personal property:** [**Personal property coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/personal-property-coverage/) protects you against losses if your personal belongings are stolen, damaged, or destroyed by a covered peril.
* **Additional living expenses:** Additional living expenses, or [**loss of use coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/loss-of-use-coverage/), pays your living costs if you’re displaced from your home while it’s being repaired or rebuilt after damage from a covered peril.
If your home needs repair or replacement and updated building codes would make the work more expensive, [**ordinance or law coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/law-and-ordinance-coverage/) pays the extra amount, up to certain limits.
Your standard policy won’t cover damage from [**earthquakes**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/earthquake-insurance/), mudslides, and other events categorized as earth movement. You can add this coverage to your policy.
[**Sewer and drain backups**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/sewer-backup-insurance/) can cause serious damage to your home and its contents. This optional coverage helps cover the cost.
Standard insurance might cover mold if it’s the direct result of a covered peril, such as water infiltration due to a hole in the roof from a fallen tree. But most insurers limit this coverage. Optional [**mold coverage**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/knowledge/mold-damage/) raises the limit and provides better protection.
The national average homeowners premium is $2,584 for $300,000 worth of coverage. With an [**average premium**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/average-cost-of-homeowners-insurance/) of $2,133 for the same amount of coverage, State Farm insurance costs less, and it also has lower rates than other large companies, like Travelers, Allstate, and Nationwide. But you might find better rates with American Family and AIG.
