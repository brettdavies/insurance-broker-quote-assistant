### Promoting the Education of Insurance Marketing, Legal and Compliance Professional

The Insurance Advertising Compliance Association (IAdCA) is a not-for-profit organization whose fundamental purpose is to promote the education of insurance marketing, legal and compliance professionals as well as to create a beneficial impact on insurance advertising regulation through active participation from these individuals and their industry firms.

The organization specifically focuses on how marketing, legal and compliance individuals can work together in support of insurance advertising compliance to create a number of benefits for themselves and the industry.
