[**Comprehensive insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/comprehensive-insurance/) pays for business vehicle damage caused by theft, vandalism, fire, or weather events. [**Collision insurance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/collision-insurance/) pays for vehicle repairs if your car hits another car or object, regardless of fault.
[**Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/coverage/uninsured-motorist-coverage/) pays for damage if a driver without insurance or inadequate coverage hits your commercial vehicle. It can cover medical bills and vehicle repair costs.
[**Medical payments coverage**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/medical-payments-coverage/) helps pay for medical expenses for the driver and their passengers after an accident, no matter who was at fault.
[**Roadside assistance**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/what-is-roadside-assistance/) pays for up to one hour of labor if a covered vehicle breaks down. Services include towing, locksmithing, flat-tire changes, fuel or fluid delivery, jump-starts, and more.
