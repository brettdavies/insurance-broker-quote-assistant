# Progressive Insurance® Announces Recognition for 2024 Platinum 25 Agents
[News](https://www.rate.com/insurance/category/news/)
**MAYFIELD VILLAGE, Ohio, April 14, 2025-** Progressive Insurance has announced the list of agents who achieved a Platinum 25 ranking based on their 2024 results. Each year, Platinum 25 honorees include the top Platinum Blue-level agents who are recognized for their commitment to placing preferred bundled home, condo, and auto business with Progressive.
Progressive’s Platinum program represents exclusive, invitation-only access to the Progressive Home™ suite of products for those agencies who commit to growing their preferred bundled home, condo, and personal auto business with Progressive. The Platinum 25 distinction continues to reward the 25 highest-performing Platinum agents who share Progressive’s vision of becoming consumers, agents, and business owners’ No. 1 destination for insurance and other financial needs.
* Couri Insurance Agency* (Waukesha, WI)
* Platinum Insurance Group* (Pleasant View, UT)
* Image* (Fort Atkinson, WI)
* Atlas Insurance Brokers* (Rochester, MN)
* Rate Insurance Agency LLC* (Summit, NJ)
* Meridian Capstone* (Dayton, OH)
* Automobile Club Insurance* (Bellevue, WA)
* Michigan Insurance & Financial* (Utica, MI)
* Goldenwest Insurance Services* (Ogden, UT)
* First Tech Insurance* (Hillsboro, OR)
* Hometown Insurance Agency* (Tulsa, OK)
* America’s Choice Insurance* (Wixom, MI)
* Legacy Insurance Group LLC* (Vernon, TX)
* Insurance Producers Network* (Eden Prairie, MN)
* Evertree Midatlantic* (Virginia Beach, VA)
*Indicates agency is a repeat Platinum 25 honoree.
Progressive congratulates the newest Platinum 25 class for their exceptional 2024 business results. Progressive agents interested in learning more about the Platinum 25 and eligibility requirements for 2025 can visit [ForAgentsOnly.com](https://www.foragentsonly.com/login/) (FAO).
Progressive Insurance® makes it easy to understand, buy and use [car insurance](https://www.progressive.com/auto/), [home insurance](https://www.progressive.com/homeowners/), and other protection needs. Progressive offers choices so consumers can reach us however its most convenient for them — online at [progressive.com](https://www.progressive.com/), by phone at 1-800-PROGRESSIVE, via the Progressive mobile app, or in-person with a local agent.
Progressive provides insurance for personal and commercial autos and trucks, motorcycles, boats, recreational vehicles, and homes; it is the second largest personal auto insurer in the country, a leading seller of [commercial](https://www.progressivecommercial.com/) auto, [motorcycle](https://www.progressive.com/motorcycle/), and [boat](https://www.progressive.com/boat/) insurance, and one of the top 15 homeowners insurance carriers.
