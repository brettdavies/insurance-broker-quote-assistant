For minor [car accidents](https://crockettlawgroup.com/irvine/car-accident-attorney/), a police report may not be necessary, especially if no one is injured, the damage is minor, all parties are cooperative, and there's valid license and insurance information exchanged.
But under California law, as the driver involved in a car accident, you must submit a written report to the California Highway Patrol or local police within 24 hours if the accident led to injuries or [wrongful death](https://crockettlawgroup.com/irvine/wrongful-death-attorney/). Additionally, within ten days after the accident, you must inform the California Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) if the accident resulted in injuries, death, or if the property damage exceeds $1,000.
