## FAQ
###  [ How do I file a claim with Progressive? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-0)
Call 1-800-776-4737 (24/7), file online at progressive.com/claims (Progressive Claims Portal), or use the Progressive Mobile App to submit details and photos.
###  [ What information do I need to report a claim? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-1)
Policyholder name, policy number, accident date, time, and location, vehicle information, and police report number (if available). You’ll receive a confirmation and claim number after submission.
###  [ How quickly will Progressive acknowledge my claim? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-2)
By state: Texas generally within 15 days; California within 40 days of receiving proof of claim; Illinois follows prompt response standards.
###  [ When will an adjuster contact me? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-3)
After intake, a centralized adjuster is assigned. First contact can take several days, especially in high-volume areas like Dallas–Fort Worth, Los Angeles County, and Cook County.
###  [ How does Progressive handle vehicle inspections and estimates? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-4)
Progressive often uses photo estimating and AI via the Mobile App. While fast for minor damage, these estimates may undervalue repairs compared to in-person inspections.
###  [ Do I have to use a Progressive Service Center or preferred shop? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-5)
No. You have the right in Texas, California, and Illinois to choose your own repair shop. Progressive Service Centers are optional conveniences.
###  [ Where can I find Progressive Service Centers? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-6)
Search progressive.com/claims or call the claims hotline for locations in major metro areas. They can coordinate inspections, repairs, and rentals.
###  [ Is there a benefit to using a non-network repair shop? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-7)
Often, yes. Independent shops may uncover hidden damage not captured in initial photo/AI estimates, which can change repair costs or lead to a total loss determination.
###  [ How are injury claims handled in Texas, California, and Illinois? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-8)
Texas policies commonly include PIP or UM/UIM; California uses pure comparative fault (you can recover even if mostly at fault); Illinois uses modified comparative negligence with a 51% bar (no recovery if more than 50% at fault). Coverage routing (PIP, MedPay, liability) can be confusing.
###  [ Should I give a recorded statement right away? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-9)
Not without preparation. You must cooperate, but you don’t have to give an immediate recorded statement. Gather facts and consider consulting an attorney first.
###  [ Should I sign documents Progressive sends me without review? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-10)
No. Documents may include binding “full and final release” language. Signing can end your right to additional compensation. Read carefully or have an attorney review.
###  [ Does delaying medical treatment affect my claim? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-11)
Yes. Waiting to seek care can let the insurer argue your injuries are minor or unrelated. Get prompt treatment and follow medical recommendations.
###  [ Should I accept the first settlement offer? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-12)
Generally no. Initial offers, especially for injuries, are often low. Negotiation commonly increases the offer. Consider legal advice before accepting.
###  [ How are settlements and payments issued? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-13)
Property damage may be paid after repairs or as a total loss valuation (often lower than expected). Payments can be by check or direct deposit. Lienholders (e.g., medical providers, lenders) may be paid first.
###  [ What are warning signs of undervaluation or bad faith by Progressive? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-14)
Very low initial offers, pressure to settle quickly, “full and final release” language without fair compensation, excessive requests for unrelated medical records, and denials of obvious liability or clear coverage.
###  [ How do I submit legal claim letters (e.g., representation or spoliation) to Progressive? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-15)
Use official channels: the Claims Portal, Mobile App, or 1-800-776-4737. If mailing, send via trackable delivery to: P.O. Box 94739, Cleveland, OH 44101. Additional contacts cited in filings: Phone 800-444-4487, Fax 440-603-4555. Always include insured’s name, policy number, accident details, vehicle info, and police report number.
###  [ How do I confirm Progressive received my legal letter or claim documents? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-16)
Follow up by calling the claims hotline to confirm receipt, obtain a claim number, and request the adjuster’s direct contact information.
###  [ How can I track my Progressive claim? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-17)
Use the Progressive app or website for status updates. Note that updates can be inconsistent; proactive follow-up calls may be necessary.
###  [ What state-specific timelines and rights should I know? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-18)
Texas: acknowledge within 15 days and decide within 15 business days after required documentation; California: respond within 40 days of proof of claim and cannot force you to use specific shops; Illinois: minimum coverage 25/50/20, modified comparative negligence (51% bar), and oversight by the Illinois Department of Insurance.
###  [ What if Progressive denies liability? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-19)
Liability disputes are common and can cause delays. Document everything and consider legal representation to protect your rights and move the claim forward.
###  [ How long does Progressive take to pay a claim? ](https://gosuits.com/knowledge-base/how-to-file-a-progressive-insurance-claim-gosuits/#faq-20)
It varies by state law and claim complexity. Injury claims often take weeks to months, especially when liability or damages are disputed.

Yes. GoSuits represents clients in Texas, California, and Illinois, pressing for timely communication and fair outcomes. Call (844) 467-8487 for a free consultation.
