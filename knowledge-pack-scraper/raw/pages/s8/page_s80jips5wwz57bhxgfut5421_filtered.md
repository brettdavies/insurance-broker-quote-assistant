## Driver Discounts in California

![](/resources/img/usa/National-Discounts_good_driver.svg)

### California Good Driver Discount

This is one of the most valuable discounts for California car insurance, rewarding drivers who maintain a clean driving record. You’ll get this discount if all drivers of your vehicle have:

* Been licensed to drive for at least 3 years, including 18 months in the US or Canada
* No more than one violation point OR at-fault accident with only property damage within the last 3 years
* Had no DUIs within the last 10 years

![](/resources/img/usa/National-Discounts_defensive_driver.svg)

### Mature Driver Improvement Course Discount

This discount is available for drivers age 55 or over whom have successfully completed a California DMV-approved defensive driver course, within the last 3 years. The discount will not apply if the course was part of a court order.
