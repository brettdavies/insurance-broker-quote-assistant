If you rent cars often, a nonowner policy is usually cheaper in the long run than repeatedly buying [rental car insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/rental-car-insurance "Buying Auto Insurance from Rental Car Companies").

If you want extra liability protection, an [umbrella policy](https://www.valuepenguin.com/should-you-get-personal-umbrella-policy-you-car-insurance-policy "Should You Get an Umbrella Policy If You Already Have Car Insurance?") is a better solution than a nonowner policy.
