#  Teen Driver Insurance Discounts
**Safe Driver Discount** – If you have a clean driving record with no accidents or violations, you may receive additional discounts.
**Student Away Discount** – If you attend school more than 100 miles from home and don’t have regular access to a car, you may qualify for savings.
###  How to Qualify for a Teen Driver Discount
**Complete a DMV-Approved Driver’s Ed Course** – Many insurers require proof of completion to apply the discount.
**Drive Safely** – Avoiding accidents and traffic violations helps maintain lower insurance rates over time.
