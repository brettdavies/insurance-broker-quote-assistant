Yes, State Farm Safe and Save does track speed using a smartphone app paired with a bluetooth device. Safe and Save does not raise rates for poor driving, however, so even drivers who have a habit of speeding can try Safe and Save virtually risk-free. Still, drivers who tend to speed should remember that they won’t be able to get all the discounts from State Farm Safe and Save that safer drivers receive.

## What State Farm Safe and Save Tracks

* Speed
* Hard braking
* Cornering
* Phone use

Even though you won’t see rate increases just from using Safe and Save, State Farm will raise rates for things like speeding tickets, at-fault accidents and moving violations since these go on your driving record and can classify you as high-risk. That’s the case with any policy, from any insurer. As a result, practicing safe driving is the best way to keep your premiums low.
