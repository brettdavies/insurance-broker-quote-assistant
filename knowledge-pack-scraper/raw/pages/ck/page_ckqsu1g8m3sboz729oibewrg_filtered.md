For the most part, State Farm’s pros outweigh the cons that customers may experience, so [buying car insurance](https://www.carinsurance.org/buying-car-insurance-how-to-find-the-best-policy-for-you/) from State Farm may be the right choice for most drivers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does State Farm have a good reputation for paying claims?

Yes, State Farm has a good reputation for paying car insurance claims with a strong rating from J.D. Power.

### Did State Farm get downgraded by AM Best?

Yes, State Farm’s financial rating was recently downgraded by A.M. Best.

### Who gets State Farm’s best rates?

Good drivers in their 40s will have the best rates from State Farm (**Read More:** [How to Lower Your Car Insurance Cost](https://www.carinsurance.org/guide/shopping-for-a-new-policy/how-to-reduce-the-cost-of-auto-insurance/)).

### Who is State Farm’s biggest competitor?

Progressive and Geico are two of State Farm’s biggest competitors, as they have the next largest market shares.

### Why does my State Farm bill keep going up?

Rate increases can be due to a number of factors, such as changes to your driving record or location risk changes. If your rates are getting expensive, [compare rates](#mdabottom) with our free quote tool to find the cheapest coverage in your area.

### Are State Farm rates negotiable?

Car insurance rates at State Farm aren’t negotiable, but you can apply for State Farm car insurance discounts to lower your rates.

### Does State Farm sell gap insurance?

State Farm does sell gap insurance, but it is called payoff protector instead of gap at State Farm (**Learn More:** [Is State Farm gap insurance worth it?](https://www.carinsurance.org/state-farm-gap-insurance/)).

### Does State Farm charge to cancel a policy?

No, State Farm does not charge a cancellation fee.

### Can I cancel a State Farm claim?

Yes, you can cancel your State Farm claim by contacting your agent or customer service.

### Does State Farm refund if you cancel?

State Farm only refunds customers who cancel if they have prepaid for car insurance coverage. The exact cancellation and refund details can be found in your policy.

Make sure to line up another policy if you are keeping your car, as you need coverage to drive (**Learn More:** [Why You Need Car Insurance](https://www.carinsurance.org/why-you-need-car-insurance/)).

### Is State Farm pulling out of Florida?

No, State Farm Insurance is not pulling out of Florida.
