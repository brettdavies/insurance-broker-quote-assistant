![Calendar icon](https://www.cellinolaw.com/wp-content/themes/cellino-law-theme/_assets/src/img/calendar-solid.svg) February 20, 2025 |  Reading Time: 6 min
Auto insurance is one of those things that many drivers will try to get for the lowest cost possible. While we can all understand the value in saving money where you can, _should_ you always opt for the cheapest auto insurance coverages? When it comes to NYS auto insurance, the minimum required coverage options may not be enough to truly protect you in the event of an accident.
* **Personal Injury Protection (PIP) / No-Fault:** $50,000 per person
* **Comprehensive & Collision Coverage: **In the event that your vehicle incurs significant body damage that exceeds the at-fault party’s liability limits, this coverage will help to cover expenses necessary to fix your car.
