**Progressive is a good choice if you have a car loan or lease because it sells[gap insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-gap-auto-insurance-works "How Gap Auto Insurance Works").** If your car is totaled, this coverage pays the difference between its current value and your loan or lease balance. With this coverage, you won't be stuck paying off a loan or lease for a car you no longer have.
**If you have a brand-new car, you might want to consider Farmers.** That's because it offers [new car replacement](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance/new-car-replacement-insurance "New Car Replacement Insurance"), which pays for a brand new vehicle if your car is totaled in an accident.
