##  Car insurance cost by age
**Younger drivers usually pay more for car insurance than older drivers** with more experience on the road do. A lot more.
* The average [18-year-old driver](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/car-insurance-for-18-year-olds/) pays $2,673 a year for minimum car insurance.
###  Car insurance rates for 18-year-olds vs. 30-year-olds
---|---|---|---
[Young drivers pay the highest car insurance premiums](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/cheap-car-insurance-for-teens/) of any age group. This is mostly because insurers see them as a high risk due to their lack of experience behind the wheel. Teen drivers usually see their rates go down as they age and keep a clean driving history.
##  Car insurance cost after a speeding ticket
**The average cost of car insurance after a speeding ticket is $2,591 a year for full coverage.** This is 23% higher than the average car insurance cost with a clean driving record.
---|---|---|---
##  Car insurance costs after an accident
**Drivers with an at-fault accident on their records pay an average of $3,147 a year for full coverage.** This is 50% higher than what your average driver with a clean record pays.
State Farm has the [cheapest car insurance for drivers with an accident](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/best-car-insurance-for-bad-driving-record/) on their records at an average rate of $1,848 a year.
---|---|---|---
##  Car insurance cost with a DUI
**The average cost of full-coverage car insurance after a DUI (driving under the influence) is $3,891 a year.** That’s an 85% increase from what a driver with a clean record pays for the same amount of car insurance.
Progressive is the best company for [cheap car insurance with a DUI](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/cheap-dui-car-insurance/), charging an average of $2,652 a year. It’s also the company with the smallest average rate increase after a DUI. Progressive charges drivers with clean records $2,076 a year, which means its DUI rate is just 28% more.
---|---|---|---
If your license is suspended after a DUI, you’ll probably have to file an [SR-22](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/sr-22-insurance/) form with the state. The SR-22 proves that you carry the minimum amount of car insurance your state requires. You’ll need this to reinstate your license.
##  Car insurance cost by credit score
If you have poor or bad credit, get a quote from Geico to see what it will charge you. We found Geico to have the cheapest [car insurance for bad credit](https://www.lendingtree.com/car-insurance/car-insurance-for-bad-credit/) at $3,408 a year, on average. This is 61% higher than the average rate it charges drivers with good credit.
