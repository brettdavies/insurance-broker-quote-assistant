              Available discounts

              State Farm offers a few discounts to help you save money on your auto insurance.

              • Drive Safe & Save : Drive Safe & Save lets you take control of your auto insurance. Simply enrolling gives you an upfront discount, and then you have the potential to save up to 30% based on your driving habits.

              • Steer Clear: If youre a new driver or under 25, you may be eligible for their Sterr Clear program. This offers you the opportunity to save money while honing your skills to become a safer, more confident driver.

              • Bundling discount: Additional savings are offered if you have other State Farm insurance products, such as homeowners, renters, or life insurance, in addition to auto insurance. The website states that you can save up to $1,073 if you bundle auto & home insurance.

              • Multiple auto savings: If State Farm insures two or more vehicles in your household, their discounts can help lower your insurance premiums.

              State Farm offers fewer discounts compared to other top insurers. If youre looking for a more robust assortment of discounts, look into Liberty Mutual or American Family. 

              What Auto Insurance Coverage Options Are Available?

              State Farm offers plenty of coverage options when it comes to car insurance. This includes:

              Collision insurance

              Collision insurance steps in to cover the costs of repairing or replacing your vehicle if its been in a collision with another vehicle or object, no matter whos at fault. This coverage is pivotal for accidents, ensuring your car gets the attention it needs to get back on the road.

              Comprehensive insurance

              Comprehensive insurance offers protection against a range of incidents that arent collisions, like theft, vandalism, natural disasters, or run-ins with animals. Its the safety net for unexpected events, guaranteeing youre covered for a wide array of unforeseen circumstances.

              Rental insurance

              Rental car reimbursement, or rental insurance, kicks in if your car is undergoing covered repairs after a collision or comprehensive claim. It handles the expenses of a rental car up to a specific daily limit, making sure you have reliable transportation while your vehicle is in the shop.

              Rideshare insurance

              Rideshare insurance is tailored for folks who drive for services like Uber or Lyft. It provides coverage that effectively bridges the gap between your personal auto policy and the insurance offered by the rideshare company. This ensures youre appropriately covered while using your car for business.

              Emergency roadside service

              Emergency roadside service offers aid in common road-related emergencies. This can include services like towing, jump-starts for dead batteries, tire changes, lockout assistance, and fuel delivery. Its your lifeline when youre stuck on the road, assuring you that help is just a call away.

              Additional coverage options

              State Farms offers extensive additional coverages that can be used to customize a policy to your needs.

              A few noteworthy add-ons include:

              Uninsured motorist coverage

              Around one in eight drivers nationwide lacks car insurance, as per a 2017 report by the Insurance Research Council. Rates vary by state, ranging from about 5 percent (1 in 20 drivers) in Maine to over 26 percent (1 in 4 drivers) in Florida. In an accident with such drivers, they might not have the funds to cover damages. State Farm may cover medical expenses, pain and suffering, and lost wages in such scenarios.

              Underinsured motorist coverage

              Underinsured motorist coverage assists in covering accident expenses when youre not at fault. This applies even if the other driver has liability insurance but lacks sufficient coverage to pay for all the damages resulting from the accident.

              Medical payments

              Medical payments coverage (Med Pay) assists in covering medical and funeral expenses for the policyholders, passengers, or their family members injured in an auto accident, regardless of fault. This includes situations like being injured while in someone elses vehicle, using public transportation, or even while walking across the street.

              Liability coverage

              Liability car insurance covers costs for the other drivers property damage and injuries if youre deemed at fault in an accident. Your insurer covers expenses up to the specified limit for property damages and injuries.

              How To Buy State Farm Car Insurance

              Purchasing State Farm auto insurance can be done online or by calling a local State Farm agent. 

              1. First, begin the quote process with State Farms quote tool. You can also request a quote over the phone with a local agent. 

              2. To complete the quote, youll be asked a series of questions about your vehicle and its history, as well as some of your personal information. 

              3. Once State Farm has compiled the information it needs, the company will provide you with an online quote or connect you with a local agent. 

              4. Once you have your quote, you can contact a local agent in order to purchase your auto insurance. You can also purchase a plan online.

              Methodology

              Expertises auto insurance rating methodology takes into account the following details about each company:

              • Financial strength: This category examines a companys ability to pay future claims based on assessments from independent agencies like AM Best and Demotech. Companies with a higher score from these third-party companies ranked higher in this category. 

              • Customer satisfaction: Our team examined https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2023-us-auto-claims-satisfaction-study, which provides an analysis of customers perceptions and expectations of their insurers performance throughout the entire claims experience. Companies that ranked higher on J.D. Powers list scored higher in this category. 

              • Coverage availability: Our team examined state availability for each company and rated companies with higher availability than competitors. 

              • Consumer experience: For this category, our team reviewed each companys website, mobile app, quote tool, and customer service availability. 

              • Consumer complaints: Our team reviewed complaints to state regulators as reported by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC). To assess how insurers compare to one another, the NAIC calculates a complaint index each year for each subsidiary, measuring its share of total complaints relative to its size or share of total premiums in the industry. The NAIC assigns a median complaint ratio of 1.0. Companies with an NAIC complaint score of less than 1 scored higher in this category. 

              • Premium cost: This category assesses auto insurance costs based on the quotes we gathered from each company for a sample 2014 financed vehicle in Alabama. Companies that provided lower quotes scored higher in this category.  

              To score companies in these categories, we collected data points from the companies' websites, company representatives, state regulator data, and third-party analyses. 

              These data points were then compared against each other and given a star rating score, where one star represents a poor rating and a five-star score represents an excellent rating. To calculate the star rating, we looked at each companys total weighted score, divided by the maximum score, and multiplied by five.  

              Our ratings are weighted based on our editorial opinions. We use the following weightings for our auto insurance company ratings: 

              • Financial strength (20%) 

              • Customer satisfaction (15%) 

              • Coverage availability (10%)
