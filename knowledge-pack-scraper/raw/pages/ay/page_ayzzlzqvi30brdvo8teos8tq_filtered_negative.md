The [cost of SR-22 insurance in Indiana](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/sr-22-indiana#howmuch "#howmuch") depends on the incident that caused you to need an SR-22. For example, minimum-coverage SR-22 insurance costs an average of $83 per month for an Indiana driver with a DUI. In comparison, a driver convicted of reckless driving can expect to pay $86 per month for the same coverage.
The amount of time you're [required to have SR-22 insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/sr-22-indiana#how-long "#how-long") depends on the reason you need it. Drivers who have their license suspended need SR-22 insurance for three years after their license is reinstated. If you failed to provide proof of insurance to the Indiana BMV, you're required to have an SR-22 filing for 180 days.
SR-22 insurance refers to a certificate your insurance company needs to file with the Indiana BMV to prove you have insurance coverage after a serious traffic ticket or violation. Your policy must have the [minimum amount of coverage required in Indiana](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance/indiana/requirements "Auto Insurance Requirements in Indiana"), which includes $25,000 of bodily injury liability coverage per person and $50,000 per accident, $25,000 of personal property liability per accident and $50,000 of uninsured motorist bodily injury liability coverage. However, you can get more coverage if you need it.
To [get an SR-22 policy in Indiana](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/sr-22-indiana#get "#get"), you'll need to find an insurance company that offers SR-22 coverage, buy a policy and pay the SR-22 fee, which is usually between $15 to $50. Your insurance company will file your SR-22 certificate with the Indiana BMV to prove you have an insurance policy.
[State Farm, Progressive and Auto-Owners](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/sr-22-indiana#cheapest "#cheapest") offer the cheapest SR-22 insurance for most drivers in Indiana. The cheapest company for you depends on the driving infraction that caused you to need an SR-22.
[Progressive](https://www.valuepenguin.com/progressive-insurance-review "Progressive Insurance Review") has the cheapest SR-22 insurance for drivers with a DUI in Indianapolis. In comparison, a minimum-coverage policy from Progressive costs an average of $64 per month. That's 30% cheaper than the average in Indianapolis, which is $92 per month.
