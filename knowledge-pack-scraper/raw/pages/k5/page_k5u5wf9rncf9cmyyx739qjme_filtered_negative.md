KnowYourDrive uses a mobile app to monitor driving behavior, so drivers need to allow the app to use location and other phone permissions.[[2]](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/companies/compare/american-family-vs-state-farm/#cite-2)
State Farm’s [**Drive Safe & Save**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/companies/state-farm/drive-safe-and-save/) program allows safe drivers to save up to 30% on their car insurance policy based on driving behaviors and activity. The Drive Safe & Save program uses an app, but State Farm also sends a beacon to install in the car that helps provide more accurate readings.[[3]](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/companies/compare/american-family-vs-state-farm/#cite-3)
The cost of your coverage from these insurers will vary based on several factors, including your age, driving record, credit history, and location.[[4]](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/companies/compare/american-family-vs-state-farm/#cite-4) In addition, drivers can save more money by working with the insurer that offers the best discounts for their needs. Drivers who opt for minimum coverage will pay the lowest average premiums from both companies.
Drivers with a [**clean driving record**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/driver/driving-record/clean-driving-record/) can save more money on coverage by choosing a policy from State Farm.
