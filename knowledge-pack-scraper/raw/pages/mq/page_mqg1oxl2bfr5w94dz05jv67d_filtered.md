## Average Auto Insurance Premiums in Arizona by Age

It’s a fact of life: as you grow older, your car insurance rates shift. Generally, car insurance is more expensive for teenage drivers, as insurers see inexperienced motorists as riskier.

In Arizona, car coverage runs $6,279 per year for a 16-year-old driver, versus $1,025 annually for a driver between 50 and 59 years of age.

###### ARIZONA CAR INSURANCE RATES BY AGE

| Age | Average Yearly Premium |
| --- | --- |
| 16 | $6,278.78 |
| 17 | $5,672.25 |
| 18 | $4,938.33 |
| 19 | $2,919.05 |
| 20s | $1,579.72 |
| 30s | $1,160.93 |
| 40s | $1,109.32 |
| 50s | $1,025.45 |
| 60s | $1,076.14 |
| 70s | $1,374.51 |

If you're shopping for car insurance for a teen driver, Arizona is the 26th cheapest state in which to buy auto insurance for a 16-year-old.

## Typical Car Insurance Premiums for Bad Drivers in Arizona

If you’re found at fault in a crash or convicted of a vehicular violation, your car insurance premiums are bound to grow. The more red flags you accumulate, the pricier your car insurance gets.

In Arizona, your first minor at-fault incident results in a penalty of $628.55 each year in auto insurance payments. A DUI — considered one of the most grievous driving infractions — may raise your yearly insurance rates in Arizona by an average of $815.50.

## Average Auto Insurance Costs by Coverage in Arizona

How much you pay every month for car insurance depends on the tier of coverage you select. A liability-only policy normally costs less, while low-deductible comprehensive coverage usually costs more. In Arizona, the price gap between liability-only coverage and full coverage with a $500 deductible is $602.

###### ARIZONA CAR INSURANCE RATES BY COVERAGE LEVEL

| Coverage Level | Average Annual Premium |
| --- | --- |

| $100K/$300K/$100K Bodily Injury/Property Damage — $500 Comprehensive/Collision | $1,359 |
| $50K/$100K/$50K Bodily Injury/Property Damage — Liability Only | $660 |
| $50K/$100K/$50K Bodily Injury/Property Damage — $1,000 Comprehensive/Collision | $1,136 |
