## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is State Farm Drive Safe & Save?

State Farm Drive Safe & Save is a program offered by State Farm, one of the largest insurance companies in the United States. It is designed to reward safe drivers by providing potential discounts on auto insurance premiums based on their driving habits.

### How is my driving data used by State Farm?

State Farm uses the data collected from the telematics device or mobile app to evaluate your driving behavior. Based on your driving habits, you may be eligible for premium discounts.

### Will my insurance premiums increase if my driving data shows poor habits?

Participating in the Drive Safe & Save program will not cause your premiums to increase. However, if the data collected indicates poor driving habits, you may not be eligible for premium discounts. The program is primarily focused on rewarding safe driving rather than penalizing poor driving behavior.

### How do I enroll in State Farm Drive Safe & Save?

To enroll in State Farm Drive Safe & Save, you need to contact your State Farm agent. They will provide you with the necessary information and guide you through the enrollment process.

### Can anyone participate in State Farm Drive Safe & Save?

State Farm Drive Safe & Save is available to most State Farm auto insurance customers. However, eligibility may vary based on your location and the specific terms and conditions set by State Farm. Contact your State Farm agent to determine if you are eligible for the program.

### How does Drive Safe & Save work?

Drive Safe & Save monitors driving habits using a mobile app or Bluetooth beacon to calculate discounts based on safe driving behaviors.

### How long do you have to use State Farm Drive Safe & Save?

There is no specified time requirement; you can use Drive Safe & Save for as long as you are a State Farm customer and choose to participate.

### What is a good score for State Farm Drive Safe & Save?

State Farm does not publicly disclose a specific score, but higher scores typically reflect safer driving habits and can result in larger discounts.

### Is State Farm Drive Safe & Save worth it?

It can be worth it for drivers who consistently practice safe driving habits and want to potentially earn discounts on their auto insurance premiums.

### What is the State Farm driving score?

The State Farm driving score is a metric used to evaluate a driver’s habits and behavior on the road, influencing insurance rates.
