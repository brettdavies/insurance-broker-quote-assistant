# AAA vs Progressive: Prices, Discounts & Which Works Best for Your Driving
![AAA versus Progressive auto insurance comparison with car keys and policy document](https://blakeinsurancegroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/AAA-vs-Progressive.webp)
Comparing **AAA vs Progressive**? AAA pairs auto insurance with _club membership_ and legendary roadside benefits—though the insurers underwriting AAA policies vary by region. Progressive brings _nationwide pricing tech_, a deep discount stack, and a strong digital claims experience. Below, we break down costs drivers often qualify for, coverage differences, telematics, roadside, and who tends to come out ahead based on how and where you drive.

## Key takeaways before you choose
AAA policies are underwritten by regional affiliates; Progressive files rates nationally. Always compare real quotes for your _ZIP code_ and driver profile.
## AAA vs Progressive — at a glance
Area | AAA (club-based) | Progressive (national) | What to check
---|---|---|---
Pricing | Competitive in many regions; membership fee applies | Broad rating appetite; strong for bundlers & clean records | ZIP-specific quotes; membership cost vs net premium
Roadside & perks | Industry-leading roadside, travel, and retail perks | Roadside options available as add-on | How often you use towing/lock-out vs add-on price
Telematics | Telematics availability varies by affiliate/state | Snapshot® widespread; clear app feedback | Eligibility, discount range, privacy settings
Claims | Handled by regional AAA insurer | Large national network & digital tools | Local shop network and rental arrangements
Bundles | Auto + home/condo renters in many regions | Extensive bundle options (home, renters, toys) | True bundle savings at your address
Availability, underwriting companies, and program names vary by state and AAA club.
## Discounts & telematics programs
Category | AAA | Progressive | Pro tip
---|---|---|---
Safe driver | Common; criteria vary by affiliate | Accident-/violation-free tiers widely available | Ask for written criteria and lookback period
Telematics | Club/affiliate-specific programs in some states | Snapshot® app/device with driving feedback | Enroll all regular drivers for best results
Bundle | Auto + home/condo/renters (varies) | Auto + home/renters/umbrella/toys | Run quotes both bundled and unbundled
Student/family | Good student, distant student (varies) | Good student, teen driver programs | Provide GPA proof and transcripts early
Pay plan & paperless | Common, check with your club | Auto-pay, paperless, online quote | Stack easy credits before shopping coverages
Discount names, amounts, and eligibility change over time. Your quote and policy determine the final savings.
## Coverage & add-ons most drivers compare
Coverage/add-on | AAA | Progressive | What to confirm
---|---|---|---
Liability, comp, collision | Standard forms via regional insurer | Standard forms; customizable limits/deductibles | State minimum vs 100/300/100 or higher
Roadside assistance | Included with AAA membership (tiers) | Add-on roadside available | Number of tows/mi, lock-out, fuel delivery
Rental & trip interruption | Available in many regions | Rental reimbursement & trip interruption options | Daily limits, max days, covered events
Glass & OEM parts | Varies by club/insurer | Options in many states | OEM endorsements and calibration coverage
Rideshare | Varies; ask your club | Available in many states | Coverage for app-on to passenger drop-off
SR-22/FR-44 filing | Availability varies by region | Available in many states | Filing fees and state rules
Coverage names and endorsements differ by state. Your policy declarations and endorsements control.
## Claims, roadside & membership value
Area | AAA | Progressive | Decision tip
---|---|---|---
Claims handling | Through your regional AAA insurer | Large national network & digital tools | Ask about preferred shops & rental partners
Roadside help | Core membership benefit with tier choices | Optional add-on to your policy | Frequent tow/lock-out users often favor AAA
Membership perks | Travel, retail, DMV services (by club) | Carrier-specific perks via app/partners | Weigh perks you’ll actually use this year
Digital experience | Club & insurer portals vary | Strong quoting & app experience | Test apps before binding if usability matters
Response times and options depend on location, incident type, and partner availability.
## Who should choose which?
Choose | Best fit if you… | Why it works | What to check first
---|---|---|---
AAA | Value premium roadside & club perks; drive older vehicles; travel frequently | Membership benefits may outweigh a slightly higher premium | Total cost = premium + membership; roadside tow limits
Progressive | Want strong digital tools, Snapshot®, multi-policy bundling | Transparent discounts and broad appetite | Bundle price vs stand-alone; telematics comfort level
Either | Need rental, glass, or rideshare endorsements | Both offer options depending on your state | Endorsement availability and limits in your ZIP
There’s no universal “cheapest”—ZIP code, driver history, vehicle, and annual miles drive most of the difference.

## Frequently Asked Questions
Is AAA cheaper than Progressive?
It depends on your ZIP, driver history, vehicle, annual miles, and whether you value AAA’s membership perks. Run identical limits/deductibles to compare net cost including any club dues.
Do I need a AAA membership to buy AAA insurance?
AAA insurance is typically offered with club membership. Benefits and dues vary by regional club. Confirm the membership tier required for the policy you want.
Does Progressive include roadside assistance automatically?
Roadside is usually an optional add-on with Progressive. Compare the add-on price vs AAA membership value if you expect frequent use.
Which company is better for telematics discounts?
Progressive’s Snapshot® is widely available with clear app feedback. AAA telematics availability and names vary by club and state—ask what’s offered in your area.
Can you help me switch without a coverage gap?
Yes—bind the new policy first, match drivers/vehicles/limits, then set the effective date to the same day you cancel the old policy. We’ll provide written confirmations and ID cards.
