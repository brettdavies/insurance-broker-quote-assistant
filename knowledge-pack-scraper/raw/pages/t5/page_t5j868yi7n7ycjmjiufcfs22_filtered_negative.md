* How To Save on Auto Insurance
![Man and woman reviewing document.](https://www.statefarm.com/content/dam/sf-library/en-us/secure/legacy/simple-insights/how-to-save-on-auto-insurance.jpg)
Written by [State Farm® Editorial Team ](https://www.statefarm.com/simple-insights/editorial-team-and-process)
**_Overview:_**_Discover ways that may help you save on auto insurance by leveraging available discounts. Insurers commonly offer reductions for safe driving, good grades, vehicle safety features,_[_bundling policies_](https://www.statefarm.com/simple-insights/auto-and-vehicles/bundle-insurance) _, insuring multiple cars, usage-based programs (telematics), loyalty rewards and professional affiliations. Speak with your agent to confirm eligibility and ensure you’re optimizing your savings. Below, we’ll explain how to save on car insurance by exploring common auto insurance discounts._
One of the easiest ways to help save you on auto insurance is by exploring the variety of programs offered, like [auto discounts offered by State Farm](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/discounts). Insurance companies provide numerous opportunities for discounts based on driver behavior, vehicle attributes and policy details. Here are some of the most common:
* **Safe driving** —
* **Good student** — full-time students who keep their grades high (usually a “B” average or better) can qualify for savings. This is a great way for families to save on [car insurance for teens](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/car-insurance-for-teens).
* **Anti-theft systems** — vehicles with security alarms, tracking systems or other theft-deterrent devices often qualify for reduced rates on [comprehensive coverage](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/coverage-options/comprehensive-coverage).

* **Bundling policies** — combine your auto insurance with [renters](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/renters), [homeowners](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/homeowners) or [life insurance](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/life) to receive a significant [multi-policy discount](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/bundling).
* **Telematics programs** — companies like State Farm offer telematics options such as [Drive Safe & Save®](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/discounts/drive-safe-save), which provides potential savings for safe driving.
Enroll in programs that demonstrate safe driving habits (e.g., [Drive Safe & Save®](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/discounts/drive-safe-save)).
Complete an approved driver safety course (e.g., [Steer Clear®](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/discounts/steer-clear)).
