Any driver can purchase car insurance from GEICO, but you must have an AAA membership to get AAA car insurance.
While AAA and GEICO are two of the best car insurance companies, each one has strengths and weaknesses. Before choosing an insurance company, it’s important to [**compare rates**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/) as well as coverage options, discounts, customer service, and prices.

However, the cost of car insurance depends on many factors besides your insurance company. Your location, ZIP code, [**age**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/driver/age/), claim history, driving record, and your vehicle’s safety features will also affect your premium. It’s a good idea to get quotes from both companies to see which one will be the cheapest for your situation.
