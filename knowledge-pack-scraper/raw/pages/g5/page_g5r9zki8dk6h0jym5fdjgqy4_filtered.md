# Intent to Enter Massachusetts Marketplace

State Farm is excited to begin offering products and services through State Farm agents in early 2027.   
  
For more than 100 years, State Farm has offered its products and services to consumers across the US. Today, we’re pleased to announce that State Farm products and services will be offered through our agent network in Massachusetts.   
  
We expect availability in early 2027. Until then, you can learn more [here](https://www.statefarm.com/agent/us/ma).
