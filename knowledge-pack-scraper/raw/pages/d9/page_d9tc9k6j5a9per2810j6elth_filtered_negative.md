Texas law recognizes several different kinds of property, including real property and personal property. It is personal property that is most likely to become the subject of a [San Antonio personal injury claim](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/). Someone might damage your vehicle in a [car accident](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/san-antonio-car-accident-lawyers/), for example.
* $60,000 per accident bodily injury [liability](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/personal-injury-resources/liability/) insurance; and
* [Uninsured motorist](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/san-antonio-car-accident-lawyers/uninsured-motorist-claims/) insurance covers damage that arises while the at-fault party lacks insurance or leaves the scene of the accident without identifying themselves (“hit and run”).
Negligence is a legal term that means something like “carelessness.” Negligence accounts for most property damage claims. To win a [negligence](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/personal-injury-resources/negligence/) claim, you need to prove that:
* You suffered harm. Perhaps “black ice” caused your [truck](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/san-antonio-truck-accident-lawyer/) to slam into the side of a building.
[Product liability](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/san-antonio-product-liability-lawyer/) claims generally arise when someone suffers an injury caused by a defective product. Nevertheless, product liability claims are not limited to claims for bodily injury. You can also seek compensation for the loss of the property itself.
A [chattel](https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/chattel) is a tangible item of personal property. Someone commits trespass to chattels when they intentionally (and without your permission) interfere with your exclusive right to use your property. “Borrowing” your property temporarily is an example of trespass to chattels. You can claim compensation for trespass to chattels even if the defendant acted innocently, believing the property to be their own.
Some property damage claims are trivial. You probably don’t need a lawyer to help you resolve a fender bender claim, for example. The greater the value of your claim, however, the harder the defendant will fight it. The harder the defendant fights, the more you will need a [San Antonio truck accident attorney](https://www.salinastriallaw.com/san-antonio-truck-accident-lawyer/).
