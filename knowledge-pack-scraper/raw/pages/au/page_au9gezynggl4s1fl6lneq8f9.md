##  Types of State Farm car insurance
[State Farm](https://secure.money.com/pr/i4ce986d7c7d) is available nationwide except in Massachusetts and Rhode Island.
### **Basic coverage**
[Full coverage car insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-full-coverage-car-insurance/) is typically required if you are financing or leasing your car.
  * **Liability car insurance:** The[liability ](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-liability-car-insurance/)pays for damages and expenses if you or someone else driving your car is at fault for an accident.
  * **Collision coverage:** Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident.
  * **Comprehensive coverage:** Pays for damages or losses not caused by a collision, including theft, [weather damage](https://www.cnbc.com/select/does-car-insurance-cover-hail-damage/) or hitting an animal.


### **Additional coverage**
These policies are required in some states in addition to full coverage**.**
  * **Personal injury protection (PIP):** Also known as no-fault insurance,
  * **Medical payments:** Also known as MedPay, this covers medical bills for you and any passengers or pedestrians involved in an accident, regardless of fault.
  * **Payoff Protector:** In place of gap insurance, if your car is financed through State Farm Bank, the difference between your policy payout and your outstanding loan balance is canceled.
  * **Uninsured/underinsured motorist:** Covers repairs and medical bills if you are in an accident with a driver with little or no insurance.


##  Add-ons
Optional features that are offered by [State Farm](https://secure.money.com/pr/i4ce986d7c7d)
  * **Accident forgiveness:** Available if you've been accident-free for at least nine years, considerably longer than many insurers require.
  * **Rental car reimbursement:** Covers the cost of a rental while your vehicle is being repaired after a covered accident.
  * **Rideshare insurance:** Expands coverage to any time the rideshare app is on, including when a driver is waiting to match with a rider.
  * **Roadside assistance:** Get help with jump starts, fuel delivery, lockouts, flat tires, towing and winching.
  * **Travel expenses:** Pays up to $500 for food, lodging, transportation and related costs if your car breaks down more than 50 miles from home.
  * **Vehicle protection:** Covers major systems, minor dents and lost keys for newer cars.


##  State Farm car insurance discounts
[State Farm](https://secure.money.com/pr/i4ce986d7c7d) offers numerous discounts. Offerings vary by state and some discounts may not be combined.
  * **Anti-theft:** Lower your rates if your vehicle has an alarm or another security device.
  * **Away at school:** A covered driver under 25 who only uses the vehicle while visiting from school can earn a discount.
  * **Defensive driving course:** Depending on where they live, drivers who complete a driver safety course can qualify for a discount.
  * **Drive Safe & Save app: **Save up to 30% with this usage-based insurance program that tracks your driving habits.
  * **Driver training discount:** Drivers under 21
  * **Good driver:** New customers can earn up to 25% off for avoiding at-fault accidents and violations for three years.
  * **Good student:** Full-time high school and college students can save up to 25% by keeping at least a B average or meeting other academic standards.
  * **Multi-policy:** Bundle your State Farm auto policy with life, home, condo or renters insurance and you could earn up to 25% off.
  * **Multiple vehicle:** Save up to 20% if you insure two or more vehicles through State Farm
  * **Passive restraint:** Cars from 1993 or earlier equipped with airbags or other passive restraints are eligible for up to 40% on medical-related coverage. 
  * **Steer Clear**[](https://www.statefarm.com/insurance/auto/discounts/steer-clear)**app:** Drivers under 25 can earn up to 15% off for maintaining a clean driving record and completing safe-driver tutorials. 
  * **Vehicle safety** : Save up to 40% on medical-related coverage if your vehicle was made in 1994 or later, depending on the repair record for your vehicle's make and model.
