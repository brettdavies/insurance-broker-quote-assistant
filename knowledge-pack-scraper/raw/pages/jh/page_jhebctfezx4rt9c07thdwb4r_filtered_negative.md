## Frequently asked questions
Do I have to use Progressive’s preferred repair shops in Texas?
No. You can generally choose your own licensed repair facility. Preferred networks can speed up digital estimates and parts sourcing, but Texas drivers typically retain shop choice.
How do rental cars work during repairs?
Rental reimbursement is subject to daily and maximum limits on your policy. Book within those limits, coordinate with your shop’s timeline, and ask your adjuster about extensions during parts delays.
Is hail covered under comprehensive?
Hail and wind damage are typically covered under comprehensive (minus your deductible). Paintless dent repair may be used where appropriate; your adjuster and shop will advise.
What if the other driver’s insurer is Progressive?
You can file a third-party claim with Progressive or use your own coverage (if applicable) and let your insurer seek reimbursement. Consider timelines, rental coverage, and deductibles when choosing a path.
When is a car considered a total loss?
When projected repair costs and related factors exceed a threshold relative to the vehicle’s value, the insurer may declare a total loss. Provide service records, options, and comparable listings to support fair value.
