[State minimum car insurance](https://www.carinsurance.com/Articles/minimum-liability-car-insurance-requirements-by-state.aspx) is the minimum level of coverage required by New York law. While it may be sufficient to meet New York’s legal requirements, it won’t necessarily provide adequate protection if you are involved in an accident.
In addition to liability coverage, there are other [types of car insurance coverage](https://www.carinsurance.com/types-of-car-insurance) available in New York, including:
Comprehensive coverage in New York reimburses you for damage to your car caused by events such as theft, vandalism, animal strikes or weather events.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/coverage-definition/comprehensive-insurance.aspx)
Collision coverage pays for damage to your car caused by a collision with another car or object.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/what-is-collision-coverage-insurance)
Personal injury protection or medical payments coverage is optional in New York. It covers medical expenses for you and your passengers if you are injured in a car accident, regardless of who is at fault. [PIP protection](https://www.carinsurance.com/coverage-definition/personal-injury-protection.aspx) also may offer additional benefits, such as reimbursing you for lost wages.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/coverage-definition/medical-coverages.aspx)
Insurance companies in New York are required to offer UM/UIM coverage to drivers as an option. It pays for damages and injuries caused by a driver who does not have enough insurance to cover the costs of an accident.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/uninsured-motorist-coverage.aspx)
Car insurance companies in New York offer roadside assistance coverage as an option if your car breaks down or you need a tow.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/Articles/your-car/roadside-assistance-plans)
In New York, rental reimbursement coverage pays for a rental car while your car is being repaired after an accident.
[Learn More](https://www.carinsurance.com/coverage-definition/rental-car-reimbursement.aspx)
