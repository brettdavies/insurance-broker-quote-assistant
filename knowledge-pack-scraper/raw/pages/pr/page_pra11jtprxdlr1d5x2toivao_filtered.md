## How to Get Cheaper Full Coverage Insurance in California

* Compare quotes from at least three different companies to make sure you're getting the best deal.
* Increase your [deductible](https://wallethub.com/edu/ci/car-insurance-deductible/69574). Higher deductibles lead to lower premiums, but remember to only pick a deductible you'd be able to afford in an emergency.
* Make sure you're getting all the [discounts](https://wallethub.com/edu/ci/car-insurance-discounts/11950) you can qualify for.
* Sign up for [usage-based insurance](https://wallethub.com/edu/ci/usage-based-insurance/14118). Some insurance companies use either a plug-in device or a smartphone app to track you driving and will give you a discount for driving safely.
