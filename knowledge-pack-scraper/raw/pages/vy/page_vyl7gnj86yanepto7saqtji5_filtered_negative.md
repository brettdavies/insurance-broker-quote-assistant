Geico may be the most online-friendly car insurance company. You can report an accident and file a claim on their [Geico Claims Center](https://www.geico.com/claims/). They have multiple-choice selections to get you started, such as “**I am insured with Geico** ” or “**I had an incident with a Geico customer**.”
In some instances, you’ll immediately schedule a damage inspection. This may be as simple as sending Geico photos of the damage to your vehicle. It may involve a physical inspection with a claims adjuster. In some cases, victims can schedule repair services the same day they file.
