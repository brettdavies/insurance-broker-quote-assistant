* California law requires seniors to renew their driver’s licenses **in person every five years**. They can start the renewal online to save time at the DMV.
![Key Things to Know about Senior Driver’s License Renewal in California](https://www.bestonlinetrafficschool.co/senior-drivers-license-renewal/)
Expect to receive a renewal notice by mail about 60 days before your license expires. After that, you must [complete a driver’s license renewal application](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/dl-id-online-app-edl-44/). You can do much of this online.
