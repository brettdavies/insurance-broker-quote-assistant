At the NAIC Summer 2023 National Meeting, the SAP Working Group adopted _INT 23-01T – Net Negative (Disallowed) Interest Maintenance Reserve_, an interpretation of statutory accounting principles that provides optional, limited-term guidance for the admittance of net negative (disallowed) IMR under SSAP No. 7 for up to 10% of adjusted general account capital and surplus. INT 23-01T is effective through December 31, 2025, but may be nullified earlier or extended based on actions by the SAP Working Group to establish specific accounting guidance on net negative (disallowed) IMR to serve as a long-term solution. The SAP Working Group will be reviewing this timeline and assessing this interpretation.
