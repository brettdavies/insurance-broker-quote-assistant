Esurance offers more affordable full-coverage insurance than Progressive. A policy from Esurance costs an average of **$1,372 per year**, or $114 per month, which is $237 per year cheaper than Progressive. It's also less expensive than the [national average](https://www.valuepenguin.com/average-cost-of-insurance "Average Cost of Car Insurance") by $596 per year.

However, drivers with an at-fault accident on their record should consider Progressive. The company offers [accident forgiveness #accident-forgiveness ](https://www.valuepenguin.com/compare-esurance-vs-progressive-car-insurance#accident-forgiveness "#accident-forgiveness") and doesn't raise rates after your first at-fault accident if the payout is under $500. As a result, a full-coverage policy from Progressive costs **$552 less per year** than Esurance.
