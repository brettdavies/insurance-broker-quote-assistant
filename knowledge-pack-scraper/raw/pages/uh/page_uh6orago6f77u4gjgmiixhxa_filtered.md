# Progressive

Personal and Commercial Auto Insurance

IIAT Advantage and Independent Market Solutions have partnered together to provide IIAT members access to Progressive Auto, the largest insurer for Personal Auto in the independent agency channel. And, with the addition of Recreational Lines and Commercial Lines, appointed agencies will be able to serve an even broader range of customers.

**Program Features**

* Comprehensive product offering including Personal Auto, Commercial Auto, Boat, RVs, ATVs, and more
* Online quoting platform
* Policy download
* Broad marketing resources
* Agency ownership of expirations
* Competitive commissions
* *Opportunity to "Graduate" to a direct appointment*

**Enrollment** **Eligibility**

* Remain an IIAT member in good standing
* Maintain E&O policy with $1,000,000 limit
* Have automation capabilities necessary to access the Progressive portal
* Have at least 2-3 years of agency ownership and P&C insurance experience
* Have storefront or retail location (may be waived during COVID-19 period)
* Not previously terminated from Progressive
* Agree to monthly minimum production requirements
* *Requires Progressive approval for subcode appointment*
* *See agency net commissions [HERE](https://www.imsaccess.com/states/texas/)*
