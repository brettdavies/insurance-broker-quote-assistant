## [How do I get Progressive's sign online discount?](https://wallethub.com/answers/ci/progressive-sign-online-discount-2140703893/)

You can get Progressive’s sign online discount by signing your policy documents electronically when you get a quote online or over the phone. Once you choose your coverage, Progressive will email the documents you need to sign electronically.

The Progressive sign online discount is about 9% on average and can be combined with Progressive’s paperless discount for even greater savings. The sign-online discount from Progressive is not available to drivers in California.
