# State Farm Medicare Supplement Review: Good Service, but Rates Vary
![Cate Deventer](https://res.cloudinary.com/value-penguin/image/upload/w_30/employee_photos/Cate_Deventer_VP_V2)
Written by [ Cate Deventer ](https://www.valuepenguin.com/author/cate-deventer "Cate Deventer")
Updated Jul 11, 2025
State Farm Medigap is a good option for Plan G if you live in the Western U.S., but you should compare it to other companies before you buy a plan.

Plan G is the best Medigap plan for most people, unless you became [eligible for Medicare](https://www.valuepenguin.com/medicare-eligibility "Medicare Eligibility: Age 65 and Other Ways to Qualify") before 2020. And State Farm has cheap rates for Plan G in [many states](https://www.valuepenguin.com/state-farm-medicare-supplement-review#by-state "State Farm rates by state"). Plan F has more coverage if you qualify, but State Farm's Plan F is expensive.
