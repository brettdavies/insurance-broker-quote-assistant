As you determine the best option between State Farm and Farmers, it’s essential to [**compare rates**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/), customer service, discounts, and coverage options. Your driving record and ZIP code also matter when choosing between these two auto insurers.
Farmers, on the other hand, has more extensive coverage options, including customized equipment coverage and [**accident forgiveness**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/knowledge/what-is-accident-forgiveness/). But the extra coverage options come at a cost since Farmers’ average rates are nearly double what you can find with State Farm.
Farmers’ [**car insurance discounts**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/discounts/) might offset some of the higher prices, but if you want [**cheaper car insurance rates**](https://insurify.com/car-insurance/cheap-car-insurance/), State Farm is probably a better fit.
