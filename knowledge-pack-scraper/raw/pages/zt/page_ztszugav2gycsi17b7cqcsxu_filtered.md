### Average car insurance rates in Idaho by driver age

It's a common phenomenon: as you age, your car insurance prices shift. Generally, auto insurance is more expensive for young drivers, as insurers view inexperienced drivers as riskier to insure.

In Idaho, auto insurance runs $5,816 annually for a [16-year-old motorist](/auto-insurance/driver/age/car-insurance-16-year-olds/), compared to $875 per year [for a driver in his or her 50s](/auto-insurance/driver/age/car-insurance-50-year-olds/).

One of the pros of getting hitched — aside from the wedding gifts — is the better deal you typically receive on your car insurance policy. In Idaho, married drivers save $59 each year on auto insurance policies. This is less than the national average post-wedding price cut of $76.

Car insurance underwriters utilize credit score to gauge how reliable a customer will be. Historical trends show motorists who carry superior credit scores are usually more dependable car insurance customers: they are less likely to file claims that insurance companies must repay.

### Typical car insurance prices for bad drivers in Idaho

If you are defined as at fault in a car crash or found guilty of an on-road violation, your auto insurance costs are bound to rise. The more violations you accumulate, the more costly your auto insurance becomes.

In Idaho, your first minor at-fault accident results in a penalty of $465 annually in car insurance premiums. A [DUI or DWI](/auto-insurance/tickets-violations/car-insurance-after-dui/) — considered one of the most severe vehicular offenses — may increase your yearly insurance rates in [Idaho](/auto-insurance/idaho-car-insurance/) by an average of $547.

### Average auto insurance costs by coverage level in Idaho

The amount you pay each month for auto insurance is dependent on the level of protection you purchase. A liability-only policy normally costs less, while low-deductible comprehensive coverage usually costs more. In Idaho, the premium gap between liability-only coverage and [comprehensive coverage with a $500 deductible](/auto-insurance/coverage/best-car-insurance-500-deductible/) is $567.
