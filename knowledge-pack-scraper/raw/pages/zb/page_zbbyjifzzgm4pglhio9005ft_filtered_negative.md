Have you ever wondered if adding uninsured motorist coverage to your [car insurance in California](https://www.costulessdirect.com/car-insurance-quotes-online/) is worth it? California’s highways connect you to opportunity, adventure, and—unfortunately— _risk_. You might be the most cautious driver around, but you share the road with countless others whose choices could impact your safety and your wallet.
Let’s set the scene: you’re stopped at a red light, radio humming, when another car crashes into your car. The driver steps out, sheepish and apologetic, but admits they are an[ ](https://www.costulessdirect.com/blog/what-to-do-if-an-uninsured-driver-hits-you-in-ca/)[uninsured driver](https://www.costulessdirect.com/blog/what-to-do-if-an-uninsured-driver-hits-you-in-ca/). Suddenly, your world tilts. Who’s going to pay for your medical bills, your car repairs, and your time off work?
Uninsured motorist insurance in California is designed for these very moments. When the at-fault driver has no insurance or disappears after a[ ](https://www.costulessdirect.com/blog/hit-and-run-accident-california/)[hit-and-run accident](https://www.costulessdirect.com/blog/hit-and-run-accident-california/), this coverage steps in as your financial shield.
