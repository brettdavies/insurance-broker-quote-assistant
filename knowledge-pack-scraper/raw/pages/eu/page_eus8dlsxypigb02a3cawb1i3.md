# Progressive Homeowners Insurance: Protection with Flexible Coverage Options
![Progressive Homeowners Insurance: Protection with Flexible Coverage Options](https://us.pixin.com.br/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Progressive-Homeowners-Insurance.webp)
August 14, 2025  • By [ Mayara Marques  ](https://us.pixin.com.br/author/mayara/ "View all posts by Mayara Marques")
  

Progressive Homeowners Insurance is a solution offered by one of the largest insurance companies in the United States. Known for its strong presence in the auto insurance sector, the company also provides coverage for homeowners through customizable policies, combining broad protection with competitive rates and access to a network of partner insurers.
Designed for those who want to protect their home against damage, loss, and liability, Progressive’s homeowners insurance can be tailored to different types of properties and customer profiles, while maintaining the brand’s practicality and reliability. Discover everything it can offer for your home and family!
## Main Coverage Options
Progressive Homeowners Insurance offers protection against a variety of risks, which may include:
Dwelling coverage: Covers repair or rebuilding costs if the home is damaged by fire, storms, falling objects, and other covered events.
Other structures: Protection for buildings not directly attached to the home, such as fences, sheds, and detached garages.
  

Personal property: Covers furniture, appliances, clothing, and other belongings against theft or damage.
Loss of use: Helps pay for temporary housing expenses if the home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered event.
Personal liability: Protects against legal costs if someone is injured or their property is damaged while on your premises.
Medical payments to others: Covers medical expenses for guests injured in your home, regardless of who was at fault.
Additional coverage options are also available, such as flood and earthquake insurance, protection for high-value items, and security system upgrades.
## What Are the Benefits of This Insurance?
Progressive Homeowners Insurance not only offers a wide range of coverage options for homeowners, but also provides competitive advantages:
  * Customization: Ability to adjust coverage and limits based on the home’s value and the customer’s needs.
  * Discounts: Save money by bundling homeowners insurance with other Progressive policies, such as auto insurance.
  * Quick online quotes: Get a quote and purchase coverage digitally, with phone support available.
  * Partner insurer network: Access to more pricing and coverage options.
  * 24/7 claims service: Continuous support for emergencies and repairs.


Another appealing point is the transparency in the purchasing process, allowing customers to clearly see the costs and benefits of each coverage option.
## Rates and Terms
It’s also worth considering the conditions and costs of **[Progressive](https://us.pixin.com.br/progressive-car-insurance-special-discounts-just-for-you/) **Homeowners Insurance. The price of the policy depends on:
  * Location and value of the home
  * Type of construction and year built
  * Installed security features
  * Customer’s claims history
  * Additional coverage selected


On average, annual premiums range between $1,200 and $2,800, but these amounts vary based on profile and location. That’s why it’s important to request a personalized quote from a licensed agent. Progressive also offers discounts for customers who have:
  * Installed alarm and security systems
  * A claims-free history
  * Bundled coverage with auto insurance
  * Set up automatic or annual payments


These factors can make the policy even more affordable when it comes to rates and final costs.
## Who Is It For?
Progressive Homeowners Insurance is designed to serve a variety of homeowners who want to **[protect their property](https://us.pixin.com.br/american-family-homeowners-insurance-complete-personalized-protection-p1/)**. Generally, you must be the legal owner of the residence, but it’s ideal for anyone seeking flexible coverage that can be tailored to the value and type of home.
Additionally, customers who already have or plan to get auto insurance with Progressive can take advantage of exclusive discounts. Those who prefer online quotes will also appreciate the insurer’s process, which combines competitive pricing and a strong brand with the convenience of digital service.
## How to Apply for This Insurance
If you’re interested in Progressive Homeowners Insurance, the process to get a quote is simple:
  1. Visit Progressive’s official website and select “Homeowners Insurance.”
  2. Provide information about the property, location, and usage history.
  3. Choose your desired coverage and limits.
  4. Receive your personalized quote and compare options.
  5. Complete your purchase online or by phone.
  6. Receive your digital policy and get access to 24/7 customer service.


Even with the digital process, the company offers agents who can assist at any time during the quote or final purchase.
## Is Progressive Homeowners Insurance Worth It?
For those seeking flexibility, competitive pricing, and an easy application process, Progressive Homeowners Insurance is an appealing choice. It allows for personalized coverage, offers multi-policy discounts, and features a straightforward digital process with 24-hour support.
By working with partner insurers, it helps customers find the best prices, even when adding extra coverage. This makes it a strong option for anyone who wants to protect their property with a policy tailored to their needs, without giving up convenience.
