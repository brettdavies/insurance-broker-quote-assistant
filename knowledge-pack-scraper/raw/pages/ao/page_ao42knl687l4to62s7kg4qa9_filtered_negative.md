[SR-22 insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/sr-22-auto-insurance "SR22 Auto Insurance") in California is often required if you have a record of high-risk driving and your license has been suspended.
The length of time you'll need to have SR-22 [insurance in California](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-cheap-car-insurance-california "Cheap California Car Insurance") depends on your conviction, which should state how long you're expected to have the SR-22 filing.
SR-22 insurance is often called [DUI insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-dui-car-insurance "Best Cheap DUI Insurance") because you'll probably have to file an SR-22 to reinstate your driving privileges if you have a DUI conviction in California.
In California, there are three forms of financial responsibility: an owner's policy certificate, a broad coverage policy certificate and an operator's policy certificate, also called a [non-owner](https://www.valuepenguin.com/non-owner-car-insurance-who-should-have-it "Non Owners Car Insurance") SR-22 policy.
