Written by [State Farm® Editorial Team ](https://www.statefarm.com/simple-insights/editorial-team-and-process)
Determine how much those savings can accrue over the years.

Once the calculation is complete, you will see how making the noted changes can make a difference on a monthly basis. This can be helpful information if you are looking to [build a budget](https://www.statefarm.com/simple-insights/financial/budget-breakdown "Budget breakdown") and more importantly this savings calculator can help you see what spending less could mean for your retirement savings goals.
