    Founded in 1936, GEICO is one of the top insurance providers in the country. In addition to home, auto, liability, and renters insurance, GEICO also provides pet insurance through Embrace pet insurance, which has been specializing in comprehensive pet health coverage since 2003.

    GEICO Pet Insurance Overview

    GEICO is a well-known name in the insurance industry. However, GEICO doesn't provide its own pet insurance, instead using its name to direct pet owners to Embrace, a pet insurance provider.

    The policies that GEICO Pet Insurance offers really stand out for its breadth of coverage offered. The accident and illness policy offers coverages that most competitors don't provide, like behavioral therapy, dental services, and prosthetic and mobility devices.

    Additionally, it's wellness plan is one of the most comprehensive, offering coverage for grooming, nutritional supplements, and even training. This breadth of coverage is very uncommon among wellness plans in the pet insurance industry.

    Signing up and submitting claims with Embrace is relatively quick and easy through its mobile app, which is a huge plus if you prefer to manage things online.

    However, GEICO Pet Insurance, which is provided through Embrace, may not be the best fit if you're looking to enroll pets that are over 14 years old. Pets 15 and older can only be enrolled in an accident-only plan. Another downside of GEICO Pet Insurance is that its expensive compared to other top pet insurance companies, especially for cat owners.

    GEICO Pet Insurance Plans and Coverage

    GEICO does not underwrite the pet policies it sells. Instead, it partners with Embrace to cover your cat or dog in case of an unforeseen injury or illness. If you click a link to get a rate quote on GEICO's website, you'll be redirected to Embrace to finish the rest of the quote process. 

    GEICO currently offers two pet insurance plans in partnership with Embrace: an accident and illness plan and an accident-only plan. 

    GEICO Accident & Illness Plan

    If your pet is between 6 weeks and 14 years old, you may qualify for GEICO's most popular pet insurance policy: the accident and illness plan. You can customize your policy with annual reimbursement limits of $5,000, $8,000, $10,000, $15,000, and unlimited. You can also select an annual deductible of $100, $250, $500, $750, or $1,000, and choose a reimbursement percentage of 70%, 80%, or 90%.

    What this plan covers: 

    • Congenital and genetic conditions like allergies or hip dysplasia 
    • Common illnesses like ear infections, vomiting, and diarrhea
    • Chronic conditions such as diabetes
    • Dental trauma
    • Cancer treatment
    • Hospitalization and emergency care
    • Lab tests and biopsies 
    • Physical therapy 
    • Diagnostic testing like MRIs, X-rays and ultrasounds
    • Alternative therapies
    • Orthopedic conditions
    • Specialist care such as oncologists and behaviorists
    • Surgeries, including anesthesia and rehabilitation

    What this plan doesn't cover:

    • Preexisting conditions (though curable conditions may be covered)
    • Routine or preventative care
    • Injury or illness resulting from neglect, cruelty, or fighting
    • Breeding 
    • Spay/neuter

    GEICO Accident-Only Plan

    The accident and illness plan isn't available to pets aged 15 years and older, but the accident-only plan is. Embrace's accident-only plan is non-customizable and comes with a $100 annual deductible, a 90% reimbursement, and a $5,000 annual coverage limit. 

    What this plan covers:

    • Bloat
    • Getting hit by a car
    • Foreign body ingestion
    • Torn cruciate ligament
    • Poisoning
    • Cuts and  lacerations
    • Prescription drugs for covered conditions

    What this plan does not cover:

    • Preexisting conditions
    • Chronic Conditions
    • Illnesses
    • Breed-specific and genetic conditions
    • Cancer
    • Congenital Conditions
    • Dental Illness

    • Wellness Rewards Program. The Wellness Rewards Program reimburses the cost of many preventative care services, like grooming, spay/neuter surgery, and dental cleanings, up to an annual limit of $300, $500, or $700. Here are more details on what it comes:

    How to Purchase and Manage a GEICO Pet Insurance Policy

    Since GEICO partners with Embrace Pet Insurance, you'll be directed to Embrace's website to get a quote, select a plan, and complete your purchase. Embrace will ask you to provide details about your pet, such as breed, age, sex, and species, to generate a personalized quote. And if you insure more than one dog or cat, Embrace offers a 10% multi-pet discount. 

    After buying your policy, you can manage it through the MyEmbrace customer portal or the Embrace Pet Insurance app. These platforms let you easily submit and view claims, add pets and change coverage, download policy documents, and reach out to the customer service team. The Embrace Pet Insurance mobile app is available on both the App Store and Google Play. 

    Note that Embrace's pet insurance policies have a 14-day waiting period for illnesses. Waiting periods for orthopedic conditions only pertain to dogs and vary by state. If your pet experiences any conditions during this waiting period, it'll be considered preexisting and will not be covered by the policy. 

    Pet owners who purchase Embrace have a 30-day free-look period in which they can cancel their policy and receive a full refund as long as they haven't filed a claim. After this period, pet owners who cancel their policy will receive a pro-rated refund based on when they canceled their policy within the pay period.

    How to file a claim with GEICO Pet Insurance

    The fastest way to file a pet insurance claim is by submitting all the necessary information through your MyEmbrace customer account online or in the Embrace Pet Insurance app.

    When you log into your account, you'll be asked to upload your invoice and select the diagnosis or reason for the vet visit. Once you've done that, Embrace will process your claim in 10 to 15 business days. You can choose to have your covered claims paid by check or with direct deposit. 

    Other ways to file a claim with Embrace include:

    • Email: Send your completed claim form to claims@embracepetinsurance.com
    • Fax: Send to (800) 238-1042
    • Mail: Send to: Embrace Pet Insurance, PO Box 22188, Beachwood, OH 44122-0188

    GEICO Pet Insurance Customer Reviews and Ratings

    Since GEICO Pet Insurance is administered by Embrace, we reviewed third-party sites to gauge how the company handles customer support. Embrace has an average rating of 4 out of five stars on Trustpilot and a 2.3 on its Better Business Bureau page.

    Many customers appreciated how easy and quick it is to submit claims with Embrace. However, we also noticed recurring complaints about unresponsive customer service, including unanswered emails and phone calls. Additionally, some customers reported having to submit medical records multiple times and experiencing long delays in receiving reimbursements.

    Let's look at some third-party rating of Embrace.

    The National Association of Insurance Commissioners' complaint index rates insurance companies based on how many complaints they receive against its share of the market. A 1.00 means that a company has an expected number of complaints based on its size. A lower score indicates fewer complaints than expected and a higher number indicates more complaints than expected.

    Embrace's pet insurance plans are underwritten by American Modern Home Insurance Company, which scored 4.14, indicating that it has over four times the number of complaints than expected given its share of the market.

    American Modern Home Insurance Company has an A+ (Superior) Financial Strength Rating from AM Best, indicating that it has the ability to meet its financial obligations, like paying out claims.

    GEICO Pet Insurance Alternatives

    See how GEICO compares to these alternative options for pet insurance coverage: Lemonade and Spot. 

    GEICO Pet Insurance vs. Lemonade Pet Insurance

    Lemonade Pet Insurance is known for its cheap premiums and customizable service. It's specifically far cheaper than GEICO. 

    That said, Lemonade's base policy is pretty bare, lacking some of the features found in GEICO's plan, like dental illness coverage and behavioral treatments. However, you can add these to your Lemonade plan for extra. Even with these add-ons, Lemonade policies still come out cheaper than GEICO.

    Additionally, Lemonade offers several opportunities to bundle: 10% off if you bundle with its renters, homeowners, auto, condo, or co-op insurance; 5% if you pay annually instead of monthly; and 5% for enrolling multiple pets. So if you prefer to manage all your insurance policies in one place, Lemonade can be an option worth looking into. 

    However, Lemonade Pet Insurance is only available in 37 states and Washington, DC, whereas GEICO is available in all 50 states.

    Read our Lemonade pet insurance review.

    GEICO vs. Spot Pet Insurance 

    If your pet is 15 years or older, Spot Pet Insurance might be a better choice for you. GEICO Pet Insurance has an age limit of 14 years for enrollment in its accident and illness plan, whereas Spot plans have no upper age limits for new enrollments. As long as your pet is at least eight weeks old, you're welcome to apply for coverage. 

    Another reason you may want to consider Spot is that they cover microchip implantation in all their core plans, while Embrace only covers it in its Wellness Rewards Program. 

    However, if your pet's dental care is a major concern for you, Embrace may be more suitable. While Embrace and Spot both offer dental coverage, Embrace's dental illness coverage is some of the best you'll find, covering issues like gingivitis, root canals, and periodontal disease. On the other hand, Spot's dental coverage is an accident-only plan and is not as comprehensive.

    FAQs

    How do I cancel my GEICO pet insurance?

    To cancel GEICO pet insurance, email a cancellation request to hello@embracepetinsurance.com or call (800) 511-9172. If you cancel within 30 days of your purchase, you can receive a full refund.

    Does GEICO pet insurance own Embrace pet insurance?

    No, GEICO pet insurance does not own Embrace. GEICO simply partners with Embrace to offer accident and illness as well as accident-only pet insurance policies.

    Does GEICO pet insurance cover preexisting conditions?

    While GEICO doesn't cover incurable preexisting conditions, curable conditions are eligible for coverage after your pet is symptom- and treatment-free for 12 months.

    Does GEICO pet insurance have waiting periods?

    Yes, GEICO pet insurance has a 14-day waiting period for illnesses. It does not have a waiting period for accidents.

    Does GEICO pet insurance cover preventive care?

    GEICO pet insurance does not cover preventive care in its base policy, but pet owners can add a preventive care package to their coverage.
