Chaos in Texas Today! Brutal Large Hail Storm Destroy Homes, Cars in Houston
Severe weather hit Southeast Texas again Monday. Storms dropped hail across the region, from Brenham to Pearland and Rosharon. As skies cleared, many of you saw photos and videos of the hail the storm brought. on Monday, officials in Texas are working to locate a child who was swept away in "rapidly rising floodwaters." The Brenham Fire Department said the child, described as a "school-aged" girl, was swept away in the flooding at approximately 4:25 p.m. The fire department says bystanders and police attempted a rescue, but those efforts were not successful. In an interview with loal media Houston, officials said the girl was walking home from school with her sister when the adjacent creek rose rapidly, pulling her into the swift-moving water. Meanwhile, Lightning ignited a house fire in Colleyville during Monday afternoon’s storms in North Texas. The fire broke out around 2 p.m. in the 2500 block of Twelve Oaks Lane. Security video from a nearby home captured the moment the house was struck by lightning, just after thunder was heard in the area.
