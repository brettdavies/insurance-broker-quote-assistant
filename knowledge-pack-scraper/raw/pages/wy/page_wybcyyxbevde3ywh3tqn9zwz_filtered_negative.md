##  Common NAICS Questions
[ l Can a Business Have More than One NAICS Code?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
**Yes, some agencies assign more than one NAICS codes to one establishment.** For instance, the SAM (System for Award Management _formerly CCR_), where businesses register to become federal contractors, will accept up to 5 or 10 classification codes per establishment. You will need to contact the other agencies to find out what their policies are. For access to a list of federal government agencies, [click here](https://www.firstgov.gov/Agencies/Federal/All_Agencies/index.shtml).
[ l Can I get a NAICS Code or Other Statistical Data for a Specific Company?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)

[ l Can I look up a NAICS Code by Company Name?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
Yes. By using the [US Company Lookup Tool](https://www.naics.com/company-lookup-tool/), you can purchase a company record that includes NAICS and SIC Information, DUNS Numbers, Company Size Information, Addresses, Phone Numbers, Contact Names, Lat/Long and More.
[ l Can I Use My NAICS Code to Determine my SIC Code?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
Yes! Just search for the NAICS code in our NAICS search tool ([https://www.naics.com/search/](https://www.naics.com/search/_wp_link_placeholder)), then click on the “View SIC” Button next to the NAICS code you are interested in and you will see which SIC(s) relate to that NAICS code. Then select the SIC code that best reflects your line of revenue producing activity.

[ l How does NAICS 2012 Differ from NAICS 2007?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
1.     1. Sector 22, Utilities – 2007 NAICS code 221119, Other Electric Power Generation, was deleted and portions of it were reclassified, resulting in the addition of five new 6-digit industries: 221114, Solar Electric Power Generation; 221115, Wind Electric Power Generation; 221116, Geothermal Electric Power Generation; 221117, Biomass Electric Power Generation; 221118, Other Electric Power Generation.
[ l How Does NAICS 2017 Differ from NAICS 2012?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
#### Highlights of NAICS Changes Include:
The 2012 NAICS Code for Lead Ore and Zinc Ore Mining (2012 NAICS Code 212231) is expanding to cover Nickel Ore and Lead Ore Mining (2012 NAICS Code 212234) in one inclusive 2017 NAICS Code: 212230.
The 2012 NAICS Code 541711 will no longer encompass “Research and Development in Biotechnology (except Nanobiotechnology)”. Also, the 2012 NAICS Code 541712 will no longer encompass “Research and Development in the Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences (except Nanotechnology and Biotechnology)”. Instead, these will each be classified under the 2017 NAICS Code 541715, “Research and Development in the Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences (except Nanotechnology and Biotechnology)”

[ l What is the relationship between NAICS and the Small Business Administration’s (SBA) size standards?  ](https://www.naics.com/search/)
Category: [Common NAICS Questions](https://www.naics.com/search/?include_category=naics)
NAICS categories do not distinguish between small and large business, or between for-profit and non-profit. The Small Business Administration (SBA) develops size standards for each NAICS category. To find more information about the SBA size standards, or when the SBA will update their size standards to reflect 2022 NAICS revisions, visit the SBA Web site at [www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-guide/size-standards](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-guide/size-standards). You may also contact SBA’s Office of Size Standards Answer Desk on [800-827-5722](tel:8008275722) or via email to answerdesk@sba.gov. [SOURCE: US NAICS MANUAL]
