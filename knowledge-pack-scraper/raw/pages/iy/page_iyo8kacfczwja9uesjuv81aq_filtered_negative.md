# Does State Farm Have Accident Forgiveness?
![](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Kayda-Norman-150x150.png)
Written by Kayda Norman and 1 other
Updated Jan 30, 2025
Written by
Authors
[![](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Kayda-Norman-150x150.png) Kayda Norman Writer and Editor ](https://jerry.ai/author/kayda-norman/)
Editors
[![](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Giselle-Gomez-150x150.png) Giselle Gomez Writer and Editor ](https://jerry.ai/author/giselle-gomez/)
State Farm doesn’t offer accident forgiveness — but that doesn’t mean that you can’t keep your rates low after an accident. Here’s how.
![Two black vehicles in a rear end accident](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Depositphotos_115372202_XL-scaled.jpg)
Auto insurance companies offer accident forgiveness as a way to avoid a rate increase after an accident, but not State Farm. In fact, [State Farm](https://jerry.ai/reviews/car-insurance/state-farm/) is one of the only major insurance companies that doesn’t offer any form of accident forgiveness to its policyholders.
Instead, State Farm customers can reduce the cost of [car insurance](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/) coverage with safe driving discounts based on real-time data or their long-term driving record.
Because State Farm doesn’t offer accident forgiveness, if you do get into an accident, you can expect your insurance rate to rise. This is especially true with an [at-fault accident](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/at-fault-accident/).
State Farm may not have [accident forgiveness](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/accident-forgiveness/), but many competitors — such as [Allstate](https://jerry.ai/reviews/car-insurance/allstate/), [Farmers](https://jerry.ai/reviews/car-insurance/farmers/) and [Geico](https://jerry.ai/reviews/car-insurance/geico/) — do. In some cases, this perk is an optional add-on. In other cases, it’s added to your policy automatically once you reach the eligibility threshold (typically three to five years with no accidents or insurance claims).
If you’re looking for an insurance provider that offers accident forgiveness, compare at least three [car insurance quotes](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/car-insurance-quotes/) from different companies before settling on a policy.
For instance, check if your policy includes [full coverage](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/what-does-full-coverage-car-insurance-cover/), which generally consists of comprehensive, collision, and any state-mandated coverage, such as liability insurance. If you have full coverage insurance, you may be able to drop some coverage or opt for a lower limit. Just be sure you’re meeting [your state’s requirements](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/is-car-insurance-required/) for bodily injury and property damage liability, along with any other required coverage such as uninsured motorist coverage.
If you have full coverage, raising the [deductible](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/car-insurance-deductible/) on your collision or [comprehensive insurance](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/comprehensive-insurance/) can temporarily reduce your policy’s monthly cost.
* **Drive Safe and Save:** State Farm’s usage-based [telematics](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/what-is-telematics/) program allows insurance agencies to track customers’ driving habits to better personalize rates
State Farm also offers loyalty discounts to customers who combine products, such as [bundling home and auto insurance](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/car-and-home-insurance/), or those who insure multiple vehicles with State Farm.
