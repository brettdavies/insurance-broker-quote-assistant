* California [increased ](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=202120220SB1107)their minimum car insurance requirements from 15/30/5 to 30/60/15 on January 1, 2025.
* North Carolina [increased ](https://lrs.sog.unc.edu/bill-summaries-lookup/S/452/2023-2024%20Session/S452#:~:text=October%201%2C%202023.-,Part%20XIII.,-Amends%20the%20proof)their minimum car insurance requirements from 30/60/25 to 50/100/50.
* Utah [increased ](https://le.utah.gov/~2023/bills/static/HB0113.html)their minimum car insurance requirements from 25/65/15 to 30/65/25 on January 1, 2025.
* Virginia [increased ](https://legacylis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+sum+SB1182)their minimum car insurance requirements from 30/60/20 to 50/100/25 on January 1, 2025.
