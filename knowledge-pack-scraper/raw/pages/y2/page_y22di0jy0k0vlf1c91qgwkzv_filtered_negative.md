## State Farm vs. Progressive: Coverage Options
State Farm ranks No. 2 and Progressive ranks No. 5 in our study of the[ best renters insurance companies](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/renters-insurance). While Progressive doesn't rank among the[ cheapest renters insurance companies](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/renters-insurance/cheapest-renters-insurance-companies) in our study, State Farm takes the top spot.
According to our study, State Farm's average monthly premium is $13.04, whereas Progressive's is significantly higher, at $22.69. Both insurers offer a multi-policy discount, but State Farm also provides a home alert protection discount and a claims-free discount. On the other hand, Progressive offers discounts for advanced quotes, going paperless, paying in full, and living in a secure/gated community.
Both insurers offer standard renters insurance policies that include[ coverage](https://www.usnews.com/insurance/renters-insurance/what-does-renters-insurance-cover) for personal property, liability, additional living expenses, and guest medical expenses.
Additionally, State Farm offers optional coverages for cyber events, identity restoration, fraud loss, sewer or drain backup coverage, and earthquake insurance. Progressive offers various optional coverages, including water backup, personal injury, and an insurance rider for those who need more coverage for high-value items.
State Farm is available in 47 states and Washington, D.C., excluding California, Massachusetts, and Rhode Island. Progressive is available nationwide, including Washington, D.C. Both insurers offer online quotes. However, you may need to work with a State Farm agent to finalize your policy purchase.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Bedbugs?
Neither company covers bedbug damage. Most insurance companies don't cover bedbugs or pest infestations because they are considered preventable through standard home maintenance.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Water Damage?
State Farm and Progressive cover sudden and accidental water damage caused by an internal source, such as a broken appliance, burst or frozen plumbing, or water seepage from neighboring units. However, water damage caused by flooding isn't covered.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Flood Damage?
Renters insurance typically does not cover flood damage, and neither does State Farm nor Progressive. Flooding is a natural disaster caused by outside or underground sources.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Mold Damage?
Neither company covers mold damage unless it's caused by a covered peril, such as a burst pipe. Mold damage is considered preventable with standard home maintenance.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Property Damage?
Both insurers cover damage or loss of your personal property up to your policy limits for a covered peril. Personal liability is also included if you accidentally damage someone else's property.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Fire Damage?
State Farm and Progressive cover fire, including the loss or damage of personal property due to fire or smoke. Also, if you must temporarily relocate due to a fire, the additional expenses portion of your policy will help provide coverage.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Car Theft?
Similar to most renters insurance companies, State Farm and Progressive don't cover car theft, even if it happens on the rental property. However, if personal belongings are stolen from your vehicle, both insurers' renters insurance policies will help cover them.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Moving Costs?
Neither State Farm nor Progressive covers moving costs. However, if you're temporarily displaced from your home due to a covered peril, the additional expenses portion of your policy may help cover moving costs.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Cover Temporary Housing?
State Farm and Progressive both cover temporary housing costs if you're forced to relocate from your home due to a covered peril. The additional living expenses portion of your renters insurance policy helps pay for costs that exceed typical living expenses, such as hotel and restaurant bills.
### Do State Farm and Progressive Offer Renters Insurance Bundling?
Both State Farm and Progressive offer insurance bundling with your renters policy. Insurance bundling, also known as a multi-policy discount, helps consumers save on their premiums.

## Bottom Line
State Farm and Progressive are both good choices for renters insurance. Both companies offer optional coverages and discounts. They are also widely available and offer online quotes.
Both companies rank well in our study of the best renters insurance companies. State Farm has cheaper premiums than Progressive, but the best way to determine your rate is to get a quote from both companies.
