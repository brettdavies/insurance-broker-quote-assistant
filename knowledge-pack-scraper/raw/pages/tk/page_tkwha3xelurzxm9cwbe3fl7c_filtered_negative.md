In the past five years, Florida has experienced several severe hurricanes, including Idalia, Helene, and Milton.[[1]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-1) These storms caused billions of dollars in damage and emphasize the importance of maintaining the right insurance for your home.
In Florida, standard [**home insurance**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/) policies include hurricane coverage rather than it being a separate policy. But Florida residential policies have a separate deductible for losses due to hurricanes.[[2]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-2) Homeowners may also need to purchase separate flood insurance to ensure they have coverage for flooding from hurricanes.
Here’s what [**hurricane insurance**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/hurricane-insurance/) in Florida covers, what it excludes, and what you need to know to protect your home.

* Hurricane deductibles range from 1% to 15% of a home’s insured value.[[4]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-4)
[**Florida**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida/) is highly susceptible to hurricanes. Numerous homeowners across the state have experienced significant losses in recent years, causing many insurers to leave the market.
* **Homeowners insurance:** Your homeowners insurance policy pays to repair your home and replace damaged belongings after a hurricane.[[5]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-5)
* **Wind endorsement:** Florida law requires insurance companies to pay for windstorm damage.[[6]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-6) But there are exceptions. Homeowners living in high-risk or coastal areas need to purchase a separate [**windstorm policy**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-windstorm-insurance/) or add a windstorm endorsement to their home insurance.
* **Flood insurance:** A[**flood insurance policy**](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/flood-insurance/) is a separate form of coverage. It pays for damage related to rising water, flooding, or storm surges.[[7]](https://insurify.com/homeowners-insurance/florida-hurricane-insurance/#cite-7)
