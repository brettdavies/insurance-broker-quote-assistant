## The Need for SR-22 Auto Insurance
[What is SR-22 insurance?](https://www.AutoInsuranceEZ.com/blog/sr-22-insurance/) Whether you require an SR-22 hinges on your driving history, with insurance rates typically elevated for individuals with a poor driving record and often necessitating SR-22 certification.
Drivers who have too many accidents or severe traffic violations (DUI, reckless driving, or racing) may require an SR-22 form before their driving privileges are restored.
SR-22 certification is like a promise to your state that says you’ll carry the state minimum car insurance. Your state could suspend or revoke your driver’s license for failing to meet state requirements for auto insurance.
If you’re a high-risk driver in Indiana, you’re required to get SR-50 certification. Meanwhile, Florida and Virginia’s high-risk drivers who commit serious driving offenses need double the minimum requirements through FR-44 certification.
