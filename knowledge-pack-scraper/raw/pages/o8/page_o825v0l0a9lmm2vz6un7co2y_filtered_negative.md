#  Is It Illegal to Drive Without Insurance in California? (Insurance Laws Explained)
Standard auto insurance quotes must include uninsured motorist coverage, and drivers who don’t want to purchase it must opt out in writing. The limits of uninsured motorist coverage are usually the same as the liability limits – except uninsured motorist property damage coverage is [limited to $3,500](https://www.insurance.ca.gov/01-consumers/105-type/95-guides/01-auto/auto101.cfm#:~:text=Uninsured%20motorist%20property%20damage%20\(UMPD,The%20limit%20is%20$3%2C500.).
