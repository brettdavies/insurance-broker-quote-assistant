## Average Cost of Home Insurance in Iowa by Coverage Amount

In Iowa, the average annual homeowners insurance premium for a policy with $250K in dwelling coverage, $125K in personal property coverage, and $200K in liability coverage is $1,828 — lower than the national average of $2,423.

When deciding [how much coverage you need](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/how-much-home-insurance-do-you-need/), make sure your policy can fully rebuild your home and replace your belongings after a loss. For example, a policy with $100K in dwelling coverage costs about $1,080 per year, while increasing that to $500K raises the average premium to $2,918.

## Average Cost of Home Insurance in Iowa by Claims History

Iowa homeowners who haven't filed claims in the past five years can expect an average annual premium of $1,828 for their insurance. If you filed one claim within the last five years, the average premium rises to $2,192 — an increase of $364 from the claim-free baseline.

Those with two claims within the same period pay an average annual premium of $2,445 — $617 more than homeowners with no claims. Your claims history substantially affects insurance costs.

## FAQ: Homeowners Insurance in Iowa

### On average, what is the cheapest insurer in Iowa?

In Iowa, Auto-Owners Insurance offers the lowest average premium at $1,177 per year. For more options, explore [cheap national providers](https:/www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/cheap-homeowners-insurance/).

### Are tornadoes in Iowa covered by homeowners insurance?

Tornadoes, a common natural disaster in Iowa, are usually covered by homeowners insurance, along with other [specific coverages and exclusions](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/homeowners/does-homeowners-insurance-cover-natural-disasters/).

### What is the biggest factor that impacts the price of homeowners insurance in Iowa?

Credit rating is the biggest factor impacting homeowners insurance in Iowa, with a difference of $7,409 between excellent and poor credit.

### In Iowa, how much, on average, can I save bundling my homeowners insurance with auto?

On average, Iowans can save around 15% per year by [bundling homeowners and auto insurance](https://www.moneygeek.com/insurance/auto/best-home-auto-bundle-in-iowa/).

### Are floods in Iowa covered by homeowners insurance?

Flood insurance isn't included in a standard home policy. You can purchase it through the National Flood Insurance Program or a private flood insurer, and some home insurers offer it as an endorsement.
