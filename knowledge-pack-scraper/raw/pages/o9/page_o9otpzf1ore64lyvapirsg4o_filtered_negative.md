1. **Policy number**: This is your unique policy number, which you’ll need when [filing a claim](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/accidents/car-insurance-claims/).
4. **Vehicle information:** The insured car's make, model, year and VIN are listed here.
5. **Coverages, limits and deductibles:** Lists [bodily injury](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/insurance-guide/bodily-injury-liability/) and [property damage liability ](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/insurance-guide/property-damage-liability/)(required), as well as any elected coverages such as [comprehensive, collision](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/insurance-guide/comprehensive-vs-collision/), etc. The limits list the maximum amount that each coverage will pay as well as any required [deductible](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/coverage/car-insurance-deductibles/) you have to pay out of pocket.
