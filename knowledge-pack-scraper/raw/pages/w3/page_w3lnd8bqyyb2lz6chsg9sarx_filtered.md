## What a Defensive Driving Course Teaches You

Defensive driving courses improve awareness, judgment, and reaction time, teaching proactive techniques to reduce accidents and promote safer driving.

* **Hazard Recognition**: You’ll learn to spot and anticipate dangers like erratic drivers, pedestrians, bad weather, and sudden traffic changes, helping you react quickly and avoid collisions.
* **Safe Following Distances**: Courses teach safe following distances and speed adjustments to prevent rear-end collisions, important when considering [collision vs. comprehensive auto insurance](https://www.quote.com/auto-insurance/collision-vs-comprehensive-auto-insurance/).
* **Managing Distractions and Impairments**: Defensive driving courses highlight the dangers of distractions and explain how fatigue, alcohol, and medications impair reaction time and judgment.
* **Traffic Laws and Safe Driving Best Practices**: You’ll review current traffic laws, including state-specific updates, and reinforce habits like signaling, lane changes, and yielding for safer driving.
* **Emergency Maneuvers and Accident Avoidance**: Learn how to handle emergencies like skids, sudden stops, or tire blowouts, and use evasive tactics to avoid accidents in unexpected situations.

Completing a defensive driving course helps you build safer habits, sharpen your awareness, and respond confidently to road hazards.

## How to Get a Defensive Driving Discount

Obtaining a defensive driving discount is not complicated, and by adhering to all the steps you can receive your full benefit.

1. **Contact Your Insurance Provider**: Start by contacting your insurer to confirm if they offer a defensive driving discount and ask about age, course, and state-specific eligibility requirements.
2. **Choose an Approved Course**: Choose a defensive driving course approved by your insurer and certified by your state. Most providers accept both online and in-person options.
3. **Complete the Course**: Complete the course on time, covering safe driving, traffic laws, and hazard avoidance. You’ll receive a certificate by email or mail upon completion.
4. **Submit Your Certificate**: Share your completed certificate with your insurance company through their app, website, email, or regular mail.
5. **Confirm and Track Your Discount**: When your insurer receives your certificate, the discount is applied, either immediately or at renewal.

By completing a defensive driving course and following the right steps, you can earn a valuable discount on [comprehensive auto insurance](https://www.quote.com/auto-insurance/comprehensive-auto-insurance/) while becoming a safer, more confident driver.
