You can choose one of these two options **at the moment of getting a quote**. That’s why we recommend you [contact a producer](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/find-a-producer), get assistance, and make sure you get exactly the coverage you need.
At PSIC, we’re all about protecting your assets and yourself. Our [standard Homeowners Insurance coverage](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/coverage/) protects **your house and your personal belongings, and provides liability protection**, among others. If you’re looking to customize your policy, you can also [add optional coverages.](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/faq/optional-homeowners-insurance-coverage/)
The insurance deductible is **the amount you’ll pay out-of-pocket before your coverage kicks in**. A higher deductible can mean lower premiums, but more expenses when you file a claim. At PSIC we recommend you [contact a producer ](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/find-a-producer/)to personalize your Homeowners Insurance deductible to suit your financial situation.
[Homeowners Insurance costs vary based on factors](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/faq/homeowners-insurance-cost/)**like location, home value, and coverage needs**. We can’t pinpoint an exact price here, but PSIC offers competitive rates and [Homeowners Insurance discounts](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/discounts/) that make protection accessible.
Moreover, there are [many optional add-ons](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/faq/optional-homeowners-insurance-coverage/) you can include in your policy, affecting its final price. Everyone has their own particular needs, **at PSIC we’re ready to help you make your Homeowners Insurance policy fit your needs**. We recommend you [contact a producer](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/find-a-producer) and get more details.
We currently offer Homeowners Insurance in the states of **[Arizona](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/arizona/)**, **[California](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/california/)**, **[Connecticut](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/connecticut/)**, and **[New Jersey](https://www.pacificspecialty.com/homeowners-insurance/new-jersey/)**. To learn more about how you can customize your policy please select your state.
