# How Do You Add a Car to State Farm Insurance Online?
![](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Kevin-Berry-150x150.png)

[![](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Giselle-Gomez-150x150.png) Giselle Gomez Writer and Editor ](https://jerry.ai/author/giselle-gomez/)
You have three ways to make changes to your State Farm policy such as adding a car: online, on the phone or in person.
![christin hume hBuwVLcYTnA unsplash scaled](https://jerry.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/christin-hume-hBuwVLcYTnA-unsplash-scaled.jpg)
**Answer:** Adding an additional car to your [State Farm](https://jerry.ai/reviews/car-insurance/state-farm/) auto insurance policy can be done in a variety of ways, including going online. If you don’t want to go into a local branch or call customer service, making changes to your policy online is your best option.
* **File and manage claims**. You can [file a claim](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/car-insurance-claim/) or take care of details to keep the process moving.
* **Access your ID cards**. If you don’t have your physical insurance card with you, most states will allow digital versions to be used as [proof of insurance](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/proof-of-insurance/) with law enforcement or in the case of an [accident](https://jerry.ai/car-insurance/at-fault-accident/). You can access your ID cards for State Farm auto and other policies right in the app.
