Most drivers will get a discount as soon as you sign up for Geico DriveEasy. But Geico doesn't share the amount of its sign-up discount.
**Not all companies punish you for phone use during these discount programs.** You might want to consider another company for their [usage-based discount](https://www.valuepenguin.com/best-usage-based-car-insurance "Best Usage-Based Car Insurance Companies") if you use your phone often, like when using GPS. Just keep in mind that phone use while driving is dangerous, and in some states, it's illegal.

* **Better options:** [State Farm Drive Safe & Save](https://www.valuepenguin.com/state-farm-drive-safe-and-save-insurance-review "State Farm Drive Safe & Save Review") and [Liberty Mutual RightTrack](https://www.valuepenguin.com/right-track-liberty-mutual-insurance-review "Liberty Mutual RightTrack Review") both offer discounts up to 30%.
* **Better options:** [Liberty Mutual RightTrack](https://www.valuepenguin.com/right-track-liberty-mutual-insurance-review "Liberty Mutual RightTrack Review") and [Nationwide SmartRide](https://www.valuepenguin.com/nationwide-smartride-insurance-review "Is Nationwide SmartRide Worth It?") are good options, because neither program tracks your phone use.
**DriveEasy also continuously monitors your driving, which means your discount can go up and down each policy period based on your score.** Liberty Mutual, Nationwide and [Progressive](https://www.valuepenguin.com/progressive-snapshot-insurance-review "Progressive Snapshot Review: How Does it Work?") only track your driving for a set period of time. After the monitoring period ends, your discount is applied and  won't change.
