###  [Comprehensive vs. Collision Insurance Coverage](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/insurance-guide/comprehensive-vs-collision/)
The cost of comprehensive coverage depends largely on your vehicle. Unlike [liability coverage, ](https://www.thezebra.com/auto-insurance/coverage/liability-insurance/)which pays for injuries or damage to others, comprehensive coverage pays to repair or replace  _your_ car.
