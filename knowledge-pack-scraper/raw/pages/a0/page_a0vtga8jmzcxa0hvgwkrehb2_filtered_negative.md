# GEICO
![geico-insurance \(1\)](https://www.planforfreedom.com/wp-client_data/21908/4977/uploads/2024/11/geico-insurance-1.png)
****Summary:** Founded in 1936 by Leo and Lillian Goodwin, GEICO started as a small insurer for federal employees and military personnel, growing into one of the world’s largest insurance carriers. Warren Buffett’s investment guided GEICO through financial challenges, and under CEO Olza “Tony” Nicely’s leadership in the 1990s, a bold advertising push with the now-famous Gecko mascot made the company a household name. Now a Berkshire Hathaway subsidiary, GEICO offers auto, home, renters, motorcycle, and commercial insurance, plus services like DriveEasy, roadside assistance, and Mechanical Breakdown Insurance. Today, over 17 million policyholders trust GEICO’s affordable and accessible coverage.  Estimated Read Time: 9 mins**
* The GEICO app makes it easy to manage your policy and contact your carrier whenever you need to.
* Known for their bundles, GEICO specializes in providing personalized protection for less.
* From roadside assistance to easy claims filing processes and more, GEICO delivers popular modern features and excellent customer service—including free accident forgiveness for loyal customers.
