Must I notify DMV before I cancel my insurance?  Expand Accordion Plus Collapse Accordion Minus
Yes. To prevent a vehicle registration suspension, you must notify DMV before you cancel your insurance. If you are not operating your currently registered vehicle, and it is not parked on a California roadway, you may submit an [Affidavit of Non-Use (ANU) (REG 5090) (PDF)](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/file/affidavit-of-non-use-reg-5090-pdf/) to DMV.
