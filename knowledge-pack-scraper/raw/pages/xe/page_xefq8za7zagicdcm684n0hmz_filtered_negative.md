#  The Cost of Umbrella Insurance: Is It Worth the Investment?
Most umbrella [policies ](https://www.floridariskpartners.com/understanding-personal-umbrella-liability-insurance-your-comprehensive-guide/)start with a coverage limit of $1 million, with additional increments available. The premiums are relatively low compared to the level of coverage provided, which is why umbrella insurance is often considered a cost-effective way to secure financial protection.
