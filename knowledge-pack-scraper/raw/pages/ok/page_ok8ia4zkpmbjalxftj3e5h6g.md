## Company Overview
Founded in 1937, Progressive has grown to become the second-largest auto insurer in the United States, capturing 14% of the market share. The company is known for its innovative approach to insurance, offering tools like the Name Your Price tool and the Snapshot program, which uses telematics to reward safe driving. Over the years, Progressive has consistently ranked high in terms of customer satisfaction and financial stability, making it a reliable choice for many drivers.

## Coverage Options
Progressive offers a wide range of coverage options, ensuring that drivers can find policies tailored to their specific needs. Standard coverage options include:
  * **Liability Coverage:** Covers bodily injury and property damage to others if you're at fault in an accident.
  * **Comprehensive Coverage:** Protects against damage to your car from non-collision events such as theft, vandalism, and natural disasters.
  * **Collision Coverage:** Pays for repairs to your car if you're involved in a collision.
  * **Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage:** Covers your expenses if you're hit by a driver without insurance or with insufficient coverage.
  * **Medical Payments Coverage (MedPay):** Pays for medical expenses for you and your passengers after an accident.
  * **Personal Injury Protection (PIP):** Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and other related costs, regardless of fault.


**Additional Coverage Options:**
  * **Roadside Assistance:** Provides help for flat tires, dead batteries, lockouts, and towing.
  * **Rental Reimbursement:** Covers the cost of a rental car while your vehicle is being repaired after a covered loss.
  * **Gap Insurance:** Pays the difference between the actual cash value of your car and the remaining balance on your loan or lease if your car is totaled.
  * **Custom Parts and Equipment Value:** Covers custom parts and equipment, such as stereos or custom paint jobs.
  * **Rideshare Coverage:** Extends your personal auto policy while you're driving for a rideshare service like Uber or Lyft.

#### **Discounts and Savings**
Progressive offers a plethora of discounts to help policyholders save on their premiums. Some of the most notable discounts include:

  * **Multi-Car Discount:** Available when you insure more than one vehicle with Progressive.
  * **Continuous Insurance Discount:** Reward for maintaining continuous coverage, even with another insurer.
  * **Snapshot Discount:** Based on safe driving habits tracked via a mobile app or plug-in device.
  * **Good Student Discount:** Available for full-time students with a "B" average or better.
  * **Distant Student Discount:** For students who live more than 100 miles away from home without a car.

  * **Online Quote Discount:** Receive a discount for getting a quote online.

#### **Claims Process**
Progressive's claims process has received mixed reviews. According to J.D. Power's 2023 claims satisfaction survey, Progressive scored 861 out of 1,000, slightly below the industry average of 873. Customers have reported issues with the speed of claims resolution and communication during the process.
**Claims Handling Ratings:**
  * **Overall Claims Satisfaction:** 4.0/5
  * **Ease of Filing a Claim:** 4.1/5
  * **Claim Resolution Speed:** 3.8/5


Despite these challenges, Progressive offers several tools to streamline the claims process, including online claims filing, a mobile app, and access to a network of repair shops that guarantee repairs for as long as you own the vehicle.
_Source: J.D. Power_
