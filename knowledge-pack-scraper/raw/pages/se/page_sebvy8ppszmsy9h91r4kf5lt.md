### AAA car insurance coverage options
AAA offers a few coverages not available at State Farm that you can use to customize your policy, like gap insurance and new car replacement. However, these options may not be available in every state.
Gap coverage
[Gap coverage](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-gap-auto-insurance-works "Gap Insurance: How Does It Work and Do I Need It?") ensures you won't owe money on a car you no longer have if you total your leased or financed vehicle.
For instance, if you have comprehensive coverage and a tree branch falls on your car, that's covered by your insurance.
If you don't have comprehensive coverage, the insurance company won't cover this situation. That means your gap insurance wouldn't apply. 
Enhanced exterior repair
This coverage guarantees AAA will fix the exterior of your car using original manufacturer parts after an accident. Without this coverage, insurance companies may pay for cheaper aftermarket parts that are of lower quality. 
Nonowners coverage
If you don't own a car, but regularly drive someone else's, you'll need [nonowners coverage](https://www.valuepenguin.com/non-owner-car-insurance-who-should-have-it "Non-Owner Car Insurance: What Is It and Who Should Have It?") to drive legally. 
New car replacement
[New car replacement](https://www.valuepenguin.com/auto-insurance/new-car-replacement-insurance "New Car Replacement Insurance") will replace your car with a brand new model after an accident. This coverage is typically only available for cars that are 1 or 2 years old. 
### State Farm car insurance coverage options
State Farm offers classic car coverage and rideshare coverage. AAA doesn't typically offer these coverage add-ons.
Rideshare coverage
[Rideshare coverage](https://www.valuepenguin.com/how-ridesharing-affects-your-auto-insurance "Rideshare Insurance: Why You Need It and What It Costs") helps bridge the gap between your personal auto insurance and the coverage provided by the rideshare company you drive for. 
Classic car coverage
[Classic car insurance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/classic-car-insurance "Classic Car Insurance: What Is It? How Does It Work?") is typically for cars over 25 years old, exotic cars, muscle cars or highly customized vehicles. 
### State Farm roadside assistance vs. AAA
State Farm and AAA both offer roadside assistance programs as an add-on to your car insurance policy.
Both companies charge about $1 per month for this service.
**AAA's roadside assistance add-on is very limited.** It doesn't cover oil or tire delivery, which can be important if you're stranded on the side of the road.
However, AAA offers more comprehensive roadside assistance as part of its membership programs. Membership programs typically start at $59 per year. They can also include perks like travel planning and identity theft protection.

### AAA auto insurance discounts
One of AAA's largest discounts is its low-mileage discount. You can save up to 19.1% off your auto insurance rate if you occasionally provide odometer readings to AAA. Drivers who belong to a professional organization or alumni association can save up to 7.4%, and a car club membership can save you up to 5%.
After having AAA insurance for one year, you're also eligible for its loyalty discount. This can save you up to 5.6%.
### State Farm auto insurance discounts
[State Farm's Drive Safe & Save program](https://www.valuepenguin.com/state-farm-drive-safe-and-save-insurance-review "State Farm Drive Safe & Save Review") offers drivers a discount of up to 30% for practicing safe driving habits. The company tracks your mileage and driving habits using your mobile phone to determine your overall discount.
Drivers under 25 years old can take advantage of [State Farm's Steer Clear](https://www.valuepenguin.com/car-insurance/state-farm-steer-clear-review "State Farm Steer Clear Review") program, which offers a discount of up to 15%. You must complete course training, practice driving and have an older driver mentor you. To qualify, you can't have any tickets or at-fault accidents for three years.

## AAA insurance vs. State Farm customer service ratings
![](https://res.cloudinary.com/value-penguin/image/upload/w_90//icons/award)
State Farm earned a better customer service score from our editors than AAA.

## Frequently asked questions
### Do I need AAA if I have State Farm?
State Farm offers [roadside assistance #roadside ](https://www.valuepenguin.com/compare-aaa-vs-state-farm-car-insurance#roadside "#roadside") as an optional coverage add-on. You can buy State Farm's coverage instead of a AAA membership and will get most of the same benefits.
### Is State Farm better than AAA?
State Farm is a better choice than AAA for most people. State Farm customers pay 27% to 71% less for auto insurance than AAA customers. State Farm also offers more reliable customer service, which is important if you're ever in an accident.
### Does State Farm have roadside assistance?
State Farm offers [roadside assistance](https://www.valuepenguin.com/roadside-assistance-car-insurance "Roadside Assistance Program Comparisons") for about $1 extra per month. The State Farm emergency roadside assistance program pays for towing, tire changes and fuel delivery, and other issues you may have if your car breaks down on the side of the road.
### Is State Farm roadside assistance as good as AAA?
State Farm and AAA offer many of the [same coverage features when it comes to roadside assistance #roadside ](https://www.valuepenguin.com/compare-aaa-vs-state-farm-car-insurance#roadside "#roadside"). State Farm includes delivery of oil and tires, and AAA does not. On the other hand, AAA will send help if your car is stuck in mud or snow, which State Farm doesn't cover.
## Methodology
ValuePenguin used car insurance quotes from thousands of ZIP codes across  three states to compare pricing from AAA and State Farm. Rates are for a 30-year-old man with good credit who owns a 2015 Honda Civic EX.
Full coverage auto insurance quotes include comprehensive and collision coverage along with higher liability limits than required in each state.
  * **Bodily injury liability:** $50,000 per person and $100,000 per accident 
  * **Property damage liability:** $25,000 per accident 
  * **Uninsured and underinsured motorist bodily injury:** $50,000 per person and $100,000 per accident 
  * **Comprehensive and collision:** $500 deductible 


To determine the cost of roadside assistance, our experts gathered quotes for a driver living in Arizona from State Farm and Auto Club Arizona, AAA's Arizona subsidiary.
ValuePenguin's analysis used insurance rate data from Quadrant Information Services. These rates were publicly sourced from insurance company filings and should be used for comparative purposes only. Your own quotes may be different.
