### **The coverage of Commercial Auto Insurance**

Key Auto Insurance Coverage Options

Progressive’s commercial auto coverage ensures you are protected against a wide variety of risks. Here are the primary coverages:

| Coverage Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Liability** | Covers injury and property damage if you’re at fault. |
| **Collision** | Pays for damage to your vehicle after an accident. |
| **Comprehensive** | Covers non-collision events: theft, fire, vandalism, or weather damage. |
| **Medical Payments** | Pays for injuries to you or your passengers. |
| **Uninsured Motorist** | Covers your vehicle if hit by an uninsured or hit-and-run driver. |
| **Hired/Non-Owned Auto** | Protects vehicles not owned by your business but used for work. |
| **Trailer Interchange** | Covers trailers in your possession that you don’t own. |

These coverages can be adjusted based on your vehicle use and risk exposure.

## FAQ Section

###### **What is Progressive commercial auto insurance?**

Progressive commercial auto insurance is a specialized policy that covers vehicles used for business purposes. Unlike personal auto insurance, it offers expanded protection, including higher liability limits, coverage for equipment and employees, and optional protections like downtime coverage and cargo insurance.

###### **Who needs Progressive commercial auto insurance?**

Any business that uses a vehicle for commercial activities should consider this coverage. This includes:

* Contractors
* Delivery drivers
* Landscapers
* Real estate agents
* Rideshare and gig economy workers
* Fleet operators
* Food trucks

Even if you’re a sole proprietor using your personal car for work, Progressive’s commercial policy can keep you fully protected.
