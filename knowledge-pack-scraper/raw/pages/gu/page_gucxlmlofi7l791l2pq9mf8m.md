# RegEd to Spotlight AI-Driven Advertising Compliance Solutions as Platinum Sponsor at 2025 IAdCA Conference 
* [ March 25, 2025March 27, 2025 ](https://www.reged.com/reged-to-spotlight-ai-driven-advertising-compliance-solutions-as-platinum-sponsor-at-2025-iadca-conference/)
* [Madelyn Matthews](https://www.reged.com/author/madelyn-matthews/)
RegEd, the leading provider of compliance technology solutions to the financial services industry, is proud to announce its platinum-level sponsorship at the [2025 Insurance Advertising Compliance Association (IAdCA) Conference](https://web.cvent.com/event/fb6d5aeb-0f72-4299-af8b-9bd82fb78ce6/summary), scheduled for April 2-4 in Charleston, SC. RegEd will present cutting-edge advancements in AI and advertising compliance during a special 15-minute demonstration on Thursday, April 3.
Mark Mashewke, Principal Product Manager at RegEd, will lead the session, titled “AI and Advanced Technologies: Revolutionizing Advertising Compliance and Accelerating Market Readiness.” Mashewske will demonstrate how RegEd’s solutions leverage AI and advanced workflow automation to streamline the advertising compliance review process and accelerate time-to-market.
The IAdCA conference offers insurance industry professionals an opportunity to network and gain insight and understanding of insurance advertising compliance issues and trends. [RegEd recently announced its expansion of AI capabilities](https://www.reged.com/reged-expands-ai-capabilities-with-launch-of-new-ai-powered-media-transcription-for-advertising-compliance-review/) with the launch of a new AI-Powered Media Transcription tool. “As firms increasingly leverage digital media to engage clients, regulatory expectations for compliance oversight remain stringent and continue to evolve,” said Mark Mashewke. “This latest innovation ensures that advertising review teams, as well as content submitters, can quickly and efficiently assess all forms of marketing content, reducing manual effort while improving accuracy and consistency. Our AI-powered media transcription tool is a game changer, enabling firms to stay ahead of compliance requirements and accelerate their go-to-market strategies.”
RegEd’s Enterprise Advertising Review solution is designed to automate and simplify compliance approvals, significantly reducing review times while improving accuracy and consistency. Key features include Lexicon Detection for identifying compliance risks in content, Concurrent Review with Annotations for real-time collaboration, and seamless integration with marketing automation and third-party systems.
RegEd representatives will be on-site to meet with conference attendees to understand their compliance challenges and discuss solutions that enable firms to meet compliance requirements and proactively manage their compliance program. For more information about RegEd’s participation at the 2025 IAdCA Conference and to schedule a personalized demonstration, visit www.RegEd.com
**About RegEd**
RegEd is the market-leading provider of RegTech enterprise solutions with relationships with more than 200 enterprise clients that represent more than 35 of the top 50 insurance companies. 
Established in 2000 by former regulators, the company is recognized for continuous regulatory technology innovation with solutions hallmarked by workflow-directed processes, data integration, regulatory intelligence, automated validations, business process automation and compliance dashboards. The aggregate drives the highest levels of operational efficiency and enables our clients to cost-effectively comply with regulations and continuously mitigate risk. 
Trusted by the nation’s top financial services firms, RegEd’s proven, holistic approach to RegTech meets firms where they are on the compliance and risk management continuum, scaling as their needs evolve and amplifying the value proposition delivered to clients. For more information, please visit www.reged.com.
