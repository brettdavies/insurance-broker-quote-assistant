## Geico auto insurance pros and cons

Consider [Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp)'s benefits and drawbacks before you buy.

### Pros

* **Available nationwide:** Unlike other low-cost insurers, Geico is available in all 50 states.
* **Low rates:** Premiums for both full and minimum coverage are much lower than the national average.
* **Few complaints:** Geico has far fewer complaints than other major car insurance companies.
* **Accident forgiveness:** Your rates won't increase after a collision if you've been accident-free for five years.

### Cons

* **Unexemplary customer service:** Geico scored below average on J.D. Power surveys for both customer service and the claims process.
* **No gap insurance:** If you have a leased or financed car, you'll have to look elsewhere for [gap insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-gap-insurance/).
* **No rideshare insurance:** Geico doesn't offer coverage for those who drive for [rideshare](https://www.cnbc.com/select/best-rideshare-insurance-companies/) apps.

## Types of Geico car insurance

[Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp) offers car insurance in all 50 states and Washington, D.C.

### Basic coverage

A [full coverage policy](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-full-coverage-car-insurance/) is typically required if you leased or financed your car.

* **Liability car insurance:** Minimum coverage allowed in most states,[liability](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-liability-car-insurance/) pays for damages if you or someone else driving your car is at fault for an accident.
* **Collision coverage:** Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident.
* **Comprehensive coverage:** Pays for damages not caused by a collision, including theft, [weather damage](https://www.cnbc.com/select/does-car-insurance-cover-hail-damage/) or hitting an animal.

### Additional coverage

These policies are required in some states**.** Note:Geico does not offer gap insurance.

* **Personal injury protection insurance:** Also known as no-fault insurance,PIP covers medical bills, lost wages, child care and even funeral costs.
* **Medical payments insurance:** Covers medical bills for you, your passengers and any pedestrians, regardless of fault.
* **Uninsured/underinsured motorist insurance:** Covers repairs and medical bills if you are in an accident with a driver with little or no insurance.

### Add-ons

Geico offers a selection of coverage options.

* **Accident forgiveness:** Geico won't raise your rates after your first at-fault collision. Accident forgiveness is free if you remain accident-free for five years or it can be bought as an upgrade when you purchase or renew your policy.
* **Emergency road service:** Helps with towing, winching, jump-starting, tire changing, lockouts, and fuel delivery through Geico's mobile app. It starts at $14 per year.
* **Mechanical breakdown insurance:** This coverage can cover repairs after a manufacturer's warranty expires.
* **Rental car reimbursement:** Pays for a rental while your car is being repaired

## Geico car insurance discounts

There are plenty of opportunities to lower your [Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp) premiums, with discounts based on driving history and habits, affiliation, education and customer loyalty. Not all discounts are available in all states and some discounts may not be combined

* **Anti-theft system:** A car alarm, tracking system, VIN etching and other security features can save you up to 23% on comprehensive coverage.
* **Defensive driving:** Save on your premiums when you complete an approved defensive driving course.
* **Driver's education.** Young drivers who complete a driver's ed course can earn a discount on most policies.
* **Eagle discount:** Current or retired federal employees may qualify for up to 12% off.
* **Good driver:** If you remain accident-free for five years, you can save up to 22% off your rate.
* **Good student:** Full-time students with a good academic record could get a discount of up to 15% on certain policies.
* **Military discount:** Active duty or retired military are eligible for a discount of up to 15%. If you're part of an emergency deployment, you could receive a rate reduction of up to 25%
* **Membership discount:** Receive rate discounts and other benefits if you're a member of one of Geico's 500-plus partner organizations, including alumni associations, fraternities and sororities, and professional organizations
* **Multiple vehicles:** Get 25% off most coverages when you insure more than one vehicle with Geico
* **Multiple policies:** Save when you [bundle auto and homeowners insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/best-home-auto-insurance-bundles/) from Geico.
* **New vehicle:** If your car model is three years or newer, you can get a discount of up to 15%.
* **Safe driving:** Eligible drivers can save up to 25% using Geico's DriveEasy app.
* **Seat belt use:** Always buckle up and get a discount on the personal injury protection and medical payments portion of your premium
* **Safety equipment:** Airbags, anti-lock brakes, daytime running lights and other safety devices can save you as much as 23%

## Geico customer satisfaction

[Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp) hasn't exactly wowed users with its customer service: It scored below average in seven out of eleven regions in [J.D. Power's 2024 US Auto Insurance Study](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2024-us-auto-insurance-study), which polls customers on price, ease of doing business, product offerings, problem resolution and other categories.

It also ranked below average in J.D. Power's surveys on [the claims process](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2023-us-auto-claims-satisfaction-study), on [insurance shopping](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2024-us-insurance-shopping-study) and the [digital experience](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2024-us-insurance-digital-experience-study).

Despite this, Geico has close to 40% fewer complaints than other insurers of its size, according to the [National Association of Insurance Commissioners](https://content.naic.org/cis_refined_results.htm?TABLEAU=CIS_COMPLAINTS&amp;COCODE=14138&amp;:refresh). The company received a 0.62 on the NAIC complaint index, where the standard is 1.0.

A J.D. Power survey did rate Geico very highly on [usage-based insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/safe-driving-discounts-how-do-they-work/), which includes [pay-per-mile policies](https://www.cnbc.com/select/pay-per-mile-car-insurance/) and [safe-driving programs](https://www.cnbc.com/select/safe-driving-discounts-how-do-they-work/). ( Eligible drivers can save up to 25% with Geico's DriveEasy app.)

The [Better Business Bureau](https://www.bbb.org/us/md/chevy-chase/profile/insurance-companies/geico-0241-8768) also gave Geico an A+, its highest grade, based on factors like transparency, truthful advertising and how it responds to consumer complaints.

## How do I buy car insurance from Geico?

[Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp) makes it easy to buy car insurance online but you can also speak to an agent at 855-347-3749.

You'll need to give your driver's license number and your car's VIN number, as well as details about your driving history.

You'll choose coverage, limits and deductibles that meet your needs and enter your payment information. According to Geico, if you set up autopay you can save up to $36 a year on processing fees.

You can also manage your policy, get your insurance ID and even request roadside assistance on the Geico app.

## Is Geico car insurance right for me?

[Geico](https://secure.money.com/pr/zc1d02986163?s1=107276270&s2=amp) is a great choice if you're looking to save money and don't want to waste time with providers who don't serve your state. The easy-to-use website makes buying coverage, filing claims and making payments simple and fast.

While it tops for price, Geico has a ways to go with customer service. If that's a major concern, get quotes from several other providers.

## FAQs

### Why is Geico so much cheaper?

Because it sells insurance directly to consumers and doesn't have to pay commissions to brokers, Geico can offer some of the lowest rates in the market. Its many discounts allow policyholders to save even more.

### Who is better: Geico or Progressive?

Both Geico and Progressive are solid choices for auto insurance. Geico stands out for low rates and high customer satisfaction, while Progressive tends to offer lower premiums to high-risk drivers.

### Does Geico cover rental cars?

A Geico auto insurance policy typically extends coverage to a rental car but only to the limits you have for your vehicle. In addition, Geico will pay for a rental if your car is being repaired after a covered incident.

### Who owns Geico?

Geico is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, which also issues property and casualty reinsurance policies.

### Does Geico offer gap insurance?

Geico doesn't offer [gap insurance](https://www.cnbc.com/select/what-is-gap-insurance/). Although it's usually more expensive, you may be able to buy coverage from your dealership.
